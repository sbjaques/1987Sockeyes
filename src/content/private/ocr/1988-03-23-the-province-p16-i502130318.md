## Paragraphs

Staff photo by Rick Loughran the Sockeyes in Richmond last scored the opening goal.

The victims last night were the defending B.C. Junior Hockey League champion Richmond Sockeyes, who were mowed down 6-2 at Richmond Arena by the swift-skating and even swifter-passing Vernon Lakers. Vernon, the Interior Division champion, leads the best-of-seven league final with a 3-0 lead and could eliminate the Coastal Division champions in tonight's 7:30 p.m. meeting in Richmond.
