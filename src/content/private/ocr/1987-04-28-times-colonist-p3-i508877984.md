'Eyes go up 3-2 in Tier 2 tussle The Canadian Press RICHMOND - Matt Hervey, a former member of the Victoria Cougars in major junior hockey, scored two goals to lead the Richmond Sockeyes to a 5-3 win over the Humboldt Broncos in a Centennial Cup hockey playoff game that finished late Sunday night. The victory gave the Sockeyes a 3-2 lead in the best-of-seven Western Canada Tier 2 junior final. The sixth game is scheduled in Richmond tonight.
