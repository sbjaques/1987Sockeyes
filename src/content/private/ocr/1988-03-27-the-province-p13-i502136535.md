## Paragraphs

VERNON Vernon Lakers defeated the defending Centennial Cup champion Richmond Sockeyes 9-6 here last night to clinch the best-of-seven B.C. Junior Hockey League final series 4-1. In Grande Prairie, Alta., Prince George downed the host North Stars 4-2 in a Peace-Cariboo Junior Hockey League final. The best-of-seven series is tied at 1-1. The winner meets Vernon in the best-of-three Mowat Cup final.
