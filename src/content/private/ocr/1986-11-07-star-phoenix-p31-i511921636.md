"This is nothing but a hobby for me. There's no way I want to get back into coaching full time." . Orland Kurtenbach

RICHMOND, B.C. (CP) Orland Kurtenbach is back in hockcoaching at the Tier Two junlor level, far from the pressure packed National Hockey League. The former NHL player and coach of the Vancouver Canucks for two years is behind the bench of the Richmond Sockeyes this season in the British Columbia Junior Hockey League. He finds the days hectic between his regular job and coaching fuzzy-cheeked teenagers. The man who became the first member of the NHL Canucks in 1970 he captained and later - coached the team - wears three hats. His average working day is 8 a.m. until midnight.
