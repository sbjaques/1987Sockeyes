## Paragraphs

RED DEER. Alta. - The Richmond Sockeyes scored five first-period goals and coasted to a 7-3 victory over the Red Deer Rustlers to square their western Centennial Cup semifinal at three wins each last night. The Sockeyes, British Columbia Junior Hockey League champions, led 5-1 after the first period and 5-3 after the second. The Sockeyes outshot the Rustlers, Alberta Junior Hockey League champions, 36-33. Rob Clarke scored three times for Richmond and Tony Bobbitt, Dave Tomlinson, Brian Kozak and Mike McCormick added single goals. Pete DeGraaf with two goals and Dixon Ward replied for Red Deer. The Rustlers took 18 of 31 minor penalties and two misconducts. The deciding game in the series will be played tonight in Red Deer.
