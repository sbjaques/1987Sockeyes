WINNIPEG (Special) The Winnipeg Jets rookie team will face the University of Manitoba Bisons Sunday and Monday in exhibition games at the. university arena. A number of players competing in the contests will later line up with Moncton Hawks of the American Hockey League. The lineup which has been chosen for the games includes former Montreal Canadiens ' netminder Steve Penney; ex-Sherbrooke Canadiens defenceman Bobby Dollas; U.S. Olympic team cut Scott Schneider and Guy Larose, son of former NHLer Claude Larose.
