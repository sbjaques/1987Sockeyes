WHO'S HOT: Canadiens defenceman Mathieu Schneider has scored a goal in each of the Canadiens' last two games. and has emerged as an. offensive threat with 13 goals this season, while Kirk Muller, the team's leading scorer, has three goals in Montreal's last three games. John Cullen has blossomed for the Leafs after being acquired from Hartford for a second-round draft choice. He had one assist last night against San Jose, giving him a goal and four assists in Toronto's last four games.
