## Paragraphs

By GREG MEACHEM of The Advocate RICHMOND, B.C. - Red Deer Rustlers may have dropped the first two games of their Western Canada Centennial Cup semifinal series to Richmond Sockeyes, but they're not returning home emptyhanded. Rustlers were at least able to grasp the confidence that they can indeed play with the large and extremely mobile Sockeyes, who tore to shreds their competition in the BCJHL playoffs before meeting Red Deer. After an embarrassing 11-4 setback the night before, the Rustlers were vastly improved Wednesday, leading 4-2 after two periods but eventually falling 6-4.
