## Paragraphs

HUMBOLDT, Sask. (CP) Tony Bobbitt's goal at 1:04 of a 10-minute overtime period gave the Richmond Sockeyes a 4-3 win over the Humboldt Broncos in a Centennial Cup junior hockey playoff game yesterday night. The victory gave the Sockeyes a 2-1 lead in the best-of-seven Tier 2 western final series. The fourth game will be played in Richmond Saturday. Dave Tomlinson, Mike Claringbull and Rob Clarke scored the other goals for the Sockeyes, who were outshot 39- 23 and trailed 1-0 after the first period and 2-1 after the second. Bill McDougall had two goals and Curtis Chamberlin one for the Saskatchewan champion Broncos.
