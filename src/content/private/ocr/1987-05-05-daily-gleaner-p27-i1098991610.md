## Paragraphs

HUMBOLDT, Sask. (CP) - The Richmond Sockeyes exploded for three goals in the third period to beat the Pembroke Lumber Kings 4-1 Monday night in the Centennial Cup junior A hockey championship tournament. The win gave the British Columbia champions a 2-0 record in the roundrobin portion of the tournament, and a share of first place with the Saskatchewan champions, Humboldt Broncos. It was the second straight loss for the Lumber Kings, Ontario's representatives in the four-team tournament. Dartmouth Fuel Kids from British Columbia, the other team in the tournament, are also 0-2. Dave Tomlinson led Richmond with two goals, while Brian Kozak and Stan Czenczek scored one each. Czenczek scored the winning goal at 6:35 of the third period, after his team held a 1-0 lead through the first two frames.
