## Paragraphs

By ELLIOTT PAP The 'Eyes have it. Orland Kurtenbach's Richmond Sockeyes collected their first B.C. Junior Hockey League championship in eight years Tuesday night, edging Kelowna Packers 3-2 at Richmond Arena to sweep the bestof-nine final five straight. The Sockeyes, who are loaded with major junior veterans, made it look easy as they romped to 13 consecutive wins in three BCJHL series. "I must admit that is a surprise," said Kurtenbach, the former Vancouver Canuck captain and coach. "'You just never know what the fickle finger of fate will do. But we seemed to have it in gear. I'm very honored and very happy to be a winner my first year coaching in junior." The Sockeyes' season is far from over, however. As a tier two t team, their particular dream goes all the way to the Centennial Cup national championship.
