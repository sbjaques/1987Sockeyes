## Paragraphs

RICHMOND, B.C. (CP) - Matt Hervey scored two goals Sunday night to lead the Richmond Sockeyes to a 5- 3 win over the Humboldt Broncos in Centennial Cup hockey playoff action. The victory gave the Sockeyes a 3-2 lead in the best-of-seven Western Canada final. The sixth game is scheduled here Tuesday night. Humboldt led 2-0 after after the first period but Richmond tied the game 2- 2 after the middle frame. Brian Kozak, Dave Tomlinson and Dave Rutherford added goals for Richmond. Bill McDougall scored two goals for Humboldt while Garnet Kazuik completed the scoring for the Saskatchewan club. Goalie Frank Romeo made 30 saves for Richmond while Bruce Hoffort blocked 22 shots for Humboldt.
