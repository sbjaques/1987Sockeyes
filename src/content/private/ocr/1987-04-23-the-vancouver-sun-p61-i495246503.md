Sun News Services HUMBOLDT, Sask. - With a bus idling in the parking lot for a trip to the West Coast, Richmond Sockeyes weren't about to get into any marathon overtime contest Wednesday. Tony Bobbitt scored for the B.C. Junior Hockey League champions after only 1:04 of extra play and the Sockeyes made the goal stand up for the remainder of the 10-minute overtime period for a 4-3 victory over Humboldt Broncos in a Centennial Cup Western Canadian final series game. The victory gave the Sockeyes a 2-1 lead in the best-of-seven battle for the Abbott Cup. The series now shifts to Richmond Arena for the remainder of the games, beginning Saturday at 7:30 p.m. "We kind of stole one tonight," said Sockeyes' coach Orland Kurtenbach.
