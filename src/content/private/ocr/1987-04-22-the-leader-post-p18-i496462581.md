Richmond ties series HUMBOLDT (CP) - Jason Phillips scored three goals as the Richmond Sockeyes beat the Humboldt Broncos 5-3 in a Centennial Cup junior hockey playoff game Tuesday night. The victory tied the best-of-seven tier-two western final series 1-1. The third game will be played tonight in Humboldt. Stan Czenczek and Brian Kozak scored the other goals for the Sockeyes, the champions of British Columbia. The Broncos, who had a 45- 31 edge in shots on goal, got goals from Dave Shyiak, Kevin Luke and Bill McDougall.
