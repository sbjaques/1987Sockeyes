By FRANK LUBA Sports Reporter Add Humboldt Broncos to the Richmond Sockeyes' hit list. The Sockeyes' 5-4 win last night over the Broncos allowed the B.C. champions to win the best-of-seven Abbott Cup Western Canadian junior A hockey championship 4-3 and relegated the Saskatchewan champions to the long list of teams that have succumbed to the Sockeyes.

the list - Nanaimo Clippers, Delta Flyers, Kelowna Packers and the Alberta champion Red Deer Rustlers the Broncos get another shot at the Sockeyes because they are hosts of the Canadian championship Centennial Cup that begins Saturday in Humboldt. The Broncos were soundly booed by a finally-noisy Richmond Arena crowd for not showing up for the traditional post-game handshakes at centre ice.
