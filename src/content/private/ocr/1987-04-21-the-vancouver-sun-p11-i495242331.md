Sockeyes lose first in overtime Canadian Press HUMBOLDT, Sask. - The Humboldt Broncos scored twice in an overtime period to beat the Richmond Sockeyes 5-3 Monday night t in the opening game of the best-of-seven Centennial Cup Western Canada junior hockey final. Curtis Chamberlain had three goals, including the winner, for the Saskatchewan champion Broncos. Brad Bergen and Bill McDougall had the other Humboldt markers. The Sockeyes - British Columbia champions - had goals from Bryan Moller, Matt Hervey and Jason Phillips. Richmond led 2-1 after the second period and the teams were tied at 3-3 after regulation time.
