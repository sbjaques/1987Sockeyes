Sockeyes gain final in Centennial Cup

HUMBOLDT, Sask. - The Richmond Sockeyes whipped the Pembroke Lumber Kings 9-3 Thursday to advance to Saturday's Centennial Cup Tier II Junior A hockey final against the Humboldt Broncos. Richmond scorers were Dean Rutledge, Steve Jaques, Matt Hervey, Brian Kozak, Paul Rutherford, Jason Phillips, Tony Bobbitt, Mike McCormick and Bill Hardy.
