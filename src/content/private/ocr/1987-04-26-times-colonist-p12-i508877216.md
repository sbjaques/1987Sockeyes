The Canadian Press RICHMOND- Bill McDougall and Brad Bergen scored one goal apiece as the Humboldt Broncos defeated the Richmond Sockeyes 2-1 Saturday night in Centennial Cup Tier II junior hockey action. The best-of-seven Western Canadian final is tied 2-2. Dean Rutledge replied for Richmond. The teams meet here tonight and Tuesday.
