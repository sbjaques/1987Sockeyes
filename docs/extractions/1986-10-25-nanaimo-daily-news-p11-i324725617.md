TOP SCORER Leading the way for the Sockeyes is former Seattle Thunderbird Mario Desjardins. The diminutive Terrace native was traded last year from Seattle to Lethbridge. During the off-season, he was sent to Kamloops and eventually found himself with the Sockeyes. Desjardins is leading Richmond in scoring with 11 and 22. points in 10 games. Desjardins is playing on a relatively new line as his mates, Brian Kozak and Tony Bobbitt have just joined the team.
