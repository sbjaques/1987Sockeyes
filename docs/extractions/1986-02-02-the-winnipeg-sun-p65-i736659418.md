Pagnucco. Prentice-Hall, 332 pp. $12.95. - The subtitle of this Where-Are- They-Now? hockey reference suggests that the book covers only the stars of hockey's golden era. Actually, anyone who played at least three season's in the NHL between 1952 and 1968 receives an entry. Heroes recalls a time when the Uke line of Bronco Horvath, Johnny Bucyk, and Vic Stasiuk terrorized opposing defences and Glen Hall flopped into a butterfly formation to fend off shots from the likes of Claude Larose and Orland Kurtenbach. If these names send you reeling thrillingly into the past, you'll want to snatch up this book. Though it could use more action photos and some of the 173 player bios provide as scant information as the back of a hockey card. Heroes is and well-researched.
