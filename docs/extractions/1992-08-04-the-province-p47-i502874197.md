TRANSACTIONS BASEBALL Atlanta recalled pitcher Armando Reynoso from Richmond of International League; optioned pitcher Mark Wohlers to Richmond. Cincinnati activated outfielder Reggie Sanders; optioned outfielder Cesar Hernandez to Chattonooga of Southern League. Cleveland placed pitcher Scott Scudder on 15-day disabledlist; recalled pitcher Dave Otto from Colorado Springs. Detroit fired team president Bo Schembechler; announced that Jim Campbell, chairman of the board and chief executive officer, also will be leaving the team; placed pitcher Mark Leiter on disabled list; recalled David Haas from Toledo of International League (AAA). New York (NL) placed right fielder Bobby Bonilla and outfielder Howard Johnson on 15- day disabled list; placed pitcher Bret Saberhagen on the disabled list; recalled outfielder Pat Howell and called up outfielder Jeff McKnight from Tidewater of International League. Pittsburgh signed pitcher Jason Phillips and assigned him to Bradenton of Gulf Coast League (Rookie). FOOTBALL Cleveland signed linebacker Clay Matthews; agreed to terms with wide receiver Lawyer Tillman. Denver signed tackle Jeff Davidson to series of one-year contracts; placed running back Carlos Snow on injured reserve; released defensive lineman Stacey Satterwhite and tight end Todd Walker. Miami signed linebacker Marco Coleman to -year contract. New England signed defensive back Rod Smith; named Chuck Studley advance scout. NY Giants agreed to terms with running back Lewis Tiliman and linebacker Bobby Abrams; waived wide receiver Merton Harris and safety Ramsey Bradberry. NY Jets traded tight end Corwin Anthony to Green Bay for conditional draft pick. Pittsburgh agreed to terms with tackle Leon Searcy; placed cornerback Shawn Vincent on reserve-left squad. San Francisco signed tackle Steve Wallace to two-year contract. Seattle signed tackle Ray Roberts to four one-year contracts. HOCKEY Providence (AHL) named right winger Mark Kumpel player -assistant coach. NY Rangers signed defenceman John Bary.
