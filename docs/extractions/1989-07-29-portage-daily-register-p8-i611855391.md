## Paragraphs

PORTAGE •Lee Realty clinched at least a tie for the league crown after dumping Party House, 13-3, in Portage Fastpitch Softball Association action Thursday night. Lee improved to 15-3 with two games remaining - Tuesday versus Fisherman's Cove and a makeup contest against Village Pub. No date has been set for that matchup. Service Drug Store is currently second with an 11-5 mark. Service Drug has four games remaining. In one other contest Thursday, Village Pub dumped Trecek ChevOlds, 19-11, in three innings. Lee Realty put the game away in the second inning by scoring nine runs. Lee sent 15 batters to the plate. Center fielder Kris Hall and left fielder Dave Tomlinson smacked home runs, while catcher Dave Mills cracked a triple and first baseman Bryan Albrecht drilled : a double in the outburst. Lee finished with 11 hits, including a perfect 4-for-4 by Hall. Party House also smashed a couple of homers - solo shots by Dan Fearing and Rick Lobenstein. In the evening's opener, Village Pub sent 14 batters to the plate in the second inning and- -in the process scored eight runs. Ken Staveness, Donn Paul and Joe Westcott clubbed doubles.
