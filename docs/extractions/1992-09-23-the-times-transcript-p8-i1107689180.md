Zhamnov to report WINNIEPG (CP) - Russian centre Alexei Zhamnov, who has been threatening court action break his contract with the Winnipeg Jets, is expected to join the NHL team soon, general manager Mike Smith said Tuesday night. Zhamnov has claimed he was coerced into signing a $400,000-a- year deal with Winnipeg last May and wanted out of the agreement plus compensation from the Jets. Ron Salcer, Zhamnov's agent, sent the Jets a fax Tuesday saying he was advising Zhamnov to report to Winnipeg.
