## Paragraphs

RICHMOND Ben Biagiaoni and Bruce Major scored twice each to pace Richmond Sockeyes to an 8-3 B.C. Junior Hockey League win over Sidney Capitals scored on Tuesday. twice for Sidney and Tim Townley once. Goaltender Tim Renton made 31 saves for Sidney while Glen Penser blocked 22 shots for Richmond. The Sockeyes, third in the six-team Coastal Division, improved their won-lost-tied record to 22-16-2.
