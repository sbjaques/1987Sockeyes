## Paragraphs

Royals also played in Queen's Park after the Bruins first left in 1981. That edition of the Royals folded when the Nanaimo Islanders moved back into Queen's Park as the new Bruins in 1983. Former Richmond Sockeyes owner Bruce Taylor has acquired a one-third interest in the Royals franchise. The other owners are Byron Hitchens of Abbotsford and Cloverdale's Kurt Bonnes. Taylor, whose Sockeyes won the Canadian championship Centennial Cup two seasons ago, sold the Richmond franchise to Al Brew last week.
