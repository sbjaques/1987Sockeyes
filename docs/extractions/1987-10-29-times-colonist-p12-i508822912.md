BRANDON -Outstanding and satisfying were the words general manager Al Patterson used Wednesday night to describe the Victoria Cougars' : 3-1 Western Hockey League win over the Wheat Kings. "It was an outstanding junior hockey game because it was scoreless for 2½ periods," Patterson said. Peter Fry had a spectacular game in goal for Victoria, stopping many difficult shots and winding up with 29 saves. George Maneluk was also impressive in the Brandon nets, making 33 saves.
