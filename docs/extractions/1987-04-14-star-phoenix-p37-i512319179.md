Sunday's game was delayed 30 minutes as fights broke out on and off the ice. RCMP Sgt. Bob Williams said the man was arrested for breaching the peace. He was released without being charged, but Williams said charges may : still be laid. There were four fights on the ice and a fracas behind the Sockeyes' players bench that police say was started by a group of disorderly fans who threw eggs and other debris at the Richmond players. The winner of the series is slated to meet the Humboldt Broncos.
