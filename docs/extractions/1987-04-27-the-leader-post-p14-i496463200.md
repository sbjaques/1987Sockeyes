RICHMOND, B.C. (CP) - Matt Hervey scored two goals Sunday night to lead the Richmond Sockeyes to a 5-3 win over the Humboldt Broncos in Centennial Cup hockey playoff action. The victory gave the Sockeyes a 3- 2 lead in the best-of-seven Western Canada final. Saturday, the Broncos evened the series at 2-2 with a 2-1 victory. The sixth game is scheduled here Tuesday night. Humboldt led 2-0 after after the first period but Richmond tied the game 2-2 after the middle frame.
