## Paragraphs

VANCOUVER (CP) - First-place Kelowna Packers placed six players on the British Columbia Junior Hockey League's Interior Division all-star team which was announced today. Second-place PPenticton Knights had four players selected while third-place Vernon Lakers captured five selectios. However, in the Coastal Division, Delta Flyers edged the first-place Sockeyes with five all-star selections to four for the Richmond club. Both division-leading coaches rated all-star mention with Bill Hogaboam of Kelowna handling the Interior team and Orland Kurtenbach of Richmond taking the Coast squad.
