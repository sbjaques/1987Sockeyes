I ABOUT THE PANTHERS: After winning in Boston and Hartford, the Panthers are 4-1-2 in their past seven games, have won three in a row are tied with the Sabres for sixth in the Eastern Conference playoff race. G John Vanbiesbrouck has led the way. He's 3-0-2 in the past five he's started. In the last 16 periods he's played, he's stopped 162 of 167 shots. Keep an eye on RW Scott Mellanby: in 23 games against the Sabres, he's scored 10 goals. D Randy Moller (sprained left knee) is out. ABOUT THE SABRES: They've lost twice in their past nine games, but it's not as glamorous as you think - 3-2-4. They had a four-game unbeaten streak stopped Saturday when the Penguins won 6-2. C Dale Hawerchuk, who has missed six games with a groin pull, is expected to be in the lineup. LW Brad May is out four to six weeks with a broken right hand. D Alexei Zhitnik missed Saturday's game with pain from a broken thumb. D Richard Smehlik and LW Peter Ambroziak are day-to-day with the flu.
