Red Wings 4, Maple Leafs 2: Dino Ciccarelli scored two goals, and Ray Sheppard notched his 11th of the season in host Detroit's victory against Toronto. Ciccarelli's power-play goal triggered a three-goal third period for Detroit that wiped out a 2-1 Toronto lead after two. Blackhawks 3, Sharks 2: Tony Amonte and Christian Ruuttu scored in the third period to break a 1-1 tie for host Chicago against San Jose. Blues 4, Oilers 0: Curtis Joseph recorded his fifth career shutout and Craig Johnson and Brett Hull each had a goal and an assist as St. Louis continued its home mastery over Edmonton Oilers. The Blues have not lost to the Oilers at home since Jan. 2, 1990. Elsewhere SABRES: Defenseman Alexei Zhitnik, a key player in last week's multi-player trade with the Los Angeles Kings, will be out for at least two weeks with a broken left thumb. BRUINS: Swedish star Mats Naslund laced up for practice Monday, his first day on NHL ice in five years. Naslund, 35, was expected to sign today.
