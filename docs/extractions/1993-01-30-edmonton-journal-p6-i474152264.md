Liz Czenczek has surprised many with her decision not to pack away her curved stick for good. At an age when most international athletes are quitting or contemplating retirement, the Edmonton field hockey player has launched a comeback. Czenczek, 28, retired from the national women's team after the 1988 Seoul Olympics. But she's back bidding for a position with thoughts of playing through the 1994 World Cup.
