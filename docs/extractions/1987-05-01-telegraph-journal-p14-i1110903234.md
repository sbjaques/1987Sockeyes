VIENNA (CP) - Saying Canadians never can get enough of good hockey, Al Eagleson, the organizer of the Canada Cup tournament, has released a schedule of 34 games to be played this summer among the best players in the world. Team Canada opens camp in Montreal on Aug. 3. There are 14 exhibition games in the Canada Cup schedule and 20 tournament games, if the final goes the maximum three games. Hamilton gets 10 games. The six-team round-robin tournament begins Aug. 28 and ends Sept. 6. The playoffs are scheduled to begin Sept. 8. "We think Hamilton will do well," Eagleson said at a news conference Thursday. "It's going to be a big test for the Hamilton market." Tickets will be costly.
