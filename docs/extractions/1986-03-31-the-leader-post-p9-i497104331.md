However, the Pats picked up three more injuries in Medicine Hat. Terry Lloyd and Mark Janssens sustained left-shoulder injuries and Ken McIntyre's right eye was swollen almost shut after he was the victim of an attack by Tigers' Mike Claringbull. McIntyre and d Claringbull were jostling near the Pats bench midway through the third when Claringbull started going at McIntyre with a cast that is supporting his broken right wrist. Claringbull received a match penalty and was suspended for last night's game against Prince Albert. But while they were shorthanded for five minutes because of Claringbull's antics, the Tigers still managed two goals. Lloyd and McIntyre didn't dress against the Broncos. Janssens did but played sparingly. Brent Fedyk also played in spots for the third consecutive game. He is still bothered by a bruised back. More WHL on Page B4.
