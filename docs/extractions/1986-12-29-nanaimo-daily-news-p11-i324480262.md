OCTOBER efforts as Nanaimo Clippers outlasted Abbotsford Falcons 9-7 in BCJHL action at MSA Arena in Abbotsford. 16-Nanaimo Clippers reached an agreement with Victoria Cougars of the Western Hockey League to affiliate the two clubs. Victoria ended their agreement with Juan de Fuca during the off-season. 17-Nanaimo Clippers dropped an 8-6 BCJHL decision to Langley Eagles before 705 fans at Civic Arena. At one point, Nanaimo held a 2-0 lead. 18-NDSS Islanders. won their own Invitational Volleyball tournament as they upset No. 1 ranked Mount Douglas 10-15, 15-8, 16-14 in the best-of-three final. Alan Richardson, Si Huynh Warren Boquist all made the all star team. 19-Coast Bastion United got goals from Russell Cripps and John Timothy in edging Victoria Sagres 2-1 in Vancouver Island premier division soccer action at May Bennett-Pioneer Park. 20-John Barsby Blazers finished a four-team double round-robin volleyball tournament with a perfect 6-0 record at Wellington school. 22-Nanaimo Clippers absorbed a 9-2 beating at the hands of Richmond Sockeyes in BCJHL action at Minoru Park Arena in Richmond. 25-Tony Bobbitt scored three times to lead Richmond Sockeyes to a 74 BCJHL win over Nanaimo Clippers before 700 fans at Civic Arena. 26-D. Robinson Contracting lost a 9-0 Vancouver Island Soccer League premier division game to Victoria Athletics at Caledonia Park. 28-Neil Eisenhut scored at 8:25 of. sudden-death overtime as Langley Eagles beat Nanaimo Clipper 5-4 in BCJHL action before 400 fans at Civic Arena. 29-Columbus Hotel shaded Commercial Hotel 3-2 in Nanaimo and District Senior Soccer League action at Harewood Centennial Park.
