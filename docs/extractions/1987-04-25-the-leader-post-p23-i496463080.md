## Paragraphs

The Saskatchewan Television Network will be broadcasting the final game of the Centennial Cup hockey championship live from the Humboldt Uniplex on May 9 at 7:30 p.m. The Centennial Cup is a national tournament involving the top Tier Two junior teams in Canada. The Humboldt Broncos are an automatic entry because they are the host team. A Sask Tel fibre optics network will transmit the signal to CKTV in Regina, CFQC in Saskatoon, CICC in Yorkton and CIPA in Prince Albert. The broadcast crew includes Roger Millions of QC8 in Saskatoon, Bob Huber of CKTV and Jay Boyd of CICC. c
