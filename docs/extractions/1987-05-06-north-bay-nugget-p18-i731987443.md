HUMBOLDT, Sask. (CP) The Pembroke Lumber Kings clinched a spot in the semi game of the Centennial junior A hockey championship tournament Tuesday night with an 8-4 victory over the Dartmouth Fuel Kids. The Ontario champions will meet the loser of today's final round-robin game between the Humboldt Broncos of Saskatchewan and the Richmond Sockeyes of British Columbia, who both have 2-0 records in the tournament.
