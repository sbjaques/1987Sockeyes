RHS Wins First NORTHFIELD Gene Campbell and Darren Coloutti scored to lift Rutland to a 2-1 victory over Northfield in high school hockey on Wednesday night, giving the Raiders their first victory of the season. John Chamberlain scored for Northfield to knot the score at 1-1, setting the stage 1 for Coloutti and his game-winner. Mark Guenther and Matt Daley were credited with the assists. Rutland, 1-4, now plays in the Rutland Holiday Hockey Tournament on Friday and Saturday. "We played very well. We outshot them by a pretty wide margin," said Rutland coach Glenn Olson. "We missed a lot of shots, we're still not finishing. "But we had a lot of kids out sick and we had a lot of kids playing sick. It was a good gut check for us."

Devils Still Perfect GRANVILLE, N.Y. The Poultney Blue Devils remained undefeated (5-0) as they beat Salem, N.Y. in the first round of the boys Granville Legion Christmas Tournament Wednesday night. They will play Granville in the finals tonight. Jason Phillips led Poultney with 31 points and 13 rebounds while Dan Foley had 18 points and 13 rebounds. John Handley also chipped in with 10. Salem got 23 from Justin Brown.

Bratt Ices U-32 BRATTLEBORO - Brattleboro scored seven second-period goals en route to an 11-2 victory over U-32 in boys' hockey on Wednesday night. Bryan Bassler and Jesse BelcherTimme had three goals apiece for the 2-2 Colonels.
