## Paragraphs

By The Canadian Press The Richmond Sockeyes and Juan De Fuca. Whalers played to arr 8-8 overtime tie in a penalty-filled B.C. Junior Hockey League game Wednesday night in Richmond. The Sockeyes. appeared to have the game in hand until a string of penalties left them playing shorthanded for the last 14 minutes of the third period. The Whalers fired three power play goals to tie the game. Jason Talo got two goals for Richmond, while Mauro Padula, Mario Desjardins, Greg Hadden, Keith Fair, Rob Clarke and Kirk Phare added singles.
