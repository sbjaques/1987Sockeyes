The Canucks are getting old in goal with Richard Brodeur, are too young on defence to be effective and didn't have a 40-goal man on the forward lines. Neale, Bill LaForge, Roger Neilson, Orland Kurtenbach, Phil Maloney, Bill McCreary, Vic Stasiuk and Hal Laycoe have coached the Canucks during their first 15 years in the NHL. Watt will attempt to be successful where they have failed; no Vancouver coach has had a winning record, although Neilson came close at 51-61-21. Watt left the Canucks on good terms in 1981 to become head coach of the Jets. Winnipeg improved by 48 points that winter while the Canucks went to the Stanley Cup final with Neilson after a league suspension to Neale. It was downhill after that for both Watt and the Canucks. Watt eventually was fired by the strong-minded Ferguson after his coach had a run-in with scoring star Dale Hawerchuk, one of the NHL's most gifted players.
