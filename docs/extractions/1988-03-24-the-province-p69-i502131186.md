By FRANK LUBA Sports Reporter Speed kills. The victims Tuesday night were the defending B.C. Junior Hockey League champion Richmond Sockeyes, who were mowed down 6-2 at Richmond Arena by the swift-skating and even swifter-passing Vernon Lakers. Vernon, the Interior Division champion, leads the best-of-seven league final 3-0 and had a chance to eliminate the Coastal Division champions in last night's fourth meeting in Richmond.
