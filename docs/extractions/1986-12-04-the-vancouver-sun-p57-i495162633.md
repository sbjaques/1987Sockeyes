## Paragraphs

Bill Hardy scored at 3:50 of sudden-death overtime to give the Richmond Sockeyes a 3-2 B.C. Junior Hockey League win over the Langley Eagles Wednesday night. The teams were tied 1-1 after one period and Richmond led 2-1 after 20 minutes. Goaltenders Frank Romeo of Richmond and Jamie Stewart of Langley both had outstanding nights - Romeo stopping 43 of 45 shots and Stewart turning aside 47 of 50 shots. Jason Phillips and Tony Bobbitt scored the other Sockeye goals while Bryon Moller was credited with both Eagles markers. Richmond leads the Coastal Division with 21 wins against six losses while Langley is in fourth with a 12-11-1 record.
