Centennial Cup champs HUMBOLDT, Sask. (CP) Jason Phillips scored three goals to lead the Richmond Sockeyes to the Centennial Cup junior A hockey championship Saturday night with a 5-2 win over the Humboldt Broncos. But the Sockeyes had to fall back on an outstanding performance in goal by Frank Romeo, who stood up to 46 Humboldt shots while his teammates managed only 23 at the Broncos net. Romeo was named most valuable player in the four-team, round-robins series. British Columbia's Sockeyes skated to period leads of 2-0 and 2- 1 before Paul Rutherford scored the winning goal in the third. Dave Tomlinson also scored for Richmond, which took seven of the game's 12 minor penalties. Curtis Chamberlin and Kevin Luke scored for the Saskatchewan champion Broncos.
