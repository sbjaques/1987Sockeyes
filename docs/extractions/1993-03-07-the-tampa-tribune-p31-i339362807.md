Bergeron 24 Mike Greenlay 7 0 GOALTENDERS

Meet the stars of the Tampa Bay Lightning - Matt Hervey, Stan Drulia, Keith Osborne, Shayne Stevenson and J.C. Bergeron. Well, they're not the stars this year. But, if Lightning General Manager Phil Esposito has a say - - and he does - the Fab Five will be stars for the Lightning, maybe as early as next season. Currently, they're stars with the Atlanta Knights of the International Hockey League. With 19 games remaining, those five and others could see action in Lightning uniforms before the season is over. All have played at one time this season with the Lightning except for Hervey, who may be the best prospect the Lightning have in Atlanta. Osborne is with the Lightning now. Two weeks ago, before Osborne was called up by Tampa Bay, Atlanta 1 coach Gene Ubriaco said, "This is an outstanding minor-league team. We have scorers, great goalies and solid defensemen. Matt Hervey is going to be a star in the NHL. Mark it down. All he needs, all they all need, is NHL experience." And, according to Esposito, that's exactly what they will get within the year.
