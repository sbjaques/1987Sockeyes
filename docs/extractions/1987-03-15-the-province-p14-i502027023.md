## Paragraphs

Sockeyes in final

As expected, Richmond Sockeyes have captured the Coastal Division championship of the B.C. Junior Hockey League. The defending division champions needed overtime, however, and a goal by Paul Rutherford at 4:30 of the first extra period to record a 6-5 decision over Delta Flyers last night at Sungod Arena. Richmond's victory gave it a sweep of the best-of-seven series, which began with a double-overtime game the Sockeyes won 7-6. Mike McCormick, who had the winning goal in the double-overtime game, had what proved to be the winner again Friday. The veteran-loaded Sockeyes now advance to league final against Kelowna Packers, who beat the Vernon Lakers 6-3 Friday to win the Interior Division final 4-2. Play in the best-of-nine BCJHL final opens with 7:30 p.m. games Tuesday and Wednesday at Richmond Arena before shifting to Kelowna.
