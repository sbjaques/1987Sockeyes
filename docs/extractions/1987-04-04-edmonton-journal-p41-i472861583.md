By HEATHER LaROI Journal Staff Writer They've lit up arenas, fields, courts and spectators' faces with their feats all year. Friday, it was time to return the favor. At its 57th annual Color Night, the University of Alberta honored Elizabeth Czenczek, Parie Proft and Mike Kornak as its most outstanding female and male athletes of the year. Czenczek, centre halfback and captain of the Pandas' hockey team, was named the 39th recipient of the Bakewell Trophy. And, for only the third time since 1934, the U of A named co-winners of the Wilson Challenge trophy: Kornak, forward and captain of the Bears' basketball team, and Proft, and alternate captain of the hockey team. All three were recognized 1 not only for athletic prowess, but for their leadership, character and academic ability.
