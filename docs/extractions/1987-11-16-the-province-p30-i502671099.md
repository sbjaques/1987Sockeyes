Buckaroos 4, Juan de Fuca Whalers 8 Chilliwack Eagles 4, Merritt Centennials 8 Salmon Arm Tigers 3. Jason Talo scored four goals in Abbotsford to lead the Sockeyes to their first win in five games. Doug McDonald, Brad Trudeau, Mauro Padula and Troy Kaye scored for the losers. The Lakers scored five powerplay goals against the Bucks in Summerland. Duane Dennis and Jamie Steer led the Lakers with two-goal performances. Shaun Cyr, Eric Sanford, Tony Bower and Mike Groenhuysen scored for Summerland which was outshot 40-26. Tod Demchuck scored twice to lead the Whalers in Chilliwack. The teams were tied 4-4 going into the third period when Juan De Fuca scored four unanswered goals.
