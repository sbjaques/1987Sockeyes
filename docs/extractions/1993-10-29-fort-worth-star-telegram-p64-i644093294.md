BY J THOMAS REX Special to the Star-Telegram FORT WORTH - Philosophically speaking, first-year Fort Worth Fire hockey coach Steve Harrison speaks defense. So when the Fire opens the Central Hockey League regular season against the Memphis RiverKings at the Fort Worth-Tarrant County Convention Center at 7:30 tonight, the team's style should reflect Harrison's philosophy - tight checking and defense in both zones. "My basic concept is that we worry about defense all over the ice," •Harrison said. "We want to play in the other team's zone all the time and lock them up.
