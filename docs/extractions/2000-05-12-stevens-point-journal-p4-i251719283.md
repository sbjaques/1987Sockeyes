CZENCZEK

Jane Czenczek Jane Czenczek, 29, 2846 Highway C, Mosinee, died

Knowlton. The Rev. Joseph Grassl will officiate. Burial will be in St. Francis Cemetery, Knowlton. Visitation will be from 4 to 8 p.m. today at Beste Funeral Home, Mosinee, with a prayer service at 7 p.m. Visitation will also be held from noon until the time of services Saturday at the church. She was born Feb. 20, 1971, in Stevens Point, daughter of Stanley and Bernice (Makas) Czenczek. Jane was a graduate of Northcentral Technical College and was employed at Graebel Van Lines in Wausau. She was an avid bingo player and collected dolls and bells. She was also a very loving aunt to two nieces and nephews. Besides her parents, sur-

vivors include maternal grandmother Josephine Makas, Niles, Ill.; two brothers, Steve (Marcia) Czenczek, Bellbrook, Ohio, and James (Amy) Czenczek, Wisconsin Rapids; a sister, Christine Czenczek, Stevens Point; two nieces, Victoria and Jessica Czenczek; and a nephew, Alexander Czenczek. She was preceded in death by her maternal grandfather and paternal grandparents.
