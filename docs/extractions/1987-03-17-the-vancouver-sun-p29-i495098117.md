Halward through The Detroit Red Wings have lost defenceman Doug Halward for the season after he developed phlebitis in his left leg, the National Hockey League team said today. Halward, 31, developed the inflammation and blood clots during the club's road trip, which concluded last weekend. A 12-year veteran, Halward played in six games for the Red Wings after he was acquired from Vancouver in a trade for future considerations on Nov. 21. He missed the next 17 games with a separated shoulder, returned on Jan. 17 and played in five games before suffering a knee injury on Jan. 28.
