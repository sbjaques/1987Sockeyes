S-P Services Humboldt Broncos are finding out just how tough the competition is going to be in the Centennial Cup. The Broncos, who coasted to the Saskatchewan Amateur Junior Hockey League title, were dumped 5-3 by the Richmond Sockeyes on Sunday and fell behind 3- 2 in their best-of-seven Abbott Cup series with the British Columbia champions. Both teams will be participating in the Centennial Cup tournament which begins Saturday in Humboldt. "The guys have stopped playing hockey," said Broncos coach Bernie Lynch. "We haven't come close to reaching our potential in this series. We're going to have to hand these guys a loss and a convincing one - before we get to the Centennial Cup. "Nobody likes to lose.
