Cup matchups announced The Canadian Amateur Hockey Association announced the playoff matchups for the tier two Centennial Cup junior hockey finals yesterday. The Atlantic-champion Dartmouth Fuel Kids open against the home club from Humboldt, Sask. tomorrow. The Richmond B. C. Sockeyes play Dartmouth Sunday while Humboldt meets the Lumber Kings from Pembroke, Ont. The roundrobin concludes with Pembroke against Richmond Monday, Dartmouth versus Pembroke Tuesday and Richmond meeting Humboldt Wednesday.
