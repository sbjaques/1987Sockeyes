Lane Amateur Hockey Association Winter League

WILLAMALANE SOFTBALL - An organizational meeting for new teams wishing to play in the Willamalane summer leagues will be held at 7 o'clock tonight, March 18, at the Memorial Building in Springfield. For more information, call 746-1669. WILLAMETTE KLASSIC SOCCER - Tryouts for the Willamette Wolverines U-12 girls Klassic soccer team will be held from 5:30-6:30 p.m., March 19, from 5-6:30 p.m., March 21, and from 10 a.m-noon, March 23 at Petersen Barn. For more information, call Johanna Jaehnig at 345-8153. Tryouts for the Willamette U-12 boys soccer team will be held from 5-6:30 p.m, March 19, 21-22 at Fairfield Elementary School. For more information, call John at 689-8536. SHELDON KLASSIC SOCCER - Tryouts for the Sheldon U-12 boys Klassic soccer team will be held at 5 p.m., March 18 and 20 at Monroe Middle School. Tryouts 1 for the U-14 boys team will be held at 5 p.m., March 19 and 21 at Monroe Middle School. For more information, call Hal Williams at 484-4578. KIDSPORTS SOCCER REFEREES - A meeting for anyone interested in officiating Kidsports soccer will be held at 6:30 p.m., March 19 at the Kidsports office, 2090-C Olympic St., in Springfield. For more information, call Hal Williams at 484-4578. SOCCER COACHES CLINIC - A soccer clinic for coaches will be held d from 10 a.m. to 2 p.m., March 23 at Petersen Barn. The cost is $14. For more information, call City of Eugene Recreation at 687- 5521. TRACK OFFICIALS CLINIC - A training and certification clinic for new and experienced track and field officials will be held from 10 a.m.-3 p.m., March 24 at the Brattain House, 10th and G streets, in Springfield. The cost for USATF certification is $20. For more information, call Eric Zemper at 485-4360 or Mickie Myrmo at 746-1372. LANE AMATEUR HOCKEY - Registration is available for the Lane Amateur Hockey Association 17-game adult summer league. The registration deadline is March 30. Forms are available at Lane County Ice. For more information, call Steve Hertzberg at 302-1598. Note: : Due to limited space, notices can be published only once.
