By GREG MEACHEM of The Advocate It's been a totally unpredictable season for the Red Deer Rustlers - they've lost games they were supposed to win and won at the most unexpected times. In other words, they've refused to play by the book. So why start now? Rustlers blew a chance to put the Richmond Sockeyes in cold storage Wednesday, falling 7-3 before 2,020 fans at the Red Deer Arena. The best-of-seven Western Centennial Cup semifinal series is now tied at 3-3, with the deciding game tonight at 7:30 p.m. at the Arena.
