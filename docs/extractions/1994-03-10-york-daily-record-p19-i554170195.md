HOCKEY York Pee Wees hand Cap Boys an 8-1 loss Bill Gwilt pumped in four goals as the York Ice Hockey Club Pee Wees beat the Cap Boys 8-1 last weekend. Justin McClain chipped in with a goal and five assists. Chris Leber, Dayne Grove and Mike Harper also scored for York. The York Squirts took home a secondplace trophy from the Haverford Hawks Invitational Tournament. Wintersport beat York 7-2 in the title game. Goaltender Kevin Hare made 19 saves in a scoreless tie with Haverford. York beat Springfield 7-1. For the tournament, York got three goals each from Chris Shreiner and Mark Hall, two from Jared Hynd and one apiece from David Gleason and Adam McCreesh. The York Mites beat Hershey 5-1 behind a pair of goals from Matt Daggett. Danny Smith, John Lutz and Jared Eberly also scored for York.
