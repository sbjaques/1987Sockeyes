## Paragraphs

AREA SCENE Calgarians Michelle Conn, 25, and Liz Czenczek, 24, have been named to the Canadian Women's Field Hockey Association's Olympic team. Both have international experience, with Conn having played in 39 matches and Czenczek 43. The 16-member Olympic team will train in England, June 21 to July 11, including games against Wales, Great Britain and Scotland. Canada, seeded sixth, is in the Seoul Olympics' B Pool along with Australia, South Korea and West Germany.
