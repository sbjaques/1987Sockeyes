Cup opener HUMBOLDT, Sask. (CP) - The Humboldt Broncos scored twice i in an overtime period to beat the Richmond Sockeyes 5-3 Monday night in the opening game of the -of -seven Centennial Cup Western Canada junior hockey final. Curtis Chamberlain had three goals, including the winner, for the Saskatchewan champion Broncos. Brad Bergen and Bill McDougall had the other Humboldt markers. The Sockeyes, the British Columbia champions, had goals from Bryon Moller, Matt Hervey and Jason Phillips. Richmond led 2-1 after the second period and the teams were tied at 3-3 after regulation time. Regardless of how the teams finish, both will take part in the national tier two championship which starts May 1 in Humbolt.
