PORTAGE Dave Tomlinson drilled three hits to power leagueleading Lee Realty to a 7-1 victory over Service Drugs Wednesday in Portage fastpitch softball play. Lee Realty moved to 7-1, a full game ahead of Martin Security at 5-1. Service Drugs dipped to 2-5 with the loss. Lee Realty led 2-1 after 4½ innings, but put the game out of reach with two runs in the fifth and three more in the sixth. Tomlinson, the team's left fielder, had two singles and a double. Steve Jacobs added a triple in Lee's eighthit attack. Southpaw Tom Blum pitched the first five innings and gained the win. Clark Schlender pitched shutout ball the final two frames.
