Junior Hockey League encounter at the Juan de Fuca Recreation Centre. Dennis blocked 34 shots for the Tigers and made several key saves to help Salmon Arm cling to period leads of 1-0 and 4-2. Andy Faulkner, Rod Ewacha, Shaun Crowther and Rick Krandelmeir scored for the winners in front of a crowd of 392. The loss was disappointing for the Whalers after they had played perhaps their best game of the season on Saturday, edging the league-leading Flyers 6-5 in overtime at Delta's Sun God Arena. Randy Zulinick, Glen Whitehead and Mike Van Sickle scored for Juan de Fuca in the loss. Byron DaFoe stopped 27 shots in the Whaler net. The loss dropped the Whalers' record to 6-7-1. Juan de Fuca will have plenty of time to regroup, however, as it doesn't see action again until Nov. 7 when the defending Centennial Cup champion Richmond Sockeyes visit the Juan de Fuca Rec Centre in an 8 p.m. start. The following week, Juan de Fuca has a home-and-home series with the Nanaimo Clippers, playing in Nanaimo Nov. 10 and back at home on Nov. 12.
