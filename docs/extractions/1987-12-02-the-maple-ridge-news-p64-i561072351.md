## Paragraphs

Saturday set for stars Punch and company come to Port Coquitlam this Saturday. PoCo Molson's will take on CKNW 98ers in the CKNW Orphans' fund game at PoCo Rec Centre. The PoCo team will be coached by Ernie "Punch" McLean and will feature National Hockey League veterans Darcy Rota, Gary Lupul, Chris Oddliefson, Orland Kurtenbach, Paul Shymr and Dennis Kearns. The CKNW squad will feature the faces behind the voices of the New Westminster radio station. Game time is 6:15 p.m. and admission is by donation.
