## Paragraphs

Hockey degraded On Sunday, April 12, I attended the Rustlers vs Richmond Sockeyes gäme and was disappointed in the fans, refereeing and coaching. First, the fans were throwing garbage on the ice, raw fish for example, which delayed the game. I thought Red Deer citizens had more respect for the other team. Second, the coaches appeared to have lost control of their players. In the second period there were about six to eight fights, which shows the unsportsmanship of both teams. On the other hand, in the sport of football, if a player is fighting he IS out of the game immediately. I suggest that a player who is involved fighting should be suspended. Third. the coaches must begin to implement more positive player attitudes to insure that life injuries do not occur. Injuries may ruin the futures of great lavers. The true sport of hockey is only being degraded by that style of play on Sunday. J.K. Miller Red Deer
