Shona Schleppe, Deb Covey and Liz Czenczek of Edmonton and Michelle Conn of Calgary returned to practice Monday at the Universiade Pavilion, under the watchful eye of University of Alberta Pandas' coach Dru Marshall. The Alberta contingent, with the Canadian team coached by Marina Van der Merwe, just concluded a well-deserved week off after finishing a six-week European tour with a 13-match undefeated streak in England. "We form a little quadrant around the team," said right wing Schleppe, before mentioning how she, left wing Conn, left defender Czenczek and right defender Covey have been dubbed the Prairie Dogs, or Cowboys.
