Canadian Press Rob Clark scored three times to power the Richmond Sockeyes to a 7-3 victory over the Abbotsford Falcons in the sole B.C. Junior Hockey league game played Wednesday night at Richmond Arena. Abbotsford got goals from Dean Methorst, Doug McDonald and Jeff Horbachewsky.
