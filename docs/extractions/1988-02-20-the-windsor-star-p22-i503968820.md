## Paragraphs

WILLIE POPP, the former netminder for both the Hamilton Steelhawks and the Ottawa 67s, is embroiled in the middle of a dispute in the British Columbia Junior Hockey League. League officials are investigating an incident which cost the Richmond Sockeyes the services of Poppy while he was on emergency loan to a rival team.
