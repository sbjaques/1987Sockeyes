The National Hockey League Board of Governors will hold a special meeting Friday in Chicago to discuss the possibility of owner Peter Pocklington selling shares in the Edmonton Oilers to the public, the league announced Wedneday . .. In Red Deer, Alta., the Richmond Sockeyes scored five first-period goals and coasted to a 7-3 victory over the Red Deer Rustlers to square their western Centennial Cup semi-final at three wins each Wednesday. The Sockeyes, British Columbia Junior Hockey League champions, led 5-1 after the first period and d 5-3 after the second. The Rustlers took 18 of 31 minor penalties and two misconducts. The deciding game in the series will be played tonight in Red Deer with the winner meeting the Humboldt Broncos.
