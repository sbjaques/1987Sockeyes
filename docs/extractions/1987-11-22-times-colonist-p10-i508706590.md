By Stew Lang Times-Colonist staff More consistency on the ice has led to an increased return at the gate for the Juan de Fuca Whalers. The Whalers continued to play a fast, free-wheeling and entertaining brand of hockey Saturday, shocking the Delta Flyers, the Coast Division leaders in the B.C. Junior Hockey League, 6-2 at Juan de Fuca Arena. Adding frosting to the victory was the fact that the Whalers were using five junior B players to replace four regulars sidelined with suspensions after a brawl Nov. 7 against the Richmond Sockeyes on Juan de Fuca ice.
