The Pacific Northwest Senior Open, scheduled for its inaugural staging in early August in the Tri-Cities area of Washington, has been canceled due to a shortage of entries. HIGH SCHOOLS - Perry Stubber•field has resigned as football head coach at McMinnville High School. Stubberfield, who has been a teacher and coach at McMinnville for 24 years, coached the football team for five seasons with a combined record of 23-22. He also coached the school's baseball team for 14 seasons, winning six league championships. FISHING - Sockeye salmon are slow in their migration to British Columbia's Fraser River this year and all Indian and commercial fishing seasons in the Fraser River run area are temporarily suspended, the Pacific Salmon Commission said in Seattle. Ian Todd, executive secretary of the commission, said in a telephone interview from Vancouver, British Columbia, that fish test catches and escapement counts indicate the migration is five to seven days late. He said officials don't think the low counts are due to anything more serious than a delayed migration. The suspension of sockeye fishing in *the "Fraser River Panel" covers the Canadian waters in the southern part of the Strait of Georgia, Canadian and U.S. waters in the Strait of Juan de Fuca, and the northern part of Puget Sound.
