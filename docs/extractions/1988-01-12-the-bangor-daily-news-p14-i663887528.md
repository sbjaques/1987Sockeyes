Growing up the youngest. of four children raised by Florio and Silvana Scremin, who emigrated to British Columbia from their native Italy soon after they married, Scremin played a lot of sports as a youngster. His parents were very supportive of his endeavors. Eventually, hockey became his No. 1 sport and he helped lead the Richmond Sockeyes to the BCJHL Coastal Division title.
