The past six months have been great, and to all of you involved with the 1986-87 Quesnel Millionaire's, thank's and congratulations. Yeah, yeah, yeah!

This particular group of players will never play together again. After the season is complete, the Quesnel Millionaire's will never be the same.

So many players deserve mention. Don Doucette. Joe Snyder. Clint Schwartz; Marty Peters. Todd Springman. Brad Wolgemuth. Everyone. Those that leave, and don't come back will take a piece of the Quesnel Millionaire's with them.
