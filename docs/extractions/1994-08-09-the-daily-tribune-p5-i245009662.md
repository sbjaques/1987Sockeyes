## Paragraphs

James Gauss, division manager, recently announced the following appointments at Consolidated's Biron Division. Brett Hardt, 4531 Westwood Drive, has joined Consolidated's Biron Division as a chemical materials coordinator. Hardt graduated from the University of WisconsinEau Claire in 1992 with a bachelor of science degree in biology and a minor in chemistry. James Czenczek, Mosinee, recently joined Consolidated's Biron Division as corporate process engineer. Czenczek earned a bachelor of science degree in paper science and engineering from the University of Wisconsin-Stevens Point in 1994.
