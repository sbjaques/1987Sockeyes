by Gord Kurenoff There's an old joke in junior hockey which suggests that defence is the thing players jump over to beat decurfew. Richmond Sockeyes, had the last laugh Saturday night after they punctured Abbotsford Falcons 'swiss cheese defence' en route to a 15-2 cakewalk at MSA Arena. "It was a little hard to take," understated Falcons' assistant coach Jack Goeson following the one-sided affair. "We came up with a pretty poor effort."
