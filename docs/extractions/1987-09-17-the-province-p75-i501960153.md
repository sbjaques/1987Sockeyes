Canadian Press KELOWNA - Don't expect Richmond Sockeyes and Kelowna Packers to run away with divisional titles again this year in the B.C. Junior Hockey League. The BCJHL opens its 27th season tomorrow and Vernon Lakers are touted as the team to beat in the Interior Division with Delta Flyers favored in the Coastal Division.
