## Paragraphs

On this occasion, the gamble didn't pay off. The Juan de Fuca Whalers pulled their goalie in favor of an extra skater as they attempted to move into a with the Richmond Sockeyes in final minute of their B.C. Junior Hockey League game late Saturday. But it was the Sockeyes who scored, into an empty net, to claim a victory at the Juan de Fuca Recreation Centre. Brian McFarlane scored for mond, the defending Centennial champions, with two seconds remaining in the game. Tony Bobbitt, Mario Desjardins and Rob Clarke had a each for the Sockeyes who took period leads of 2-1 and 3-2. Mike Vansickle and Dale Walsh both tallied once for Juan de Fuca. "Richmond has lost a lot of players (through graduation) but they're a strong team," said Whalers Dale Sproule. "You hate losing but we did get strong performance from some young kids. They turned in a defensive game."
