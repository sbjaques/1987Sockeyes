Texas Rangers Signed infielder Mario Diaz to a minor -league contract. Signed pitchTerry Burrows and infielder Jon Shave to one-year contracts. Acquired pitcher Terry Bross from San Diego and pitcher Marc Lipson from Montreal. Boston Red Sox - Hired Ray Fagnant as full-time scout. Chicago White Sox Agreed to terms with outfielder Ellis Burks on a one-year contract. Atlanta Braves - Agreed to terms with infielder Bill Pecota on a two-year contract. Designated pitcher Dennis Burlingame for assignment. Pittsburgh Pirates Agreed to terms with outfielder Lonnie Smith and first baseman Ben Shelton on one-year contracts. Designated Shelton for assignment. San Francisco Giants - Hired Brian Sabean as assistant to the general manager and vice president of scouting and player personnel, and Tony Siegle as vice president of baseball administration and operations. BASKETBALL Phoenix Suns - Placed forward Jerrod Mustaf on injured reserve. San Antonio Spurs - Waiver center William Bedford. Grand Rapids Hoops (Continental Basketball Association) - Signed guard Tony Harris. La Crosse Catbirds (CBA) - Signed guard Mark Skogen. Rapid City Thrillers (CBA) - Signed forward Ralph Lewis. FOOTBALL Houston Oilers - Fired defensive coordinator Jim Eddy and defensive backs coach Pat Thomas. HOCKEY NHL - Suspended former New York Rangers coach Roger Neilson and Winnipeg Jets right wing Tie Domi and fined each $500 for a premeditated fight between Domi and Bob Probert of Detroit on Dec. 2. New York Islanders - - Assigned center lain Fraser to Capital District of the American Hockey League. New York Rangers -Fired coach Roger Neilson. Hired Ron Smith as interim coach. Binghamton Rangers (American Hockey League) - Hired Colin Campbell coach.
