Bismarck Century opened its wrestling season Friday with a 40- 22 victory over Mandan at the CHS gymnasium. The Patriots wrapped up the match by notching pins in the final three matches - Matt Geiger (171) pounds, Mike McCormick (189) and heavyweight Todd Zahn. Earlier victories by decision came from Troy Sabot (103), Glenn Shupe (125), Jason Kramer (135) and Dave Graf (152). Kyle Peterson (112) also scored a pin for the winners. Mandan got pins from Brandon Ehlis (130) and Craig Zachmeier (145) and decisions from Rob Barchenger (119), Chris Fleck (140) and Jeff Howard (160).
