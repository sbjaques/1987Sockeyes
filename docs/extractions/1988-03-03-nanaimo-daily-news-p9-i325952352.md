Larry McNabb said he would bet the farm on a Nanaimo Clipper victory Wednesday night at Civic Arena. And Delta Flyer coach Rick I Hopper, given the chance, probably wouldn't have taken McNabb up on such a bet. However, the Flyers lived up to their nickname in rolling to a relatively easy 6-1 win before 957 B.C. Junior Hockey League fans. Delta took the Coastal Division semi-final series 4-2 and now face Richmond Sockeyes in the final. The Sockeyes dusted off Juan de Fuca Whalers 6-1 to clinch their series. "I wasn't too optimistic coming over here," said Hopper. "We did some excellent forechecking and showed a lot of character and heart. The guys really dug down for this one. Everybody played well.
