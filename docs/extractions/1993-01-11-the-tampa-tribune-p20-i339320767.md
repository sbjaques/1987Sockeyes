MAPLE LEAFS STATISTICS THROUGH

TORONTO - If the Tampa Bay Lightning are serious about making the playoffs this season and Coach Terry Crisp says they are - then they're going to have to find a way to beat the Minnesota North Stars and Toronto Maple Leafs. The Stars and Leafs have a grasp on the last two Norris Division playoff spots, and the Lightning eventually will have to jump over at least one of them if they hope to earn a postseason berth. The chances of success don't seem to be in Tampa 1 Bay's favor, though. Following their 6-4 loss to the North Stars on Saturday night, the Lightning fell to 1-5 against Minnesota and Toronto, and their fourth of nine encounters with the Leafs is tonight at Maple Leaf Gardens, where the Lightning have run into some trouble.
