Falcons' Glantz out 3-6 weeks Bowling Green senior left wing Tom Glantz is out three to six weeks with a shoulder injury suffered Wednesday in practice. He is BG's third-leading scorer with 13 points and leads the team with six goals. Freshman Kevin Armbruster, who has not played during BG's first five games, is expected to replace Glantz in the lineup this weekend at Illinois-Chicago and Western Michigan.
