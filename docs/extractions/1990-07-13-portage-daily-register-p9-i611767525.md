PORTAGE - Both winners in Portage Softball Association fast pitch action Wednesday made the most of their two hits. Lee Realty, paced by Dave Tomlinson's - two-run homer, topped league-leading Service Drug Store, 2-1. After Service grabbed a 1-0 lead in the fifth, Bryan Albrecht of Lee Realty drew a one-out walk in the bottom of the fifth. After a pop out, Tomlinson belted his round-tripper. The only other hit for Lee was a single by Adam Stein. Service managed five safeties off of Lee starter and winner Clark Schlender, but they were scattered over seven innings. In the nightcap at Veteran's Park, singles by Dave Hamilton and Scott Bublitz were all Party House could muster, but that was enough as they downed Trecek Motors, 9-0.
