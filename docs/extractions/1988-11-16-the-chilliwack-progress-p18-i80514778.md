The new look Chilliwack Eagles . or is it Penquins? Well, whatever you call them, they played winning hockey in their new colors last week, defeating Richmond Sockeyes 7-5, Friday, and Nanaimo Clippers 6-5, Saturday. Now back at the .500 mark, with 10 wins and 10 losses, the uh! . . Eagles find find themselves in fourth place in the B.C. Junior Hockey League Coastal Division standings, six points up on the fifth place Clippers and eight points back of the third place Sockeyes. The Eagles took to the ice Friday, sporting new team colors - the black and yellow of the National Hockey League's Pittsburgh Penquins, crest et all . . .
