HOCKEY Sabres sign Hawerchuk: Buffalo Sabres center Dale Hawerchuk signed a multi-year contract Friday that will pay him $1.2 million per. National Hockey League season. Hawerchuk, 29, is now the second-highest paid Sabre behind Pat LaFontaine, who earns $1.8 million per year. Boston acquired right winger Darin Kimble from Tampa Bay for center Ken Hodge and defenseman Matt Hervey. The New York Rangers signed defensemen Joby Messier and Brad Tiley and left wings Craig Duncanson and Dave Thomlinson. Messier, a distant cousin of Rangers captain Mark Messier, played four seasons at Michigan State.
