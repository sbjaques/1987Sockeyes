Rustlers thrashed RICHMOND, B.C. (CP) - Bill Hardy, Dave Tomlinson and Matt Hervey scored two goals apiece Tuesday night to lead Richmond Sockeyes to an 11-4 Centennial Cup playoff victory over Red Deer Rustlers. The victory opened a best-of-seven Western semifinal between the B.C. and Alberta Tier II junior hockey champions. Kevin Melrose, Brian Puhalsky, Greg Pulliam and Rick Anderson scored for the Rustlers, who got three goals on the power play.
