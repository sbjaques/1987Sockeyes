Sockeyes sock it to Broncos with Centennial Cup victory

HUMBOLDT, Sask. (CP) Jason Phillips scored three goals to lead the Richmond Sockeyes to the Centennial Cup junior A hockey championship Saturday night with a 5-2 win over the Humboldt Broncos. But the Sockeyes had to fall back on an outstanding performance in goal by Frank Romeo, who stood up to 46 Humboldt shots while his teammates managed only 23 at the Broncos net.
