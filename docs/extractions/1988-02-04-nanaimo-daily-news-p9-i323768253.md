## Paragraphs

RICHMOND, B.C. (CP) - Rob Clarke, scored twice Wednesday night to lead the Richmond Sockeyes to a 6-4 B.C. Junior Hockey League victory over. the Delta Flyers. The win moved Richmond to within one point of -place Delta in the Coastal Division. The teams were tied 3-3 after one period and Richmond held a 5-3 lead after 40 minutes. Dwayne Wills, Tony Bobbitt, Garth Lamb and Wayne Stripp added singles for Richmond. Jeff Nottingham scored twice for Delta. Peter. Holmes and Darren Schwartz had the others. Delta outshot Richmond 39-28.
