1994-95 SEASON: The Buccaneers finished 34-2 and won the Class 6A state title. They were ranked No. 1 in Class 6A over latter part of the season and placed 15th in the USA TODAY national poll. COACH: Charlie Brinkerhoff (4th year, 85-10). ATHLETES TO WATCH: G T.T. Toliver (6-0, Sr., 11.8 ppg, 6.3 apg); F Joe Giddens (6-1, Sr., 11.6 ppg, 7.0 rpg); F/C Anthony Mims (6-5, Jr.); F/C Alonzo Barkley (6-6½, Sr.); G Phillip Adderly (6-0, Jr.); G Chris Carter (6-0, So.); F/C Matt Robinson (6-4, Fr.); G Tony Bobbitt (6-3, Fr.). OUTLOOK: Don't grieve for the Bucs in the post-Vince era (if you don't know who Vince is, turn on the television this winter). Here are the transfers: Mims from Seabreeze, Barkley from Orlando Evans and Adderly from Fort Lauderdale Dillard, the team Mainland beat in the Class 6A final last year. Freshmen Robinson and Bobbitt should make an impact on the varsity right away, along with sophomore Chris Carter, Vince's brother. Oh yeah, and Mainland also return a pair of all-county performers in Toliver and Giddens. "T.T. and Joe won't get the respect they deserve until we go out and win a few games," Brinkerhoff said.
