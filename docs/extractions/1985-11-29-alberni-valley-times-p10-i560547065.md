Richmond Sockeyes

It was just another night at the office for Richmond Sockeyes of the British Columbia Junior League. The Sockeyes whipped Merritt Warriors of the Interior Division 15-3 and coach Bob Wilms said his club must continue to look to Interior clubs for opposition the Coast Division can't provide. "We've gone two games scoring 13 and 15 goals," said Wilms. "We're probably playing with the best intensity and puck movement that we've had all year. "*Our intensity is up to the level to where it has to be for us to beat the Interior teams. We're running away with the Coast but we have to gear our game to the Interior teams."
