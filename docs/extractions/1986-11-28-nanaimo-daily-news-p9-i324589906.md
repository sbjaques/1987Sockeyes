VICTORIA (CP) Provincial coach Cam Kerr of North Vancouver has been named an assistant coach for Team Canada at next month's pre hockey tournament. Calgary, the B.C. Amateur Hockey Association announced Thursday. He will be a special aide to head coach Dave King in the series of games against the national teams of the Soviet Union n and Czechoslovakia at the Labatt Calgary Cup. Kerr coached the Pacific Region under-17 team to a bronze medal last year at the Quebec Esso Cup.
