WEDNESDAY, August 24, 1994 The Sun /C3 Jets give the boot to Cup star Meola Maybe Tony Meola should have stuck with soccer after all. The U.S. goalkeeper who played well during the recent World Cup was waived by the New York Jets Tuesday. Only one of Meola's five preseason kickoffs reached the end zone, but coach Pete Carroll said the team hopes to keep him around.
