## Paragraphs

HUMBOLDT, Sask. (CP) - Bill McDougall had a five-point night with three goals and two assists as the Humboldt Broncos blasted the Selkirk Steelers 9-1 Monday to win a Centennial Cup western semifinal series in four games straight. Rod Kavanaugh, with two, Brad Bergen, Morgan Watts, Dave Shyiak and Cory Belitski also scored for the Saskatchewan Junior Hockey League Broncos, who led 2-0 and 6-1 at period breaks.
