(CP) Orland Kurtenbach, who coached Richmond to a national championship last season, has agreed to return to the Sockeyes for another year, the British Columbia Junior Hockey League team announced Monday. Kurtenbach guided the Sockeyes to the Centennial Cup title during his first season behind the Richmond bench. The former National Hockey League player was the first captain of the NHL Canucks in 1970 and later coached the team for two years.
