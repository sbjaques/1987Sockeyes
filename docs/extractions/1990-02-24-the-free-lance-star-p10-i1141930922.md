## Paragraphs

MWC shot poorly from all areas in a 75-47 loss to Massachusetts Institute of Technology in the first round of the Eastern Invitational Tournament in Washington. The Eagles (4-20) hit only 32 percent (21-of-66) from the field, including two-for-15 from three-point range. MWC also struggled at the foul line, converting just three of 10 opportunities. MIT (11-11) scored nine straight points for an 11-2 lead and never looked back. Trae Shattuck led the Engineers with 19 points, Chris Sonne and Dave Dellagrotte had 15 each and Dave Tomlinson scored 14. Eddie Fields was the only MWC player to recah double figures, scoring 12 points. Teammate Alvin Buchanan had 11 rebounds. MIT will play host Catholic today, while the Eagles are relegated to the consolation bracket. In another first-round game, Emory topped Babson 82-76.
