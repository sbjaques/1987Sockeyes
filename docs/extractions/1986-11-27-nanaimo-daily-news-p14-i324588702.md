## Paragraphs

RICHMOND, B.C. (CP) - Rob Clarke scored two goals to lead the Richmond Sockeyes to a 7-2 win over the Abbotsford Falcons in a B.C. Junior Hockey League game Wednesday night. Tony Bobbitt, Paul Rutherford, Trevor Hendry, Dave Tomlinson and Jason Talo added singles for the winners. Abbotsford was shut out for the first 40 minutes before Joe Sobotin and Brad Smiley scored in the third period. Abbotsford goalie Darcy D'amours made 38 saves while Frank Romeo turned aside 31 shots at the Richmond net. ICE CHIPS: Vernon Lakers have acquired goalie Jason Palmer from Langley Eagles in return for forward Wes Gentles. Palmer, 18, is in his second year, while Gentles, 20, is in his third season. . . Next action for Nanaimo Clippers is Friday night when they entertain Abbotsford Falcons. Game time at Civic Arena is 8 p.m... Spokane Chiefs stopped Portland Winterhawks 6-5, while Calgary Wranglers doubled Prince Albert Raiders 4-2 in the only WHL games played Wednesday night.
