## Paragraphs

One-time Vancouver Canuck draft choice Brian McFarlane scored two goals and added four assists, leading Richmond Sockeyes to an 8-6 victory over Chilliwack Eagles Friday in the B.C. Junior Hockey League season opener for both teams. Mario Desjardins added two goals and three assists for the visiting Sockeyes, the defending league and Centennial Cup champions. In other games, Dowell Frantz scored at 1:41 of sudden-death overtime to give Penticton Knights a 6-5 victory over Vernon Lakers in Penticton, and Darren Biggs' hat trick sparked Nanaimo Clippers over Merritt Centennials 10-5 in Nanaimo.
