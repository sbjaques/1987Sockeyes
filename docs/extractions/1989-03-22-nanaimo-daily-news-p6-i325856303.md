## Paragraphs

NEW WESTMINSTER, B.C. (CP) Tony Bobbitt's overtime goal gave the Westminster Royals a 7-5 win over the Powell River Paper Kings on Tuesday, and a comeback 4-3 win in the best-of-seven Junior Hockey League semifinal series. The Royals, who at one point trailed the Coastal Division series three games to one, will meet the Interior Division-winning Vernon Lakers for the first game of the league championship series on Saturday in New Westminster. Frankie Biller led the Royals with three goals and Greg Hadden had a pair, while Blaine Moore notched a short -handed marker after Bobbitt's game-winner in the 10-minute overtime period. Judson Innes scored two for the Paper Kings, including the thirdperiod goal which tied the game 5-5
