## Paragraphs

Stu Henderson added singles for the Centennials. Gary Taylor, Shane Varey, Brent Ritchie and Kirk Young scored for Summerland. In Richmond, Rob Clarke scored two goals for the Sockeyes in a 7-1 win over Penticton Knights. Jason Talo, Dwayne Wills, Keith Fair, Barry Kress and Wayne Stripp added singles for Richmond. Brad Heschuk scored for Penticton, who dropped all three games of their Coastal road trip. In Victoria, Mike Van Sickle scored two goals for the Whalers, giving him a league-leading 53 goals so far this season, as Juan de Fuea bested Delta Flyers 7-3. Seven players from each team were ejected during the game with game misconducts for fighting. Scott Miller, Clayton Fahey, Todd Demchuk, Kevin Robinson and Randy Zulinick added singles for the Whalers, who were outshot 50- 38. Todd Voshell scored twice for Delta, while Darren Schwartz added a single. In Chilliwack, Marc Gaultier and Cam Matches scored two goals each
