When they got off the plane, Santa made his grand entrance, temporarily trading in his traditional reindeer and sleigh for the Sarasota County sheriff's helicopter. Flight 8 gave Andy Todd, 10, and Tony Bobbitt, 7, their f first ride in an airplane. Their father, William, was shot to death last February in Bradenton. Their mother, Penny, of Bradenton, said the Hospice has been providing counseling to her sons since their father's murder. Tony said the plane ride was his favorite part of the program. "It was good, when we were on the plane and it was going around," he said.
