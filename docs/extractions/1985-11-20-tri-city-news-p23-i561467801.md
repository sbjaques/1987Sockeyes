## Paragraphs

Molsons to battle old NHLers In a event that's bound to bring back memories, the Port Coquitlam Molsons Oldtimers hockey club will play the CKNW 98's this Saturday. Bolstered by the likes of former-Canucks Darcy Rota, Orland Kurtenbach, Dennis Kearns, Paul Shymr and guest coach Ernie "Punch" MacLean, the 98's should prove formidable opposition. The game goes at 6:50 p.m. Saturday in the Port Coquitlam Rec Centre. There is no admission charge, but donations will be accepted with all funds raised going to the CKNW Orphan's Fund and PoCo minor hockey.
