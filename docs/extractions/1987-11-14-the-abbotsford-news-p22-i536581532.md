Falcons continue winning ways For the first time in the 1987-88 B.C. Junior Hockey League season, Abbotsford Falcons will be going after a third straight victory when they play host to defending Centennial Cup champion Richmond Sockeyes tonight (Saturday) at 8 p.m. at MSA Arena. ABBOTS A a FALCONS Abbotsford, who won two of three games last week on a road swing through the Okanagan, looked impressive Tuesday night at MSA when they stopped Chilliwack Eagles 7-3 behind the two-goal one-assist performance of smooth-skating centreman Blaine Moore.
