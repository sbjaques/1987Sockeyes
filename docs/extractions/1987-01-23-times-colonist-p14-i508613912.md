hang on to win MERRITT The Coastal Division held on to beat the Interior Division 5-4 in the annual B.C. Junior Hockey League all-star game Thursday. The Coastal squad held period leads of 4-0 and 5-3. Mike Knapton and Darren Biggs of the Nanaimo Clippers, Aron Nosky of the Delta Flyers, Tony Bobbitt of the Richmond Sockeyes and Brad Smily of the Abbotsford Falcons scored for the Coastal Division. Goaltender Tim Renton and Randy Zulinick of the Juan de Fuca Whalers also played on the Coastal team.
