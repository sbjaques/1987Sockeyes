McFarlane pots five for Sockeyes Canadian Press Brian McFarlane scored five goals and added an assist as Richmond Sockeyes trounced visiting Abbotsford Falcons 11-1 in the only B.C. Junior Hockey League game Wednesday night. Barry Kress had three goals and two assists for the Sockeyes, who held period leads of 1-0 and 4-0. Other Richmond scorers were Dwayne Wills, Sascha Pogor and Tony Bobbitt.
