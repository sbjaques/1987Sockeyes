## Paragraphs

Columbia Junior Hockey League action Thursday night. Richmond opened up a 3-1 lead after one period and extended the margin to 5-2 after 40 minutes. Also scoring for the Coastal Division leaders were Mike McCormick, Peter Holmes, Brian Kozak, Tony Bobbitt and Kirk Phare. Derry Menard, Ben Biagoni and Link Gaetz scored for the Flyers who were outshot 44-39. Tonight, Nanaimo visits PenticVernon is at Kelowna, the Flyers are in Langley and Abbotsford travels to Victoria to play Juan de Fuca Whalers.
