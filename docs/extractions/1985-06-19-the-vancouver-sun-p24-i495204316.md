## Paragraphs

Victoria Cougars. Dixon was active. in the trade market again Tuesday, sending 20-yearold (overage) winger Larry DePalma and midget prospect Marty Prazma to Saskatoon Blades for 19-year-old winger R.J. Dundas. Dundas is the older brother of Rocky Dundas, Montreal Canadiens' third-round selection in last Saturday's entry draft. Dundas had 18 goals and 24 assists in 42 games with the Blades last season. Dixon also announced that 19-yearold right-side defenceman Stan Czenczek has agreed to join the Bruins. Czenczek attended University of Maine this past season and was an all-star with Nanaimo Clippers in the B.C. Junior League two years ago.
