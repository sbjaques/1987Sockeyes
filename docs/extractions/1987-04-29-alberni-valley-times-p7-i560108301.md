Broncos push Sockeyes

### Region 1554,5717 → 2301,6663

RICHMOND, B.C. (CP) - Mike Gaber scored at 3:31 of overtime Tuesday night to give the Humboldt Broncos a 4-3 victory over the Richmond Sockeyes and new life in their Centennial Cup playoff series. The victory forced a seventh and deciding game in the Western Canada final here Wednesday night. Bill McDougall led the Broncos with two goals and Curtis Chamberlin was the other marksman for Humboldt which trailed 1-0 at the end of the first period and was tied 2-2 entering the third period. Paul Rutherford led the Sockeyes with two goals while Dave Tomlinson added the other goal for Richmond which was outshot 46-45. Humboldt, which got spectacular netminding from Bruce Hoffort who made 42 saves, scored twice on the power play while Richmond counted once with the man advantage. There were two key mistakes Richmond coach Orland Kurtenbach felt gave Humboldt the victory.
