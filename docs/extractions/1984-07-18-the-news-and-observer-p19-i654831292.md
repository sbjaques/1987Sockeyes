all-star team stayed alive in the Eastern North Carolina Babe Ruth baseball tournament by routing Dare County 18-2 at Havelock High School Tuesday. Raleigh lost its first game of the double-elimination tournament Monday night, 12-1 to New Hanover County, as it managed only one hit off Steve Southerland. Tuesday, the Raleigh stars eliminated Dare by scoring 11 runs in the second inning. Left fielder Tony Bobbitt went 3-for-3 in the game with two doubles and three RBI. Jon Robbins threw a two-hitter for his second win of the tournament.
