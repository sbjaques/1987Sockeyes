### Region 256,871 → 917,1559

By GREG MEACHEM of The Advocate Red Deer Rustlers management is expected to learn today the status of players Richard Anderson and Brad Wilson for Saturday's third game of the western Centennial Cup semifinal series. The two Red Deer wingers were involved with a group of unruly fans following Wednesday's 6-4 loss to Richmond Sockeyes at the Richmond Arena. Anderson, who climbed over the Red Deer bench and into the crowd, was handed a gross misconduct, while Wilson got a match penalty.
