## Paragraphs

Three Nanaimo minor hockey products and two former junior Clippers were included in the latest Central Scouting Bureau list of players eligible for the June amateur hockey draft. Trent Kaese, right-winger with Lethbridge Broncos, was ranked 64th, while e his former Nanaimo Bantam Rep teammates Lee Trim (defenceman with Brandon Wheat Kings) and Bob Foglietta (right-wing with Portland Winter Hawks) were ranked 103rd and 151st respectively. Stan Czenczek, an -minded blueliner with the B.C. Junior Hockey League Clippers a year ago, was rated 102nd. Czenczek is on a scholarship at Maine University. Ken Priestlay, a centre who joined Victoria Cougars after half a season with the Clippers during the 1983-84 season, is rated 95th. The confidential list was obtained by the Toronto Globe and Mail. Bruce Major (111th and Mickey Donner 116th), centres with Richmond Sockeyes, were the only Coastal Division players listed. Ed Cristofoli, a leftwinger with Penticton Knights of the BCJHL's Interior Division, was listed 208th.
