## Paragraphs

Alastair Dimmock was installed : as Chipping Campden's new Rotary president when the outgoing president, David Feasey handed over the chain of office. On June 30 Birmingham's senior industrial chaplain and current president of Birmingham Rotary Club, the Rev Canon Denis Claringbull preached at St James Church, Chipping Campden to mark the opening of Chipping Campden Rotary club's second year. Canon Claringbull brought greetings from one of England's oldest Rotary clubs - founded in 1914 - to Campden, one of the newest. • Campden's Women's Institute summer fayre and coffce morning this Saturday will be in aid of three charities the fund for the repair and restoration of Chipping Campden's famous 15th century embroideries; a Cobalt scanner, and WI funds. The event will be held in the Town Hall and entrance is free. The final total of £2,771.77p from this year's house-to-house collection for Christian Aid was over fifty per cent up on last year's total. £2,091.40p came from Chipping Campden and Broad Campden, £572.43p from Mickleton and £107.94p from Ebrington, Hidcotes and Charingworth. Chipping Campden Chamber of Trade held a skittles evening at the Lygon Arms Hotel which was organised by the committee under the chairmanship of Ben Yates. Liz Spittle was the best skittler and Ian Spittle was second.
