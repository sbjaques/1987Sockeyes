## Paragraphs

By FRANK LUBA Sports Reporter Hockey playoffs usually draw a crowd. But you know something is wrong when the crowd includes eight RCMP cruisers, including two dog cars. That was the scene Wednesday at Richmond Arena as they were brawling on the ice and in the stands during the second game of the B.C. Junior Hockey League's Coastal Division final between the Richmond Sockeyes and the Delta Flyers. Richmond won the game 12-4 to take a 2-0 lead in the best-of-seven series but there's no winners when 450 minutes in penalties are handed out.
