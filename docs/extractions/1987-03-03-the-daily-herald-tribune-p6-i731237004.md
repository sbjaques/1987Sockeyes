EXTRA GAME IMPORTANT "With us and Grande Prairie (homeice advantage) sure is important because we both play so well in our own buldings, said Webb. Two years ago the North Stars failed to win a game in Fort St. John. This year Grande Prairie equalled their output of last year by notching a pair of victories in the unfriendly confines of Centennial Arena. Paying heed to the Newtonian concept that for every reaction there is an equal and opposite reaction, the Grande Prairie North Stars were bitterly disappointed over losing the ice advantage. "We were looking for home-ice advantage for once," said Stars president Shaune Impey. "Just so we wouldn't have to beat somebody in there home rink. The momentum definitely shifted from Saturday night." This evening the • Stars must get back to the form they showed Saturday when they outplayed the Huskies for all but 10 minutes of the game.
