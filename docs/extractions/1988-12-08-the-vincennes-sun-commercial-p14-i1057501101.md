## Paragraphs

BALTIMORE ORIOLES -Named Greg Biagini, manager, and Dick Bosman, pitching coach, of the Rochester Red Wings, in the International league. Traded Pete Blohm, pitcher, to the Pittsburgh Pirates, completing the deal that sent Randy Milligan, first baseman, to the Orioles Nov. 9. Sent Doug Kline, pitcher, to the Montreal Expos, completing the deal that sent Mike Smith, pitcher, to the Orioles. DETROIT TIGERS -Signed Ted Power, pitcher, to a minor-league contract, and agreed to terms with Dave Bergman, first basemanoutfielder, on a one-year contract. MILWAUKEE BREWERS Traded Bill Schroeder, catcher, to the California Angels for Gus Polidor, infielder. Signed Odell Jones, pitcher, to a Class AAA contract. MINNESOTA TWINS -Acquired Todd McClure, pitcher, from the Houston Astros to complete trade that sent Mark Portugal, pitcher, to the Astros. NEW YORK YANKEES- Sent Clay Parker, pitcher, outright to Columbus of the International League. SEATTLE MARINERS -Agreed to' terms with Jeffrey Leonard, outfielder, and Tom Niedenfuer, pitcher, on two-year contracts. TEXAS RANGERS -Agreed to terms with Nolan Ryan, pitcher, on a one-year contract. National League CHICAGO CUBS -Agreed to terms with Scott Sanderson, pitcher, on a one-year contract. CINCINNATI REDS -Agreed to terms with Dave Collins, first baseman, on a one-year contract. LOS ANGELES DODGERS- -Signed Mario Soto, pitcher, to a Class AAA contract. NEW YORK METS- -Traded Wally Backman, second baseman, and Mike Santiago, pitcher, to the Minnesota Twins, for Jeff Bumgarner, Steve Gasser and Toby Nivens, pitchers. PHILADELPHIA PHILLIES- -Agreed to terms with Greg Harris, pitcher, and Mike Schmidt, third baseman, on one-year contracts. SAN FRANCISCO GIANTS -Signed Lary Sorensen, pitcher, to a Class AAA contract. Pacific Coast League ALBUQUERQUE DUKES -Named Kevin Kennedy, manager. BASKETBALL National Basketball Association PORTLAND TRAIL BLAZERS Placed Ronnie Murphy, guard, on waivers. Italian League PHONOLA ROMA -Signed Cedric Maxwell, forward. FOOTBALL National Football League NFL- Reinstated Hal Garner, Buffalo Bills linebacker, who had been on the suspended list since Nov. 9 for failing drug tests. LOS ANGELES RAMS -Claimed Eric Sievers, tight end, off waivers. placed Jon Embree, tight end, on injured reserve. MIAMI DOLPHINS -Placed Jackie Shipp, linebacker, on injured reserve. Signed Nate Hill, defensive end. NEW YORK JETS -Extended the contract of Joe Walton, head coach, through at least the 1990 season. PHOENIX CARDINALS -Waived Greg Lasker, defensive back. Activated Travis Curtis, free safety, from injured reserve, waived him, then recalled him and signed him to a new contract. HOCKEY National Hockey League NEW JERSEY DEVILS Sent Paul Y sebart, center, to Utica of the. American Hockey League. NEW YORK ISLANDERS- Fired Terry Simpson, head coach. Named Al Arbour head coach. WASHINGTON CAPITALS Sent Bill Houlder, defenseman, to Baltimore of the American Hockey League.
