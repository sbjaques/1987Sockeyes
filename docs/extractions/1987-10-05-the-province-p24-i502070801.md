Meanwhile in Vernon, the Lakers scored five straight goals in the first period on their way to defeating Richmond 7-5. Jamie Steer scored twice to lead Vernon. Single goals were scored by Ian Alger, Barry Lightfoot, Mike Barkley, Greg Parnell and Darcy Martini. Rick Rozecki replied with two goals for Richmond. Other Sockeye markers came from Ken Savage, Tony Bobbitt and Rob Clarke. It was a penalty-filled night in Summerland with Juan de Fuca barely coming out on top 5-4.
