HUMBOLDT, Sask. (CP.) - The Humboldt Broncos won a place in the final of the Centennial Cup Junior A hockey championship tournament Wednesday night with a 6-1 victory over the Richmond Sockeyes. The losers, with a round-robin record of 2-1, will meet the Pembroke Lumber Kings in tonight's semifinal.
