## Paragraphs

VETS CHARITY CHALLENGE A host of hockey legends will take to Pacific Coliseum ice March 27 all in aid of Rick Hansen's Man in Motion World Tour. The 7 p.m game "Legends of Hockey: the East-West Challenge" will features some fimilar names to fans in the Vancouver area. Long-time Canucks Bobby Schmautz and Dune Wilson will join former teammates Gregg Boddy, John Grisdale, Ivan Boldirev, Gary Bromley, Jiri Bubla, Orland Kurtenbach, Bobby Lalonde, Chris Oddliefson, Gerry O'Flahery, Darcy Rota, and Dennis Ververgaert in a battle against a star-laden eastern squad. Gilbert Perreault, Bobby Hull and Yvan Cournoyer will suit up in East team uniforms. Tickets to the game are $6, $9, and $12 and are available at all VTC/ CBO outlets. All proceeds go to Rick Hansen's fund for spinal cord research. The game is sponsored by the PNE, the Vancouver Canucks and other groups, including CP Air, Molson's, CKNW, the Town of Whistler, the B.C. Hotels Association and Olympic Fitness.
