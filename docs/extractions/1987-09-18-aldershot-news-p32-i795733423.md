## Paragraphs

CONGRATULATIONS to Aldershot soccer club's chief groundsman Dave Tomlinson (above) who marked out a double celebration this week, writes PETER HUTCHINSON.

Hard-working Dave chalked up 25 years of keeping the pitch in shape - and this year is also his 25th wedding anniversary! On top of that his son Chris scored for Aldershot Youth in the 1-1 draw with Horndean at the Recreation Ground on Tuesday in an FA Youth Cup match. Father Dave is also manager of Aldershot's Youth team! Dave lives with his wife Janet at Beaumont Park in Aldershot. They have two daughters and two sons. EMPLOYEE Dave first took charge of the Recreation Ground as a council employee 25 years ago last Saturday. He was born in the town and has supported Aldershot since a youngster. And Dave - - age 46 - made: it clear: "Aldershot soccer club is the love of my life. When the team suffers, I suffer." He added: "I cannot speak highly enough of the club. I get on well with all of them and my big ambition before I retire is to set up the pitch for Aldershot's first match in Division One. People might laugh - but that is my hope." 'PROUD' And his wife Janet confirmed: "I am very proud of Dave. He would do anything for Aldershot Football Club." Certainly the Recreation Ground pitch is regarded as one of the best in the Football League. * A lot of the credit must go to that loving care of Dave Tomlinson. Keep up the good work Dave - and we all hope your dreams of Division One turn into reality! Picture by EDDIE TRUSLER.
