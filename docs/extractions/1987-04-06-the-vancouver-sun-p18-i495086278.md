## Paragraphs

Sockeyes, Red Deer to clash Richmond Sockeyes will put their 15-game playoff winnings streak on the line Tuesday night when they entertain Alberta champion Red Deer Rustlers in the first game of the best-of-seven Centennial Cup tier two junior hockey elimination series. The Sockeyes polished off Quesnel Millionaires 5-1 Saturday to take the Mowatt Cup in two straight, their fourth consecutive series sweep. Red Deer, meanwhile, finished fourth overall in the Alberta Junior League before knocking off Calgary Spurs in seven games, Calgary Canucks in seven and St. Albert Saints in six. Game time at Richmond Arena tomorrow is 7:30 p.m., with game two going Wednesday, same time, same place. The remainder of the series will be played in Red Deer.
