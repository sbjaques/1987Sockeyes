SRI International has promoted Ruth Claringbull to western regional director, in charge of marketing, client contacts and business development in 11 western states and Canada. A 15-year SRI employee, Claringbull, 39, joined the SRI research and consulting team in her native England. She has been at the Menlo Park HQ for 11 years, most recently as Western marketing manager for SRI's Business Intelligence Program.
