## Paragraphs

Salmon Arm defeated Penticton 6-4. The Royals, who outshot Richmond 54-43. received two goal performances from Jeff McLean, Frankie Biller, Kevin Hoffman and Blaine Moore. Singles came form Curt Lehner, Don Dwyer, Trevor Renkers, Perry Neufeld and Tony Bobbitt. For Richmond, Mark Kaufmann, Brad Dickson and Gunnar Hendrickson scored singles. In 1 Merritt, Derry Menard scored three goals and Scott Didmon added a pair to help the Centennials heat the visiting Vernon Lakers 8-4.
