NFL FOOTBALL Falcons activate Rade, Phillips The Atlanta Falcons placed linebacker John Rade and wide receiver Jason Phillips on the active roster and put defensive end Mike Gann on injured reserve. Rade, who has had five consecutive 100-tackle seasons, was on the physically unable to perform list because of knee surgery last January. He is in his ninth NFL season... Efforts continued toward resolving the confused ownership of the New England Patriots after Fran Murray rejected Victor Kiam's bid for a 30-day extension to buy out his minority partner. The deadline for Kiam, who owns 51 percent of the team, to make a $38 million payment to buy out Murray passed without resolution Thursday. Murray holds the remaining 49 percent. of the franchise. Neither Murray nor Kiam was available for comment.
