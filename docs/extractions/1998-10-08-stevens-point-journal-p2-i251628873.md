Wiktoria Czenczek

Wiktoria Czenczek, 91, 2020 Clarberth St., Schofield, died Wednesday, Oct. 7, 1998, at Comfort Care and Hospice Services, Wausau Hospital. Services will be at 10 a.m. Saturday at St. Francis Xavier Catholic Church, Knowlton, the Rev. Joseph Grassl officiating. Burial will be in the parish cemetery. Friends may call from 4 to 8 p.m. Friday at Beste Funeral Home, Mosinee, where there will be a prayer service at 7 p.m., and again on Saturday from 8:30 to 9:30 a.m. at the funeral home. Wiktoria was born on April 25, 1907, in Wesola, Poland, the daughter of the late Joseph and Antoinette (Hus) Barnius.
