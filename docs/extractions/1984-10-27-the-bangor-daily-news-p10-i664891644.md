Peter Maher, John McDonald, and Santini combined on a nifty goal which made it 2-0. Maher picked up the puck at center ice and, noticing McDonald skating downn the right wing, slid it into the offensive zone. McDonald beat the defenseman to the puck behind the net and shoveled to the near post where Santini snapped it home from about five feet. But Micheletti and the Gophers quieted the large crowd by cutting the lead in half just 47 seconds later. He may not have r received an assist on the play, but it was a jarring check by Erdall that led directly to the goal. Erdall nailed Bear defenseman Stan Czenczek just before Czenczek cleared the defensive zone, and Micheletti gloved down the puck before breaking in on Roy. Using Wally Chapman at the far post as a decoy, Micheletti deked Roy at the short side before slipping it past him neatly.
