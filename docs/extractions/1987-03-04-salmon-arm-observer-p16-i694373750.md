## Paragraphs

Ned's Place of Salmon Arm won the 18th annual Kiwanis pee wee hockey tournament in Oliver over the weekend.

The Esso swim program, a competitive skills course, is being run by the Sockeye Swim Club and a new session will start on Thursday, March 12. Classes are once a week for eight weeks from 5:15 until 5:45 p.m. Starts, turns, and strokes are the skills that are worked on. It is a wonderful opportunity for children to learn about swim club. You may pre-register for this right away. In early May we will be offering the Esso swim program for instructors. Participants must be at least 16-years-old and have some background in aquatics and leadership. Call us to put your name on the interest list.
