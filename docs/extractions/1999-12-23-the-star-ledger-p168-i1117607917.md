For sophomore center Scott Eberenz of Seton Hall Prep, the time is now. With the graduation of All-State forward Brandon Doria, Eberenz, a ICE second-line center last winter, was HOCKEY promoted to the top line this campaign. He hasn't disappointed. Eberenz scored twice in the final minute of the game when Seton Hall (4-1) connected three times during a 5-1 victory over Christian Brothers yesterday at the American Hockey Center in Wall Township. After scoring into an empty net with 50 seconds on the clock, Eberenz intercepted a pass in the left circle and snapped home his sixth of the season to the short side just 15 seconds later.
