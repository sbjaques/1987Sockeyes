Bismarck and Bismarck Century held on to their 1-2 positions in the state Class A high school wrestling poll and Minot jumped five spots to third to give the West Region a 1-2- 3 sweep. The poll, which was compiled Dec. 19, puts Grand Forks Central fourth and Belcourt fifth. Bismarck boasts five individual leaders, including 1990 state champions Darin Giese and Chad Renner. The BHS leaders are Jeramie Welder, Ryan Kringlie and Ben Berogan. Topping the ratings for Century are Glenn Shupe, Mike Seeger and Mike McCormick.
