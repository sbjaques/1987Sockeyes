Bruins 2, Senators 0: Rookie Blaine Lacher stopped 28 shots to record his second shutout in 11 NHL games. Cam Neely and Dave Reid scored. Lacher improved his record to 8-2-1 in his first pro game against little-used Ottawa backup Darrin Madeley - now 3-21-5 in three seasons with the Senators. Blues 3, Maple Leafs 2: Brendan Shanahan, Glenn Anderson and Brett Hull scored for St. Louis. The Blues have won four straight and moved into second place in the Central Division, ahead of the idle Chicago Blackhawks.
