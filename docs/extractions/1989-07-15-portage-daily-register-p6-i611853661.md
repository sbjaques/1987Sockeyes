PORTAGE - League-leading Lee Realty made the most of six hits Thursday evening. Lee, riding home runs by Dave Tomlinson - a pair - - and Dave Mills, clipped Party House, 8-7, in Portage Fastpitch Softball Association play. In the second game, Village Pub exploded for seven runs in the fourth inning of its 9-3 triumph over Trecek Chev-Olds. But Thursday's action belonged to Lee, particularily Tomlinson and Mills. Tomlinson, a left fielder, smacked a two-run homer in the second inning and added a grand slam in a five-run fourth that gave Lee a 7-2 cushion. However, Party House rallied in the top of the fifth.
