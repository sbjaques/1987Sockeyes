The Whalers and Phoenix Mutual once again co-sponsor the CIAC hockey finals at the Civic Center March 13. "I think it's important to showcase athletes in a facility such as the Civic Center, to emphasize the positive side of kids because athletics develop self-discipline, determination and sportsmanship," Robert Fiondella, president of Phoenix Mutual, said. In the previous two years, the finals have drawn between 4,000 and 5,000 people. "Fans should be aware that this is high hockey as evidenced by the fact that three of the four title games in the last two years have gone to overtime," CIAC Executive Director John Daly said.
