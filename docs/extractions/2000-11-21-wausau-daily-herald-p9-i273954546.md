SCHOOL DISTRICT OF MOSINEE NOTICE OF SCHOOL BOARD ELECTION Tuesday, April 3, 2001 NOTICE IS HEREBY GIVEN that at an election to be held in the School District of Mosinee on Tuesday, April 3, 2001, the following offices are to be elected to succeed the incumbents listed below. The term of office for school board members begins on Monday, April 23, 2001. Seat Incumbent At Large (3 year term) Diane Gorman (must be a resident of the nice municipalities) 3 Town of Mosinee (3 year term) Kevin Hermening (must be a resident of the Town of Mosinee) 5 Towns of Kronenwetter/Guenther/Reid (3 year term) Bernice Czenczek (must be a resident of the Towns of Kronenwetter, Guenther or Reid) 2 Towns of Emmet/Bergen/Green Valley (1 year term) Mary Borowski (must be a resident of the Towns of Emmet, Bergen, or Green Valley) NOTICE IS FURTHER GIVEN, that a declaration of candidacy and a campaign finance registration statement, must be filed by 5:00 p.m., on Tuesday, January 2, 2001, in the school district office. NOTICE IS FURTHER GIVEN, that candidates must declare their candidacy for a specific seat (1, 2, 3 or 5). NOTICE IS FURTHER GIVEN, that if a primary is necessary, the primary will be held on Tuesday, February 20, 2001. A description of the school district boundaries can be obtained from the school district office at 591 W. Hwy. 153, Mosinee, WI 54455. Hours: 7:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. Monday - Friday. Given under my hand, on November 16, 2000 Bernice Czenczek, School District Clerk 21-IT
