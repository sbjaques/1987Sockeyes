By Larry Mahoney Of the NEWS Staff ORONO Northeastern University's Huskies got goals from Dave O'Brien, Kevin Heffernan and Greg Neary, and 22-save goaltending from Bruce Racine en route to a 4-2 Hockey East victory over the University of Maine-Orono's Black Bears here Wednesday night. 0'Brien's goal, the only one in the first period, and the second-period goals by Heffernan and Neary gave the Huskies a 3-0 lead and Racine preserved the win during the third period when the Bears spent nearly the entire period in the Northeastern end looking for the equalizer. Stan Czenczek's first collegiate goal with just a minute remaining in the second period and Dave Wensley's power-play goal only 53 seconds into the final period pulled the Bears to within 3-2, but Jim Madigan's emptynet goal with 10 seconds remaining iced it.
