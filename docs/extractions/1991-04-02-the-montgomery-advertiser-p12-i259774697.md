National Football League ATLANTA FALCONS Signed Naz Worthen, Mike Williams and Jason Phillips, wide receivers; Jeff Donaldson and Tracey Eaton, safeties; Harper Le Bel, fight end; Benji Roland, defensive lineman; Rich Karlis, kicker; and Pat Chaffey, running back. CLEVELAND BROWNS Signed Joe Morris and Lee Rouson, running backs; Ed Cunningham, offensive tackle; Fulton Johnson, wide receiver; Billy Smith, Brian Hanson, and Ralph Stephens, punters; David Brandon, linebacker; Mike Lansford, kicker; and Todd Philcox, quarterback. Released Bryan Wagner, punter, and Jerry Kauric, kicker. DENVER BRONCOS Signed Robert Perryman, fullback. HOUSTON OILERS Traded Jason Grimsley, linebacker, to Miami for a thirdround draft choice. INDIANAPOLIS COLTS Signed Travis Davis, nose tackle, and Cedric Figaro, linebacker. MIAMI DOLPHINS Acquired John Grimsley, linebacker, from the Houston Oilers for a 1991 third-round draft pick. Signed Louis Cooper, linebacker, and Charles Henry, tight end. NEW ENGLAND PATRIOTS Signed Hugh Millen, quarterback, and Ivory Joe Hunter and Victor Jones, running backs. SAN• DIEGO CHARGERS Signed Chris Pike, defensive end, and Harry Swayne, offensive tackle. SAN FRANCISCO 49ERS Signed Todd Bowles, safety; and Chris Washington, linebacker. SEATTLE SEAHAWKS Signed Marcus Cotton, linebacker, and Kevin Thompson. safety, HOCKEY National Hockey League NEW YORK RANGERS Sent Jeff Bloemberg, defenseman, to Binghamton of the American Hockey League. WINNIPEG JETS -Fired Bob Murdock, coach. North American Hockey League NAHL Awarded franchises to Hamilton, Ontario and Califonia. SOCCER American Professional Soccer League SAN FRANCISCO BAY BLACKHAWKSSigned Chance Fry and Mark Kerlin, forwards.
