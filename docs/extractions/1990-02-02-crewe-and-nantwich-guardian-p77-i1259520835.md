## Paragraphs

CONGLETON only just held on to their 100 per cent record in the Men's Division 1 of the Crewe and District Badminton League in their recent fixture at home to strugglers Ruskin. An early injury to David Marshall meant he and Shing Sven had to concede their first game to Ruskin's Peter Barber and Mike Hitchem. Gavin Simpson and Andy Coppack brought the match level by beating Steve Wrench and Nigel Downes 21-14. Dave Tomlinson and Tony Ford restored Ruskin'% lead in beating Alan Rogers and Gary McDonald 21-14. The lead changed sides throughout the night until at 4-4 Gavin Simpson and Andy Coppack finally gave Congleton the victory by defeating Dave Tomlinson and Tony Ford 21-8. This win makes Congleton clear favourites for the title with a record of nine wins out of nine played.
