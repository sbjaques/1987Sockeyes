## Paragraphs

Derek Heyes scored once to help Vancouver Island edge Saskatchewan 5-4 to win the pee wee gold medal at the Mac's hockey tournament in North Delta Saturday. Heyes, who along with Jon McColm represented the Nanaimo Pee Wee Clippers on the Island squad, added five assists during the Islanders' other games. Vancouver Island split their four round-robin games, beating Alberta 5-3 and Vancouver 6-3, but losing 41 to Saskatchewan and Fraser Valley. However, the third place Islanders bested second place Vancouver 7-2 in the semi-final before ousting Saskatchewan.
