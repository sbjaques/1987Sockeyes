Canucks add two exhibitions The Vancouver Canucks have beefed up their exhibition schedule to eight games by adding contests in Saskatoon and San Diego, the team announced Wednesday. Vancouver will play Team Canada on Sept. 18 in the new Saskatchewan Place facility and the New York Rangers on Sept. 22 in San Diego. The NHL team also announced plans to have five draft choices participate in a two-week training program starting Sunday. The players - Trevor Linden, Troy Gamble, Dan Woodley, Rob Murphy and Steve Veilleux will spend time on the ice with head coach Bob McCammon and assistant coach Jack Mcllhargey as well as working off.ice with new strength coach Wayne Wilson.
