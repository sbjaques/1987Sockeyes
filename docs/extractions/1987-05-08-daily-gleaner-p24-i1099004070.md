HUMBOLDT. Sask. (CP) The Richmond Sockeyes whipped the Pembroke Lumber Kings 9-3 in a semifinal clash Thursday night to advance to the final game in the Centennial Cup Junior A hockey championship tournament. The Sockeyes, from British Columbia, will meet the Saskatchewan champion Humboldt Broncos in the sudden-death final Saturday night. Richmond. which made it to the semifinal with a 2-1 record in the roundrobin 1 portion of the tournament. got past Pembroke on goals by Dean Rutledge. Steve Jaques, Matt Hervey, Brian Kozak, Paul Rutherford, Jason Phillips. Tony Bobbitt, Mike McCormick and Bill Hardy.
