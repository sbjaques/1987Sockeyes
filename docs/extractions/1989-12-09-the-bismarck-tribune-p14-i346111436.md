GRAND FORKS (AP) - Dixon Ward's hat trick led North Dakota to a 7-4 college hockey victory over St. Cloud State on Friday. Ward scored a pair of power play goals in the second period, one at 1:04 and other at 8:38. Jason Herter, who recorded three assists and one goal on the night, assisted on both. St. Cloud led 2-0 at the end of the opening period, with goals by Chris Schied at 7:58 and Tony Schmalzbauer at 9:03. UND came back in the second with three straight goals - Ward's two and one by Brent Bobyck at 3:11. St. Cloud's Tim Hanus answered with a score at 13:42. UND scored again on a Greg Johnson goal at 18:15. Schied slapped another goal into the net for St. Cloud at 18:37.
