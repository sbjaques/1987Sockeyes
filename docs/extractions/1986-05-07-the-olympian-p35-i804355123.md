BASEBALL American League CHICAGO WHITE SOX-Purchased the contract of Joel McKeon, pitcher, from Butfalo of the American Association. Sent Rodney Craig, outfielder, to Buffalo. BASKETBALL National Basketball Association SEATTLE SUPERSONICS -Fired Jeff Troesch, media relations director, and Jean Fisken, office manager. United States Football League MEMPHIS SHOWBOATS -Traded the ninth round selection in this year's draft to the Arizona Outlaws for the rights to Chris Brewer, running back. NEW JERSEY GENERALS -Traded the rights to Keith Byars, running back, to the Arizona Outlaws for Arizona's ninth and 10th round picks year's draft. TAMPA BAY BANDITS- Traded Ron Simmons, defensive tackle, to the Memphis Showboats for a first-round choice in the 1986 draft. HOCKEY National Hockey League LOS ANGELES KINGS--Signed Chris McSorley and Brian Martin, wings. NEW JERSEY DEVILS Announced the resignation of Lou Vairo, assistant coach. COLLEGE BRIGHAM YOUNG-Named Chris Pella football recruiting coordinator. NAVY -Named Chuck Bresnahan assistant varsity football coach, Maj. Lonnie Messick lightweight football coach and Emmett Davis assistant basketball coach. NORTHEAST LOUISIANA -Dropped varsity soccer and riflery from the athletic program. NORTHERN ILLINOIS-Named Randy Eccker assistant basketball coach. OREGON -Dropped men's and women's swimming and women's gymnastics from the athletic program. SOUTHERN CALIFORNIA-Named Ron Righter and Brian Hammel assistant basketball coaches. SOUTHEASTERN CONFERENCE nounced the retirement of Boyd McWhorter, conference commissioner. WISCONSIN-Named Andy Rein wrestling coach.
