## Paragraphs

Jason Talo notched a hat trick in Vancouver's 5-2 major bantam win over Vancouver Island. The Richmond star also had nine goals and four assists in four round robin games. The major bantam allstar team was made up of forwards Russ Romaniuk (Winnipeg), Greg Leahy (Alberta) and T.J. Courtney (New Jersey), and defensmen Bruce Dirmon (Vancouver Island) and Scott Farrell (Vancouver) as well as Vancouver goalie Bob Crockett. Sakatchewan's pee wee entry was also made up of players who are going to be suspended but the prairie 12-year-olds persevered and made it to the tournament final, only to lose 5-4 to Vancouver. Andy Toljarder was the leading Vancouver scorer with two goals and one assist.
