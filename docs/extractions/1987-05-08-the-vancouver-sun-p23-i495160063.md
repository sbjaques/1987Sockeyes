By ELLIOTT PAP Sun Sports Reporter HUMBOLDT, Sask. - The showdown that has been so widely predicted will indeed come to pass: Richmond Sockeyes and Humboldt Broncos are going to meet for the Centennial Cup Saturday night. The Sockeyes advanced to the biggest game in tier two junior hockey by waxing Pembroke Lumber Kings 9-3 in Thursday's semi-final. Although the score sounds impressive, the 'Eyes were not particularly sharp last night, a fact that didn't escape the ever -vigilant eyes of coach Orland Kurtenbach.
