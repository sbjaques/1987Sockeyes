## Paragraphs

WAY -Harry E. T., suddenly, at home in Victoria, B.C. on November 26, 1987. He will be missed by his loving family: wife, Lillian; daughter, Marforie and husband, Keith McKee of Delta: daughter, Doreen and husband. Bill Bobbitt of Penticton; grandchildren, Karen McKee, Trevor McKee and wife, Diane; Michael and Tony Bobbitt. Harry was born in Broadview, Saskatchewan in 1905. He joined the Royal Bank in 1920 and after working in various branches in Saskatchewan and Manitoba, he retired from Russell, Manitoba branch in 1965, later moving to Victoria. Private family service to be held at Sands Funeral Chapel, 1803 Quadra Street on Monday, November 30. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the charity of one's choice. SANDS- -VICTORIA
