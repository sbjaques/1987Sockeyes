## Paragraphs

from Ernie McLean. "'You never know when you're going to find a player.' The camp continues today, beginning at 7 a.m. and running through to 10 p.m. They do it all again tomorrow before going 8 a.m. to 5 p.m. Tuesday, with 25 players going on the main camp. On Wednesday, the 20 veterans report to a 60-player main camp. Two rookies being counted on are goaltender Dusty Imoo of Surrey and Alberta forward Stu Barnes. Add -acquired Mark Holick of Penticton (via the Saskatoon Blades) and the changing room is full, even without additions like 6'3", 215- pound Greg Spenrath from Sherwood Park, Swedish goalie Patrik Backlund or Steve Jaques from the Centennial Cup champion Richmond Sockeyes.
