SELKIRK, Man. (CP) - Bill McDougall, who set a Tier 2 junior hockey scoring record this season, figured in every goal the Humboldt Broncos scored Thursday as they defeated the Selkirk Steelers 7-4 to take a 1-0 lead in the best-of-seven Centennial Cup western semifinal. The flashy centre from Toronto scored three times and added four assists. "It's my best game of the playoffs by far," said the 20-year-old McDougal. "But we can play better."
