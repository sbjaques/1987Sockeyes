Mike Sanderson, leading scorer on last year's fifth-place team with 37 goals and 31 assists for 68 points, returns and leads the corps of scorers. Sanderson will be flanked by Jason Brousseau (19 goals, 16 assists, 35 points with the Fire last season) on the left and Mike McCormick, who tallied 30 points with Fort Worth last season, on the right. On the power play, McCormick will move to defense while Rob Striar plays the point for added offense. "Mike is the one guy on this team that I think I can move around to play just about every position," Harrison said. "He should give us a little more offense from the point on the power play."
