New York Rangers release goaltender Peter Maro and defencemen Paul Jenkins, Paul Moore and John Davis; reassign left winger Terry Tait to Flint of the International Hockey League; return right winger Simon Gagne to Laval of the Quebec Major Junior Hockey League, left winger Dan Lacroix to Granby of the QMJHL, left winger Shaun Clouston to Portland of the Western Hockey League, centre Mike Oliverio to Sault Ste. Marie of the Ontario Hockey League, centre Darren Turcotte to North Bay of the OHL, and centre Mark Janssens to Regina of the WHL. Toronto Maple Leafs return forwards Ken Alexander, Brian Hoard and Ron Bernacci to Hamilton of the OHL, forward Stephane Giguere to St-Jean of the QMJHL, forward Danny Hie to Sudbury of the OHL, forward Sean Davidson to Toronto of the OHL, forward Mark Holick to Saskatoon of the WHL, forward. Kent Hulst to Belleville of the OHL, forward Daniel Marois to Chicoutimi of the QMJHL, forward Trevor Jobe to Moose Jaw of the WHL, defenceman Sean Boland to Toronto of the OHL and defenceman Scott Taylor to Kitchener of the OHL. Vancouver Canucks sign defenceman Garth Butcher to a multiyear contract; release Jack Bowkus, Barry Burkholder, Mike Estey, Bruce Gilles, Ron Goodall, Lou Kiriakou, Wayne MacPhee, Gary Moscaluk. John O'Connor, Scott Rawson, Tony Smith and Ken Strong; return Steve Herniman, Jeff Noble and Jason Phillips to their junior teams.

Steve Thomas, Curt Eraser and Denis Savard had Chicago's other goals, while Rick Meagher, Greg Paslawski, Tony McKegney and Brian Sutter scored for the Blues. •Vancouver Canucks released 12 players Sunday and assigned three more: to junior hockey clubs to cut their National Hockey League roster to 46 players. Five-year veteran Garth Butcher signed a multi-year contract, the club said in news release. The six-foot-two, 205-pound defenceman scored five goals, assisted on 15 others and had 207 minutes in penalties in 70 league games last season. The club also announced that defenceman Jin Agnew is out indefinitely with a torn rib cartilege. Released were: Jack Bowkus, Barry Burkholder, Mike Estey, Bruce Gilles, Ron Goodall, Lou Kiriakou, Wayne MacPhee, Gary Moscaluk, John O'Connor, Scott Rawson, Tony Smith and Ken Strong.
