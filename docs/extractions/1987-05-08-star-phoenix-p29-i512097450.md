Friday, May 8, 1987 Saskatoon, Saskatchewan Star-Phoenix Sports B13 It's Sockeyes and Broncos in Centennial Cup final

The two prize fighters of Junior A Tier II hockey will meet for the ninth time in less than three weeks, this time with the Centennial Cup championship on the line, at 7:30 p.m. on Saturday. Richmond Sockeyes earned the chance to settle their grudge series with Humboldt Broncos by defeating the Pembroke Lumber Kings 9-3 in the semi-final on Thursday at the Uniplex.
