ABOUT THE PANTHERS: The Panthers (6-9-1) are struggling offensively. They've lost two straight and three of four. They've taken only 32 shots in their past two losses. This is the first time the teams have met this season. The Panthers were 2-3-0 against the Rangers last season. RW Scott Mellanby has a two-game goalscoring streak after going 12 games without a goal. C Dave Tomlinson was recalled Monday from Cincinnati to try to help fill the hole created when C Stu Barnes was hurt against Ottawa. Barnes (left eye) and D Keith Brown (left knee) are out. Barnes could resume skating after an evaluation Wednesday. Brown is day-to-day. D Paul Laus (strained right groin) is out about a week. I ABOUT THE RANGERS: After a slow start, the Rangers (7-7-2) have taken over the top spot in the Atlantic Division. The 103-day lockout likely benefited the Rangers more than any team, allowing them to recuperate from a short summer and a long list of injuries. C Mark Messier missed all of the shortened training camp because of a contract dispute, but he is off to a quick start with four goals and eight assists. D Sergei Zubov also has 12 points (2 goals, 10 assists), and C Adam Graves has shown few effects of back surgery.
