### Region 220,5489 → 771,7056

There's something very fishy going on in Red Deer. That's where Richmond' Sockeyes are playing off against the hometown Rustlers for the right to meet Humboldt Broncos to decide a berth in the Centennial Cup of Junior A hockey. Apparently the series has been a rough one. In the first two games last week in Richmond, a pair of Red Deer players were suspended for going into the crowd. BILL As well, Red HARDY Deer fans and ...rough series Richmond fans tussled in the stands, and that spilled over on to the ice. With the series shifting to Red Deer for the remaining games, the hometown fans have decided to get a little revenge on the Sockeyes, and they have some rather unique ways of doing it. The most popular has been to throw salmon on to the ice. And we're not talking the canned variety either. Real whole salmon with a slice in the belly, SO that when it hits the ice, all the guts make a nice mess on the ice.
