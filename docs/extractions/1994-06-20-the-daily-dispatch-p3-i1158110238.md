## Paragraphs

An Oxford driver ran a stop sign and was involved in a collision at the corner of Nicholas and Davis streets Saturday, according to Henderson Police Department reports. Officer J.C. Bowden said a 1990 Chevrolet driven by Tony Bobbitt, 19, of 119 Lewis St. was westbound on Davis Street. Bobbitt told officers that he was lost and failed to notice the stop sign. The Bobbitt car then plowed into a 1991 Eagle driven by Carolyn Taylor Fields, 38, of Fayetteville, who was heading north on Nicholas Street, Bowden reported. The left front side and center of the Fields car collided with the back left bumper of Bobbitt's, causing $4,000 worth of damage to the Eagle. Bobbitt was cited for a safe movement violation. No injuries were reported in the wreck.
