BASEBALL American League KANSAS CITY ROYALS--Sent Van Snider, outfielder, Terry Bell, catcher, Rondin Johnson and Luis Delos Santos, infielders, and Gary Thurman and Chito Martinez, outfielders, to their minor -league camp for reassignment. MILWAUKEE BREWERS--Sent Joey Meyer, first baseman, Al Jones, Alex Madrid and Don August, pitchers, and Billy Bates, infielder, to their minor -league camp for reassignment. NEW YORK YANKEES Named Roy White minor league hitting instructor. TEXAS RANGERS--Sent Dwayne Henry, Frank Pastore and Jeff Zaske, pitchers, and Dave Owen, infielder, to their minor-league camp for reassignment. TORONTO BLUE JAYS Optioned Sil Campusano and Otis Green, outfielders, and Santiago Garcia, second baseman, to Knoxville of the Southern League; Sent Steve Davis, Todd Stottlemyre, Tony Hudson and Tom Filer, pitchers, Tom Quinlan, infielder, and Greg David, catcher, to their minor league camp for reassignment. BASKETBALL National Basketball Association NEW YORK KNICKS-Announced that Patrick Ewing, center, will miss the remainder of the season with a knee injury. HOCKEY National Hockey League BUFFALO SABRES- Called up Bob Logan, left wing, from Rochester of the American Hockey League. Sent Paul Brydges, center, to Rochester. HARTFORD WHALERS--Signed Jean Marc MacKenzie, center, and assigned him to Binghamton of the American Hockey League. NEW JERSEY DEVILS Sent Doug Brown, right wing, to Maine of the American Hockey League. COLLEGE NEW MEXICO Promoted John Edward Koenig, associate director of athletics, to athletic director. PITTSBURGH- Named Bill Meyers offensive line coach. STANFORD-Named Gary Gagnon tight ends and offensive linemen coach.
