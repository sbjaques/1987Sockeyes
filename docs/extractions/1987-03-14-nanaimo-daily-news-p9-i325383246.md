By The Canadian Press The Kelowna Packers earned a berth in the B.C. Junior Hockey League final Friday night, getting two goals from Kevin Taillefer en route to a 6-3 win over the Vernon Packers in the sixth game of the best-of-seven Interior Divison final. The Packers meet the winner of the Richmond Flyers Coastal Divison semifinal. The Sockeyes lead that matchup three games to none and could wrap up the divison title tonight in Delta. In Vernon, the Packers' other goals came from Jay Luknowsky, Kelly Hurd, Al Shuler and Jamie Kurki. The Läkers. got goals from Kevin Scott, Doug Spooner and Craig Bedard. The Lakers outshot Kelowna 53- 32. Lucien Carrigan played goal for Kelowna while the netminding duties for Vernon were shared by Jason Palmer and Mike Sutherland. In Delta, Mike McCormick scored what turned out to be the winning goal at 18:21 of the second period as the Richmond Sockeyes defeated the Delta Flyers 4-1.
