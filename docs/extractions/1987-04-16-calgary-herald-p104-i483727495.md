RED DEER (CP) Richmond Sockeyes scored five firstperiod goals and coasted to a 7-3 victory over Red Deer Rustlers to square their best-of-seven western Centennial Cup semifinal at three wins each Wednesday. The Sockeyes, British Columbia Junior Hockey League champions, led 5-1 after the first period and 5-3 after the second.
