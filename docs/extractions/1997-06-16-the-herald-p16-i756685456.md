## Paragraphs

One hundred, fifty-seven students graduated during commencement ceremonies. Honored for their academic achievements were Laurel Virginia Hutchinson of Lancaster, Linda Parris Knight of Lancaster, Melody Elizabeth Talley of Lancaster, and Gabriela Tirza Knecht of Waxhaw who tied for highest academic average. The Clara P. Hammond Award was presented to Laurel Virginia Hutchinson of Lancaster and Monica Gail McManus of Lancaster. Division awards went to Christina Mae Norris of Lancaster for Arts and Letters, Melody Elizabeth Talley of Lancaster for Science and Mathematics, and Linda Parris Knight of Lancaster for Business and Professional Studies. Academic Achievement in Nursing and Nursing Excellence went to Elaine DeAnne Holeman of Monroe, N.C.; the Francine Manion award was presented to Kay Luther Turner of Lancaster. The President's Scholarship in honor of commencement speaker Charles E. Hathaway was awarded to April Nannette Ellis.
