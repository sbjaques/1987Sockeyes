Former Vancouver Canuck Darcy Rota scored late in the game to snap a tie and give Port Coquitlam's Molsons Oldtimers a 5-4 win over the CKNW 98's in a charity hockey game played Saturday night. Both teams were bolstered by a bevy of former NHLers including Rota, Orland Kurtenbach, Gerry O'Flaherty, Bob Murray and Paul Shymr, who played for Molsons, while the 98's had the help of Gary Monohan, Dennis Kearns and Jim Holt. The $600 raised at the PoCo Rec Centre went to CKNW's Orphan's Fund and Port Coquitlam's minor hockey association, said Molsons team representative Gary Elgear.
