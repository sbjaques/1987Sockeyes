MONTREAL (CP) - Washington Capitals' right winger Mike Gartner led all scorers with 13 goals and 18 points in 10 games last month to win the National Hockey League's player-of-the-month award for February, the NHL announced Monday. Gartner had a nine-game scoring streak to help his club to a 6-4 wonlost record. He also played his 600th career game, tied a club record for most points in one game with six, set a team-record for his 11th three-goal game and set Capitals' mark for short-handed goals in a season at six.
