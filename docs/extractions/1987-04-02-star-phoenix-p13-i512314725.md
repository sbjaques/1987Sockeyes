## Paragraphs

Humboldt advances LLOYDMINSTER (CP) - The Humboldt Broncos won their second consecutive Saskatchewan Amateur Junior Hockey League title Wednesday with a 4-3 win over the Lloydminster Lancers. The victory gave Humboldt a 4- 0 sweep over the Lancers in the best-of-seven series. Bill McDougall, Brett Stewart, Brad Bergen and Garnet Kazuik scored for the Broncos. Darryl Plandowski, Todd Cornish and Travis Gould replied for Lloydminster. The Broncos will now meet the winner of the Manitoba Junior Hockey League in the Western Centennial Cup semi-final.
