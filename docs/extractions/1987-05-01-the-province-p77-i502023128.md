Canadian Press VIENNA - Al Eagleson is going to put his theory to the test. Claiming that Canadians can never get enough of good hockey, Eagleson has released a schedule of 34 Canada Cup games to be played this summer featuring the best players in the world. Team Canada opens camp in Montreal on Aug. 3. There are 14 exhibition games and 20 tournament one, if the final goes the maximum three games. Hamilton gets 10 games. Vancouver gets none. The six-team round-robin tournament begins Aug. 28 and ends Sept. 6. The playoffs are scheduled to begin Sept. 8. Sockeyes Kids in News Services OTTAWA: - With four finalists decided, the Canadian Amateur Hockey Association announced yesterday the schedule for the Centennial Cup junior hockey final in Humboldt, Sask. Starting tomorrow, Atlantic champion Dartmouth, N.S., Fuel Kids open against the host Humboldt Broncos. Then on Sunday, the Richmond Sockeyes play Dartmouth, and Humboldt meets the Pembroke, Ont., Lumber Kings. The round-robin concludes with Pembroke against Richmond on Monday, Dartmouth playing Pembroke on Tuesday and Richmond meeting Humboldt on Wednesday.
