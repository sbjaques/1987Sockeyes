## Paragraphs

Golden oldies invade PoCo ice It'll be a brew bash. Port Coquitlam Rec Centre will be the site of the Molson's Oldtimers Hockey Tournament starting Thursday and running through Sunday. Four teams from the PoCo Oldtimers Hockey League will join 18 squads from throughout the Lower Mainland and a pair from Vancouver Island in the four-day affair. Games start at 6 p.m. Thursday and Friday, 8 a.m. Saturday and 9 a.m. Sunday, when the finals will be played. And the following Saturday, Dec. 5, PoCo Rec Centre will host the CKNW Orphans' Fund game between PoCo Molson's and the CKNW 98ers. The PoCo team will be coached by Ernie "Punch" McLean and will feature National Hockey League veterans Darcy Rota, Gary Lupul, Chris Oddliefson, Orland Kurtenbach, Paul Shymr and Dennis Kearns.
