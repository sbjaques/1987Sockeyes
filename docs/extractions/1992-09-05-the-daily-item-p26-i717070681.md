Bruins acquire Kimble: The Boston Bruins acquired right wing Darin Kimble from the expansion Tampa Bay Lightning for center Ken Hodge and defenseman Matt Hervey. Kimble, 23, has played four seasons in the NHL with Quebec and St. Louis. The 6-foot-2, 205-pounder had a goal with three assists in 46 games for the Blues last season. In 177 NHL games, Kimble has 12 goals, 15 assists and 747 penalty minutes.
