Hernon got his first goal by flipping home a rebound after Wahlin had centered the puck to McHugh, whose shot was stopped by the pads of Doyle. McGeough picked up a loose puck at center ice and broke in alone on Ray. Roy before flipping it over Roy's glove shoulder, but the Studnicka goal recaptured the two-goal lead. In the second period, goaltenders Roy and Doyle came up with several outstanding stops apiece as Roy got his left pad on Rocket's breakaway effort in the first minute of play and later came up with a fine stick stop on Tom Aldrich's 30-footer from the slot. Doyle came up with a good stick save on Rene Comeault's blast from the point and got his stick on Jay Mazur's wrist shot from in close. Jacques made it 4-1 at the 10:30 mark when Hellen set up Studnicka, whose shot across the crease was directed in by Jacques.
