## Paragraphs

Victories were scarce in Heart Lake Oldtimers Hockey League action on the weekend. Only the Habs managed a win, a 5-4 verdict over the Stars in the six-team league. The Kings and Leafs tied 4-4 and the Hawks knotted the Bruins 3-3 in other games. Bob Eby's three-goals powered the Habs. Art Cowan added one goal and one assist and Jinian Irvine one goal. Bill Kennedy, Farquarson, Gary Hamilton and Bill Connors drew one assist each. Jim Reid led the Stars with one goal and two assists and John Bianchi had one and one. Keith Dunn and John Roberts were the other scorers. Mike Topping claimed two assists and Robert Richard, John Koop and Barry Dimaline one each. Guy Bradley's third goal boosted the Kings to their deadlock with the Leafs. John Sudak added two goals and Bob Spafford one. John McKnight figured for three assists and Larry Bradley and Roger Thiffault one each. John Matwijszyn, Neil MacLean and Jim LeSarge had one goal and one assist each for the Leafs. Wayne Martin was the other scorer. Bob Cox gained two assists and Wayne Young and Bryon Arnott one each. The Hawks' Alan Parker fired the equalizer against the Bruins. Bruce Murray assisted. Bill Hannah and Scott Gray were the other scorers and Eric Deleeuw and Ernie Dalcourt had one assist each. Gene Gibbs, George Richards and Karry shared the Bruins' scoring. Ken Bickerton, Ron Young and Bill Hales had one assist each.
