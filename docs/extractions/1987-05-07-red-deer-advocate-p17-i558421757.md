## Paragraphs

Broncs in final HUMBOLDT, Sask. (CP) Humboldt Broncos won a place in the final of the Centennial Cup junior A hockey championship tournament Wednesday with a 6-1 victory over the Richmond Sockeyes. The losers, with a round-robin record of 2-1, will meet the Pembroke Lumber Kings in tonight's semifinal. The final will be played Saturday. Bill McDougall and Garnett Kazuik led Humboldt with two goals apiece, while Duncon Ryhorchuk and Curtis Chamberlin had singles. The win gave Humboldt a 3-0 record in the round-robin portion of the tournament. Stan Czenczek scored the Richmond goal. Lumber Kings finished with a 1-2 record. The fourth team involved, the Dartmouth Fuel Kids, lost all three games.
