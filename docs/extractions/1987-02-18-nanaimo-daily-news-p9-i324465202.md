I picked Juan de Fuca fifth, not because of their players, but because of their name. If they didn't have such a good name, they would have been picked last. That honor was reserved for Delta Flyers, but solid netminding from Stewart and the scoring exploits of Lance Momotani made me look like an idiot. As for my playoff picks? On the Coast, Richmond over Nanaimo five games, Langley over Delta in six games. And finally, Vernon over Richmond in seven.
