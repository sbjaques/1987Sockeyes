The Hawks best offensive line is comprised of three rookies - two free agents out of junior and a 12th round draft pick from the American college system. The Hawks still have 37 games left in the regular season, but it's obvious now that they will not make the playoffs. In fact, if they manage to finish fifth in the seven-team Northern Division it will be a major achievement. The Hawks are last in the division with a 15-26-2 won-lost-tied record for 33 points. They are nine points out of a playoff spot and the fourth-place Nova Scotia Oilers have played three fewer games than the Hawks. Moncton still meets Nova Scotia seven more times - five of those games are in Halifax. The Hawks have won only two games on the road all year.
