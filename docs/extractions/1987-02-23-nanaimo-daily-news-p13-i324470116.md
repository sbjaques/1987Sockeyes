Tucker injured BUFFALO, N.Y. (AP) Centre John Tucker, the Buffalo 'Sabres' leading scorer, is out of the lineup indefinitely with a back ailment, a spokesman for the National Hockey League team said Saturday.

EDMONTON (CP) - Edmonton Oilers' general manager Glen Sather said Saturday left winger Jaroslav Pouzar has been invited to try out for the National Hockey League team and will join the club on the current road trip. Pouzar played for three seasons for Edmonton before leaving to play in West Germany for the last two years. During the current season, the 35-year-old former star with the Czechoslovakian national team, finished third in league scoring by firing 26 goals in 30 games with Iserlohn of the West German first division.
