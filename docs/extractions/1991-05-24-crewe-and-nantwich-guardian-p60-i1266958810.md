## Paragraphs

THE budding soccer stars of the Junior Alex showed their skills as they brushed aside opposition from Preston, Rotherham and Watford in a five-a-side competition at the weekend. Out of four competitions involving teams of 10's, Under 12s, Under. 148 and Under16s the Alex youngsters emerged victorious in every group apart from the Under 12s. The day proved very successful with the good weather encouraging a crowd of about 300 to cheer on the teams. It was organised by the Junior Alex and sponsored by Barclays. managed by Andy MarThe Under 108; were riot and John Ford, the Under 12s Alex keeper Dean Greygoose, the Under 14s by Dave Tomlinson and the Under 16s by Terry Wood. After the event Dave Tomlinson thanked all those who supported the youngsters and Barclays their sponsorship.
