## Paragraphs

NEW WESTMINSTER, B.C. (CP) Garth Geddes scored two goals to lead Vernon Lakers to a 4-2 win over New Westminster Royals in game two of the B.C. Junior Hockey League finals Sunday night. The be -seven series is tied 1- 1, with the third game scheduled to be played din Vernon Tuesday night. The Interior Division champion Lakers, who were tied with the Royals 1-1 after the first and second periods, received singles from Dean Zayonce and Cam Sylven. Blaine Moore and Wes Elliott replied for the Royals, champions of the Coastal Division. New Westminster goaltender Gord Besse faced 39 -shots, while Lakers netminder Chris Fairlie faced 25 shots. Saturday night, three goals by Greg Geldart, two of them shorthanded, paced the Royals to an 11-2 victory over the Lakers. Also scoring for the Coast Division champion Royals, who held period leads of 6-0 and 10-1, were Jeff McLean, Kevin Hoffman and Frankie Biller with two goals each. Trevor Renkers and Tony Bobbitt added singles. Duane Dennis scored both goals for the Lakers, winners of the Interior Division. . Fairlie and Kevin Baldridge
