## Paragraphs

By The Canadian Press Juan de Fuca Whalers outlasted Abbotsford Falcons 8-6 in B.C. Junior Hockey League action Sunday afternoon in Colwood. Mike VanSickle and Ian Doak each fired a pair of goals for the Whalers, who beat Chilliwack Eagles 8-4 Saturday to close within two points of third place Nanaimo Clippers. Dale Walsh, Todd Demchuk, Ron Bhala and Todd Sawtell into an empty net at 19:59 completed the Whaler scoring. Blaine Moore led the Falcons with a pair of markers. The Falcons visit Nanaimo Tuesday night. In the only other Sunday game, Salmon Arm Tigers tripped up Summerland Buckaroos 8-6. Saturday, Kelowna Packers surprised Penticton Knights 6-4, Richmond Sockeyes downed Abbotsford 8-4, Vernon Lakers bested Summerland 9-4, and Merritt Centennials got past the Tigers 8-3.
