short by two The Canadian Press RICHMOND - The Juan de Fuca Whalers could have used a few more power-play opportunities Wednesday night. The Whalers scored all of their goals with the man advantage but lost 7-5 to Coastal Division-leading Richmond Sockeyes in the British Columbia Junior Hockey League. Tony Bobbitt, Matt Hervey and Jason Philips all scored twice for the winners while Stan Czerczek added the other Richmond goal. Darren Naylor, Ian Doak, Mike Barkley, Bruce Courtnall and Kevin Nohr counted for Juan de Fuca, which converted five of eight man-advantage situations. The Whalers play the Nanaimo Clippers at Juan de Fuca Arena starting at 7:30 tonight.
