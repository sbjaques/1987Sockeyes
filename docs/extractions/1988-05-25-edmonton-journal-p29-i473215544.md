## Paragraphs

The Canadian women's field hockey team leaves for a European this week as it continues preparations for the Summer Olympics. Deb Covey, Liz Czenczek and Shona Schleppe of Edmonton are among the 16 members of the sixthranked Canadian team confirmed for the Games in Seoul, Korea in September. Michelle Conn of Calgary is another member of the team, and trains regularly with the Edmonton contingent.

The Canadians will train June 1-20 in West Germany and June 21- July 11 in England under head coach Marina van der Merwe. They also have a number of international matches scheduled. Covey and Czenczek will be adding to their total of 43 international matches and Schleppe her 31 internationals, with matches against two of the three teams in their pool in Seoul - West Germany and Australia.
