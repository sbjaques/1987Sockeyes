Compiled from Staff and Wire Reports STONY BROOK, N.Y. - Massachusetts Institute of Technology beat Trinity, 72-51, Saturday to capture third place at the USB/Polera Tip-Off Classic. Dave Tomlinson and Mike Duffy each scored 18 points to lead MIT and two other Beavers were in double figures, also. Senior wing Mackey Smith led Trinity with nine points. The Tigers were defeated by Swarthmore, 77-62, in a late game Friday night. Trinity, 0-2, will again be on the road Tuesday, when the Tigers take on Austin College in Sherman.
