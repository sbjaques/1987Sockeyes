By Dwayne Tingley Staff Reporter Matt Hervey had never heard of Langley, British Columbia. But, as a 15-year-old he hitchhiked north from his broken home in Los Angeles. He stopped in a small .Canadian town, where he hoped to forget his t troubled past and begin pursuing a hockey career. "It wasn't a good situation," said Hervey, now a third-year defenceman with the Moncton Hawks. "If I had stayed in L.A., I'd probably be working construction somewhere with an expensive drug habit to support.' Being Matt Hervey hasn't been easy..
