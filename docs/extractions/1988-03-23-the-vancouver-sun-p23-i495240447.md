By ELLIOTT PAP Orland Kurtenbach's Richmond Sockeyes are up the stream without a spawning site. The defending B.C. Junior Hockey League and Centennial Cup champions don't have what it takes this season, a fact wincingly evident at Richmond Arena Tuesday night. "We have a major problem," understated Kurtenbach, the Sockeyes' head coach. "Our backs are to the wall." Richmond was trounced 6-2 by the Vernon Lakers in game three of the B.C. Junior final and now trails the best-of-seven 3-0. Game four goes tonight, 7:30, in Richmond. This year's edition of the Sockeyes doesn't have the talent possessed by last year's national champs.
