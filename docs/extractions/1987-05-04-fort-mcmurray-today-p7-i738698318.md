## Paragraphs

HUMBOLDT, Sask. (CP) - Humboldt Broncos beat Pembroke Lumber Kings 4-2 Sunday night to take an early lead in the round-robin Centennial Cup junior A hockey championship. Humboldt also won the tournament opener, beating Dartmouth Fuel Kids 7-2 Saturday night. The Fuel Kids, crowd favorites in the early going, fell 7-3 to Richmond Sockeyes in the only other game Sunday. The four-team tournament ends Saturday. The hometown Broncos built a 3-1 lead in the first period and hung for the win after the Lumber Kings battled back to make it 3-2 in the second. Kevin Luke, Rob Rice, Duncon Ryhorchuk and Curtis Chamberlin, with the winner at 16:09 of the first period, scored for the Broncos.
