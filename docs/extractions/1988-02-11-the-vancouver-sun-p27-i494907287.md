Wills paces Sockeyes' victory Canadian Press Dwayne Wills scored three goals as the Richmond Sockeyes defeated the Chilliwack Eagles 10-1 in the only B.C. Junior Hockey League game Wednesday night i in Richmond. Jason Talo had two goals for the Coastal Division leaders, who held period leads of 2-1 and 7-1. Singles came from Rick Rozecki, Barry Kress, Wayne Stripp, Steve Rutledge and Garth Lamb. Ray Savard was the only scorer for the Eagles, who were outshot 61-35.
