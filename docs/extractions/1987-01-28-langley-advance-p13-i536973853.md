### Region 3269,3083 → 3855,4374

6-4 loss Friday's loss - 6-4 to the Lakers was more evenly matched, but again Langley allowed their confidence and concentration to slip. There was no. score in the first period. Mike Dutton scored at 10:13 of the second period for Langley and Verne Kinley added another 19 seconds into the third period. Jim Butterworth added another goal at 5:25 -of the third and MacLean made it four at 6:53. On Wednesday, Jan. 21st, Langley routed the hapless Juan de Fuca Whalers 7-1 at the Civic Centre to maintain their second-place position behind the Richmond Sockeyes. Yesterday evening the Eagles met the Delta Flyers in Delta and tonight, Wednesday, Jan. 28th, they return to the Civic Centre to take on the Nanaimo Clippers. This Friday, Jan: 30th, Langley is at home to the Flyers.
