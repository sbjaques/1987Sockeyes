## Paragraphs

Saturday, Trent Pankewicz scored a shorthanded goal at 16:55 of the third period to lift the Powell River Paper Kings to a 5-4 win over the Richmond Sockeyes. Judson Innes, Scott Frizzell, Jeff Nottingham and Heath Dennison also scored for the visiting Paper Kings, who trailed 4-3 going into the third period. Tony Bobbitt, Dean Richards, Rob Tadey and Mark Kaufmann scored for Richmond.
