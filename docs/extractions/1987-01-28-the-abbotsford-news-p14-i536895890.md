Other Coastal goals were scored by Tony Bobbitt of Richmond Sockeyes, Darren Biggs and Mike Knapton of Nanaimo Clippers and Aaron Nosky of Delta Flyers. Interior marksmen were Pat Mangold and Craig Bedard of Kelewna Packers, and Darryl Kirkpatrick and Steve Manuel of the host Merritt Warriors. Goaltender Lucien Carignan of Kelowna Packers was named the Interior's most valuable player as he shut out the Coastal snipers in the game's final 30 minutes. Coastal all-stars held 4-0 and 5-3 period leads and were outshot 42-32.
