Nanaimo Midget A Clippers won their fifth game of the season in dramatic style Saturday in Victoria-Esquimalt, as Mike Biggs scored on a breakway with two seconds left to play to give the Clippers a.4-3 win. The goal came after Darren Kennedy blocked a shot at the blueline, and Biggs picked up the loose puck and raced the length of the ice for the score.
