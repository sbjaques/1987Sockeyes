WING TIPS: Coastal Division vicepresident David Ferguson announced Tuesday that Juan de Fuca coach Dale Sproule has been handed a ninemonth suspension. The suspension was handed down after a lengthy investigation into allegations he told his players to run Richmond Sockeyes' goalie Willie Popp, who was playing for the Eagles on a loan basis on Jan. 30. Popp suffered a season-ending injury when his wrist was broken by the Whalers' Rick Cox. On Saturday (Feb. 20), the Eagles will play their final home game of the season when they host Delta at the Coliseum at 8 p.m. Eagles' defenceman Kevin Jorgenson suffered a broken ankle Tuesday night when he fell during a fight in the second period with Abbotsford's Barclay Parneta. Also of interest is the fact that coach MacPherson is turning 50 on Friday (Feb. 19), and sources say his players may have something "special" in store for Saturday.
