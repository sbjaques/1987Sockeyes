## Paragraphs

Right winger Dean Rutledge scored his second consecutive hat-trick last night as the Richmond Sockeyes blasted the Delta Flyers 9-0 in a stormy B.C. Junior Hockey League playoff game at Richmond Arena. It was a penalty-filled affair with 262 penalty minutes handed out - 128 to Richmond and 134 to Delta. Seventeen players eight from Richmond and nine from Delta were ejected for fighting. All the players on the ice were involved in a brawl at 2:24 of the third period.
