SHELDON: John Tolley and Dave Tomlinson, representing Fishers Flights, won the Pairs title completing a fine double as T White had taken the singles the week before. The eventual champions had a comfortable win in the first round over P Pudge, J McMcKeown (Yardley Ex). They turned on the style in the quarterfinals scoring 131 8 tons gaining a 2-0 success against G Gibbs S Marsh (Lyndon). In the semis they met the favourites S Frost/G James. This was a super game with the champions coming out on top by 2-1 although they had six tons scored against them. The final saw John Tolley and Dave Tomlinson against George Eyles and Barrie Cowle from the Toby Jug. With the score at one each the champions found double eight to clinch the title.
