NOTES In yet another B.C. Junior Hockey League change, Juan de Fuca Whalers coach Dale Sproute has had his suspension temporarily lifted pending the hearing of his appeal by the league governors. It's not known when that appeal will be heard or if Sproute will be behind the bench when his fourth-place Whalers take on the first-place Richmond Sockeyes tomorrow in the opening game of the best-of-seven Coastal Division semifinal.
