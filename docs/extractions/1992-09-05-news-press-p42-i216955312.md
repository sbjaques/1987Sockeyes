## Paragraphs

The Boston Bruins acquired right wing Darin Kimble from the expansion Tampa Bay Lightning for center Ken and defenseman Matt Hervey. Kimble, 23, has played four seasons in the NHL with Quebec and St. Louis. The 6-foot-2, 205-pounder had a goal with three assists in 46 games for the Blues last season.
