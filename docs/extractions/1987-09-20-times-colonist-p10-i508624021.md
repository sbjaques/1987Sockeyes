## Paragraphs

Biller, who earlier scored in the third period to tie the game 3-3, fired the winner eight minutes and 16 seconds into the 10-minute suddendeath overtime period to give Merritt the win before 187 fans. Randy Zulinie scored to put Juan de Fuca back in front with just over three minutes remaining in regulation time. But Allan Rushton, who had earlier been cut from the Whalers' training camp, connected for Merritt with 2:06 remaining to force the extra time. Sheldon Nohr, Ian Doak and Todd Sawtell scored the other Juan de Fuca goals. Allan Suzuki and Chris Kaban supplied the other Merritt markers. Juan de Fuca led 3-0 after the first period and 3-2 going into the final frame. The Centennials outshot the Whalers 58-39. The Whalers next take on the Centennial Cup national champion Richmond Sockeyes at 8 p.m. Saturday at Juan de Fuca.
