### Region 1461,2629 → 2071,4103

HUMBOLDT, Sask. (CP) - Humboldt Broncos beat Pembroke Lumber Kings 4-2 Sunday night to take an early lead in the round-robin Centennial Cup junior A hockey championship. Humboldt also won the tournament opener, beating Dartmouth Fuel Kids 7-2 Saturday night. The Fuel Kids, crowd favorites in the early going, fell 7-3 to Richmond Sockeyes in the only other game Sunday. The four-team tournament ends Saturday. The hometown Broncos built a 3-1 lead in the first period and hung on for the win after the Lumber Kings battled back to make it 3-2 in the second. Kevin Luke, Rob Rice, Duncon Ryhorchuk and Curtis Chamberlin, with the winner at 16:09 of the first period, scored for the Broncos. Mike Eastwood got both goals for the Ontario team. Humboldt outshot Pembroke 42-32 and took 14 of 27 minor penalties. Richmond 7 Dartmouth 3 In the earlier game, Jason Phillips scored three goals to lead Richmond Sockeyes to their easy win over Dartmouth. Bryon Moller, Steve Jaques, Dave Tomlinson and Matt Hervey also scored for Sockeyes, who piled up 44 shots against. Dartmouth's 29. Richmond zipped to a 3-1 lead in the first period and built the margin to 6- 2 in the second.
