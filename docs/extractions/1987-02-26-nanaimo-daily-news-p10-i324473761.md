Bisons, Bears top all-stars EDMONTON (CP) - The Manitoba Bisons and the Alberta Golden Bears dominated the 1986-87 Canada West University Athletic Association all-star hockey teams announced Wednesday. Alberta had three players on the first team and Manitoba had two players on each of the first and second teams selected by the eight coaches. Alberta players named to the first team were defenceman Parie Proft and forwards Stacey Wakabayshi and Dennis Cranston. Joining them are goaltender Larry Dyck and defenceman Chris Saint-Cyr of Manitoba and forward Paul Geddes *of Calgary, the record-breaking conference scoring champion.
