## Paragraphs

RICHMOND, B.C. (CP) Rob Clarke scored three goals to lead the Richmond Sockeyes to an 8-3 victory over the Chilliwack Eagles in the only scheduled B.C. Junior, Hockey League game Wednesday. Barry Kress added a pair of goals and Tony Bobbitt, Keith Fair and Brad Dickson scored singles for the Sockeyes, who led 4-2 and 6-3 by periods. Jim Butterworth, Bart McAstocker and Mike Dutton replied for the Eagles, who got all their goals on powerplays. Chilliwack outshot Richmond 37- 35.
