## Paragraphs

The Juan de Fuca Whalers already faced a tough enough assignment with the league-leading Delta Flyers paying a visit tonight. But the Whalers' task was practically transformed into mission impossible on Friday after four Juan de Fuca players and coach Dale Sproule were handed suspensions by the B.C. Junior Hockey League. The suspensions arose out of a third-period brawl which occurred during a 7-3 victory over the Richmond Sockeyes on Nov. 7 at the Juan de Fuca Recreation Centre. Sproule was suspended for three games for failing to restrain his players during the brawl, which took place with 1:04 left in the game. Richmond coach Orland Kurtenbach was slapped with an identical suspension. Whalers receiving two-game suspensions were Rick Cox and Glen Whitehead, although Whitehead is out with a broken leg.
