## Paragraphs

Canadian Press Richmond Sockeyes got goals from five players as they defeated the Juan De Fuca Whalers 5-2 in B.C. Junior Hockey League action Wednesday in Richmond. Jason Talo, Keith Fair, Rick Rozecki, Dwayne Wills and Barry Kress tallied for the Sockeyes, who led 3-2 after two periods. Todd Sawtell and Ian Doak countered for the Whalers.
