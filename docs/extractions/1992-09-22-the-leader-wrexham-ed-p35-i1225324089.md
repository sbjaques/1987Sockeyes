## Paragraphs

RUGBY UNION White was stopped just short of the line, but his inside pass to Neil Chapman put the centre clear for Mold's second try. This brought Loughor to life and they replied with a penalty from fly half Frazer Lloyd. Loughor squandered four penalties before Mold punished them with a penalty from Gareth Jones. Mold extended their lead before the interval. A crunching tackle from Dave Tomlinson sent the ball loose and replacement Andrew Smith picked up and fed Roger Bold who went over from close range for a converted try.

In the second half, the Clwyd side maintained their momentum and after nine minutes Dave Tomlinson caught the Loughor winger in possession. From the ruck that followed Neil Chapman found Gareth Jones who cut inside a defender for a try which he converted. Loughor salvaged some pride with a try soon after from flanker Mike Davies, but this served only to inspire Mold further. Winger Brynley Wynne outpaced the Loughor defence for a 60 metre try.
