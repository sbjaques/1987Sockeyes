## Paragraphs

Wiktoria Czenczek, 91, 2020 Clarberth St., Schofield, died Wednesday, Oct. 7, 1998, in Comfort Care and Hospice Services, Community Health Care Wausau Hospital. She was a homemaker. Survivors include one son, Stanley (Bernice) Czenczek, Mosinee; one daughter, Stephanie Debinski, Schofield; four grandchildren; three great-grandchildren; three brothers, Wojciech (Stella) Czenczek, Pawel (Mary) Czenczek and Pietr (Stella) Czenczek, all of Poland; and one sister, Magdalena Szopinska, Poland. Services will be at 10 a.m. Saturday at St. Francis Xavier Catholic Church, Knowlton. Burial will be in St. Francis Cemetery, Knowlton. Visitation will be from 4 p.m. to 8 Friday at Beste Funeral Home, Mosinee, where at 7 p.m. there will be a prayer service. Visitation will be again from 8:30 a.m. to 9:30 a.m. Saturday at the funeral home.
