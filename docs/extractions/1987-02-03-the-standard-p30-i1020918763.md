VANCOUVER (CP) - Bobby Hull. Frank Mahovolich and Andy Bathgate will be among a number of former National H' Hockey League stars who will play in a, special oldtimers' game March 27 to raise money for wheelchair athlete Rick: Hansen. All proceeds of the game, sponsored by the Vancouver Canucks and the Pacific National Exhibition, will go to Hansen's Man in Motion tour to raise money for spinal cord research. Other players who will take part include Yvan Cournoyer and Darryl Sittler, as well as former Canucks Chris Oddleifson and Orland Kurtenbach. The game will be refereed by Red Storey. Organizers hope to raise more than $100.000 from the game, to be held in the 15,400-seat Pacific Coliseum where the Canucks play their home NHL games.
