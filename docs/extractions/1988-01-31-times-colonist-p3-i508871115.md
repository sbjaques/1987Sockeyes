The Sanders curling rink, which included third Georgia Hawkes, second Louise Herlinveaux and lead Deb Massullo, were selected B.C.'s team of the year over the Richmond Sockeyes junior hockey club and the sailing team of Ross Macdonald and Bruce Macdonald. Blind and deaf wrestler Pier Morton, 28, of Burnaby, a finalist the two previous years, was presented with the disabled athlete award. The other finalists were wheelchair's Paul Clark of Terrace, the winner in 1985 and '86, and Special Olympics swimmer Kathy Davis of Victoria. Bruce Enns of the University of B.C., who guided the Thunderbirds to the Canadian final, was named the province's coach of the year.
