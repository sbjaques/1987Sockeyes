By Stew Lang Times-Colonist staff The Juan de Fuca Whalers carried on with their entertaining ways Thursday, burying the Summerland Buckaroos 7-1 in a B.C. Junior Hockey League game at Juan de Fuca Recreation Centre. The Buckaroos occupy last place in the Interior Division with a 4-24 wonlost-tied record. "Our goal at the start of the year was to make the playoffs and play exciting hockey," said Juan de Fuca general-manager Larry Olson. "We're pleased with our performance to this point and I'm looking forward to the rest of the year." Olson said the Whalers, who now have a 16-12-1 record and are tied for second place in the Coastal Division with the Richmond Sockeyes, are in a good position for the playoffs.
