### Region 177,2667 → 765,4057

Maybe we shouldn't have traded him. I opined in this space a few weeks back that Langley Eagles coach and g.m. Muzz MacPherson made a wise move when he traded scoring ace Bryan Moller to the Richmond Sockeyes. But trust a sports journalist to be proved wrong. Seems Bryan (not Byron as quoted in one Canadian Press wire story recently) has been contributing greatly to the Junior A Sockeyes climb to glory in amateur hockey ranks. On Monday, Moller scored a goal in Richmond's 5-3 losing cause to the Humboldt Broncos in the first game of the best-ofseven Centennial Cup final to decide the Western Canadian champion. Though Richmond lost, Moller has been consistently good and may help, the 'Eyes turnaround this first spell of bad luck in an otherwise sparkling season. Game two in the series took place yesterday night (Tuesday) but passed press deadline.
