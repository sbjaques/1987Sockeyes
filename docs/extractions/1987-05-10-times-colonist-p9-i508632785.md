Goaltender brilliant as Sockeyes win Centennial Cup The Canadian Press HUMBOLDT, Sask. Jason Phillips scored three goals to- lead the Richmond Sockeyes to the Centennial Cup Tier II Junior A hockey championship Saturday night with a 5-2 win over the Humboldt Broncos. The Sockeyes had to fall back on an outstanding performance in goal by Frank Romeo, who faced 46 shots. His teammates managed only 23. Romeo was named most valuable player in the tournament.
