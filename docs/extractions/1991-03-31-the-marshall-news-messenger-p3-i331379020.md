## Paragraphs

Sunday morning after shooting at a house on FM 2198 occupied by three suspects he claims assaulted him and then stole his wallet, guns and vehicle, deputies said. Marshall emergency medical personnel were called into the area of FM 2198 after Harrison County sheriff's* deputies reported Tony' Bobbitt, 39, needed to be taken to the Marshall Memorial Hospital. After being taken to the hospital, Bobbitt was arrested on charges of aggravated assault and violation of a protective order. Bobbitt was released from the hospital to the sheriff's department Saturday after being treated for facial injuries, according to a nursing supervisor. Jo Sanders Morgan, 48, was charged with. felony theft. Alfred Dwayne Banks, 29, was charged with a Dallas County warrant for theft by check after •the suspect was involved in a minor traffic accident in the 600 block of South Grove Street. Charles Edward Mitchell, 27, was charged with burglary and aggravated assault late Friday when deputies interrupted a burglary, in progress on Highway 43 North, according to reports. EMS Paul Sharpe, from Highway 80 West to the Marshall Memorial Hospital.
