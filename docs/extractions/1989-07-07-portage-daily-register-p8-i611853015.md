## Paragraphs

PORTAGE - Two of the top three teams in the Portage Fastpitch Softball Association kept up its winning ways in competition Wednesday evening. Lee Realty, riding atop the league with a sizzling 10-2 record. posted a 12-5 triumph over Trecek. Chev-Olds. In the second game, Party House cruised to a 13-6 victory over Village Pub. Party House, which banged out 13 hits, improved to 7-4. In the opener, Lee Realty, winners of nine straight, tallied four runs in the first frame and never looked back. Jeff Schlender drew a one-out walk in the first. Steve Jacobs followed with a run-scoring double. Bryan Albrecht and Dave Mills then cracked back-to-back home runs. Lee added one run in the third before exploding for seven in the fourth of the five-inning contest. Jacobs clubbed a triple, while Dave Tomlinson smacked a homer to highlight the big inning. Lee collected seven extra base hits. • Devin Beeman, Dan Daley and John Walker picked up two hits apiece for winless Trecek. Party House wasted little time in controlling its win over Village Pub. The House scored six times in the second inning. Rick Lobenstein belted a triple and Jim Schumann added a double in the uprising. A three-run fourth for Party House featured consecutive roundtrippers by Dan Fearing and Lobenstein. Fearing's was a tworun blast. Loyal Reierson and Ken Staveness were perfect in three trips to the plate for Village Pub, which rapped out 13 hits.
