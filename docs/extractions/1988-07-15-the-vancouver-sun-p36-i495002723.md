## Paragraphs

For the second time this decade, the B.C. Junior Hockey League will have a team named the New Westminster Royals playing out of Queens Park Arena. The new Royals, formerly the Abbotsford Falcons, will fill the void created by last month's departure of the major junior New Westminster Bruins to Tri-Cities, Wash. A BCJHL team named the Royals played out of Queens Park from 1981-83 after the original Bruins were moved to Kamloops and became the Blazers. Byron Hitchens, Kurt Bonnes and former Richmond Sockeye owner Bruce Taylor, who recently sold the Sockeyes to Al Brew, each own one third of the new Royals. The team is seeking a coach as Orland Kurtenbach, who coached the Sockeyes for Taylor the last two seasons, is remaining in Richmond.
