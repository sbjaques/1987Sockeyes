Tony Bobbitt, who was on Taylor's Centennial Cup championship Richmond team two seasons ago, potted the winning goal at 3:07 of the overtime period. Frank Biller led the Royals with three goals while Greg Hadden scored twice. Judson Innes had two goals for the Paper Kings, including the score early in the third period that tied the game 5-5. Also scoring for Powell River, which trailed 5-4 after the second period after the teams were tied 2- 2 in the opening period, were Dean Malkoc, Trent Pankewicz and Jim Tobin. The tentative schedule calls for the best-of-seven BCJHL final against Interior champion Vernon to begin 7:30 p.m. Saturday at Queen's Park.
