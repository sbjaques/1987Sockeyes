TRANSACTIONS BASEBALL National League PITTSBURGH PIRATES -Announced the retirement of Tommy "Mooch" O' Toole, visiting team clubhouse manager, effective at the end of the season. BASKETBALL National Basketball League MILWAUKEE BUCKS -Named Bob Zuftelato director of player personnel. UTAH JAZZ- - Announced that Clarence Martin, forward, will play in Japan. FOOTBALL Canadian Football League HAMILTON TIGER-CATS- -Named Ted Schmitz acting head coach. Released Perry Kemp, wide recelver. OTTAWA ROUGH RIDERS -Released Todd Dillon, quarterback, and Cliff Hewitt, defensive back. HOCKEY National Hockey League NEW YORK ISLANDERS -Assigned Charlie Meitner, left wing: Allister Brown, defenseman; Charlie Callahan and Tim Ferguson, centers, to Peoria of the International Hockey League. Assigned Gord Paddock, John Mokosak, Bill Berg, Duncan MacPherson, Mike Neill, and Jim Sprenger, detensemen; Kurt Lackten, Daie Kushner, Glenn Johannesen, left wings; Tom Weiss, Stu Burnle, right wings; Bruce Boudreau, center, and Roydon Gunn and Brian Ford. goaltenders. to Springfield of the American Hockey League. NEW YORK RANGERS- -Traded Terry Carkner, defenseman, and Jett Jackson, left wing. to the Quebec Nordiques for John Ogrodnick, left wing, and David Shaw detenseman. Traded George McPhee, left wing, to the Winnipeg Jets for a 1909 fourthround draft choice. COLLEGE CARNEGIE MELLON -Named ROY Gereta volunteer assistant football coach.
