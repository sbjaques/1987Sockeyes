## Paragraphs

Consistency is also the best way to describe Gamble, the Vancouver Canucks draft who rebounded from an off year last season to anchor Spokane to second place. Other announced MVPs include: Saskatoon Blades' Tim Cheveldae, currently the No. 3 goaltender in the league; Prince Albert Raiders rookie centre Mike Modano, touted as the No. 1 draft pick this year; and Surrey native Mark Janssens, a centre with Regina Pats and a New York Rangers draft. Defenceman Bill Gregoire injured a knee Sunday and his status is doubtful for the Bruins' game tomorrow in Spokane. While Kamloops clinched first place in the West Friday, Saskatoon can claim the East Division title with one more win. The best-of-seven B.C. Junior Hockey League playoff final opens Saturday with the Vernon Lakers hosting the Richmond Sockeyes. Game two is Sunday, then March 22-23 in Richmond. Further games, if needed, would be March 26 in Vernon, March 30 at Richmond and April 2 in Vernon. The winner meets the PeaceCariboo Junior Hockey League winner April 8-10 in a best-ofthree B.C. championship.-
