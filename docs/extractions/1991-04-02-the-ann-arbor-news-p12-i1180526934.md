FROM THE ASSOCIATED PRESS WINNIPEG, Manitoba Bob Murdoch was fired as head coach of the Winnipeg Jets Monday after the team falled to make the NHL playoffs for the second time in three years. Murdoch was named the NHL coach of the year In 1989-90 for turning around a dismal Winnipeg performance the previous season, when the team also falled to make the playoffs. Winnipeg closed the season Sunday night with a 6-3 loss to the Edmonton: Oilers, finishing 29-43-11. Murdoch knew he might get fired. "First of all, you have to realize that you're a hockey coach and the life ex-: pectancy isn't great," he said during the weekend.
