## Paragraphs

Erdall nailed Bear defenseman Stan Czenczek just before Czenczek cleared the defensive zone and. Micheletti pounced on the loose puck. Using a teammate at the far post as a decoy, Micheletti deked 1 Roy at the short side before slipping it past him neatly. Micheletti tied it at the 13:14 mark when he capitalized on the rebound of a Wally Chapman drive from the left wing boards. Roy made the save with his left pad, but the puck landed right on the stick of Micheletti, who never hesitated and snapped it into the short side corner before Roy could scamper back into position. The penalty-filled second period was dominated by the Gophers territorially, but Roy came up with several big stops and the defense conscious Bears were able to hold the Gophers to seven shots on net.
