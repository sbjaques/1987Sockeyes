Sockeyes oust Lumber Kings HUMBOLDT, Sask. (CP) The Richmond Sockeyes whipped the Pembroke Lumber Kings 9-3 in a semifinal clash Thursday night to advance to the final game in the Centennial Cup Junior A hockey championship tournament. The Sockeyes, from British Columbia, will meet the Saskatchewan champion Humboldt Broncos in the sudden-death final Saturday night. The Lumber Kings, the Ontario champions who. had a 1-2 record in the round-robin, got goals from Dave Van Hoof, George Dupont and Chris Clarke.
