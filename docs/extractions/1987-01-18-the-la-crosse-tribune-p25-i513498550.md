BLOOMINGTON, Minn. (AP) The Minnesota North Stars on Saturday raised a banner in Met Center to honor Bill Masterton, the only National Hockey League player ever to die as a result of an injury that occurred during a game. Masterton suffered brain injuries during Minnesota's Jan. 13, 1968 game here against the California Golden Seals. He died Jan. 15.
