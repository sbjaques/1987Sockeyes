PORTAGE - Lee Realty strengthened its hold on first place, while Rio's Village Pub picked up a victory in Portage Fastpitch Softball Association play Thursday evening. Lee Realty, riding a four-run fifth inning, clipped Party House, 5-2. Dave Tomlinson cracked a double, while Dave Mills and Kris Hall banged singles to highlight the rally. Party House closed to 4-2 in the sixth before Lee, 7-2, scored an insurance run in its half of the sixth. Rick Lobenstein led Party House
