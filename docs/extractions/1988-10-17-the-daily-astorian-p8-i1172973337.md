LEXINGTON, Ky. (AP) - College basketball's winningest program is facing one of its biggest battles. The University of Kentucky was notified by the NCAA last Friday that it found 17 new allegations of wrongdoing, ranging from Improper recruiting to unethical conduct, bringing the total to 18 against the school's lained basketball program that has won 1,453 games in 85 seasons.
