The Keg and The Daily Free Press Present THE ATHLETE OF THE WEEK Nanaimo Islander towering centre Kevin Barfoot was named the Most Valuable Player in the Jasper Place Invitational Basketball Tournament last weekend in Edmonton. Kevin contributes his success in basketball to Islander coach Rick Paquette, his teammates and lots of practice.
