RICHMOND 7 DARTMOUTH 3 In the earlier game, Jason Phillips scored three goals to lead Richmond Sockeyes to their easy win over Dartmouth. Bryon Moller, Steve Jaques, Dave Tomlinson and Matt Hervey also scored for the Sockeyes, who piled up 44 shots against Dartmouth's 29. Richmond zipped to a 3-1 lead in the first period and built the margin to 6-2 in the second.

HUMBOLDT, Sask. (CP) Humboldt Broncos beat Pembroke Lumber Kings 4-2 Sunday night to take an early lead in the roundrobin Centennial Cup junior A hockey championship. Humboldt also won the tournament opener, beating Dartmouth Fuel Kids 7-2 Saturday night. The Fuel Kids, crowd favorites in the early going, fell 7-3 to Richmond Sockeyes in the only other game Sunday. The four-team tournament ends Saturday. The hometown Broncos built a 3- 1 lead in the first period and hung on for the win after the Lumber Kings battled back to make it 3-2 in the second.
