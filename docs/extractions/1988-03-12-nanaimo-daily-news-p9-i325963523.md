## Paragraphs

DELTA, B.C. (CP) - Rob Clark scored two goals to lead the Richmond Sockeyes to a 5-3 win over the Delta Flyers in B.C. Junior Hockey League playoff action Friday. Richmond now has a 3-0 lead over Delta in their best-of-seven Coast Division semifinal series. Jason Talo, Dan Walton and Keith Fair came up with the other Richmond markers. Doug MacDonald, Todd Voshell and Jeff Nottingham scored for Delta, which tied Richmond 1-1 after the first period and fell behind 3-2 after the middle frame. Delta outshot Richmond 30-28.
