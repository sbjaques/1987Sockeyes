Of these BCJHL graduates currently playing in the U.S., the ones to watch this season and in future seasons are the above Harrison, who last played for the. Kelowna Packers, former Penticton Knights Lee Davidson, Murray Winnicki, John DePourcq, Derek Pizzey, David Shields, Mike Jeffrey and Warren Sharples, plus former Vernon Lakers and Richmond Sockeyes David Wensley, Bruce Major, Scott King, Steve Tuttle and Paul Rutherford. Others to note include Jamie Stewart who played for Langley and Abbotsford, Frank Furlan and Al Loring both of whom tended goal for the Nanaimo Clippers, former Shuswap stars Rob Klenk and Paul Tory plus Bowling Green senior Don Barber who played for the Kelowna Buckaroos.
