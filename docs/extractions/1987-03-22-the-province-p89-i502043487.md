ORLAND KURTENBACH 'intensity the key'

Dean Rutledge, Dave Tomlinson and Jason Philips each scored twice for the Sockeyes. Richmond coach Orland Kurtenbach said: "the key to any hockey game is if you've got people who are intense and taking the body. That's what it takes to win." Kelowna was led by Jason Ruff and Pat Mangold, each with two goals, while Jamie Kurki rounded out the scoring.
