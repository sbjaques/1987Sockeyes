Bryan Fogarty said Tuesday his friend and former teammate John Kordic was upset at being released by the Quebec Nordiques. "He didn't take (general manager) Pierre Page's decision very well and I can understand that," Fogarty said. "John was tested regularly (and) that proved he wasn't doing anything wrong. He was on the right track." Kordic was signed as a free agent by Quebec last summer on Fogarty's recommendation. The two had met at an alcohol rehab centre in Minnesota. Both players were tested regularly by the Nordiques to ensure they were keeping to their recovery program.
