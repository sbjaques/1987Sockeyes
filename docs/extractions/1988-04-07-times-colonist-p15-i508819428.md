## Paragraphs

Sproule, who was suspended because of threats he allegedly made against an opposing goaltender prior to a game in Victoria on Jan. 30, resumed his coaching duties prior to the playoffs when his suspension was lifted pending a review of the situation. The league claimed Sproule threatened Richmond Sockeyes goaltender Willie Popp, who had been loaned to the Chilliwack Eagles for a game against the Whalers. Popp suffered a broken wrist in the game.
