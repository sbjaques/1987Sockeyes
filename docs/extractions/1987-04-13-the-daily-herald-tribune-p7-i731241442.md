RED DEER, Alta. (CP) - Dixon Ward and Richard Anderson scored two goals each as the Red Deer Rustlers thumped the visiting Richmond Sockeyes 7-1 in a chippy B.C.-Alberta Centennial Cup hockey playoff game yesterday. Red Deer's triumph evened the best-ofseven series at 2-2. Saturday, the Rusters coralled the Sockeyes 6-3. Brian Puhalsky, Tracy Georgsen and Bob Duchak also scored for the Alberta Junior Hockey League champion Rustlers, who led 3-0 and 6-0 by periods. Dave Tomlinson had the only goal for the British Columbia Junior Hockey League champion Sockeyes. Red Deer outshot Richmond 46-34 and took 18 of the game's 28 minor penalties, 10 of 20 majors and 10 of 20 game misconducts. The teams meet again tomorrow night in Red Deer. Before, last weekend, the Sockeyes had gone undefeated in playoffs, including a 2-0 sweep of Peace Cariboo Junior Hockey Leaguechampion Quesnel Millionaires.
