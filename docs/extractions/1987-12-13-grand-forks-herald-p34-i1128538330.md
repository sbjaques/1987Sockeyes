Sports Clipboard Schilz scores 60 in loss Dan Schilz of UND-Lake Region scored a schoolrecord 60 points Saturday, but it wasn't enough as the Royals dropped a 102-99 decision to Minneapolis Community College in the championship game of the Waukesha (Wis.) Tech Holiday basketball tournament. The old record of 54 points in a game was set by Schilz two weeks ago against UND-Willison. Schilz is a sophomore from Cudahy, Wis. * Bison top Moorhead Dan Wilberscheid scored 19 ) points and two other North Dakota players were in double figures Saturday in a 66-54 non-conference basketball victory over Moorhead State in Moorhead. 1. North Dakota, 5-0, handed Moorhead State their fourth straight loss to drop the Dragons' record to 2- 5. Mike Hilley had 13 for the Bison and Joe Regnier 5 added 11. Brian Paulson had a team-high 14 points for Moorhead State before fouling out. Green Wave win The East Grand Forks Senior High hockey team used a strong first period to defeat the Moorhead Spuds 4-1 in a Minnesota Section 8 hockey game Saturday night in Moorhead. "Our early big lead was keyed by putting so many shots on goal," Senior High Coach Mike Lundby said. The Green Wave, who outshot Moorhead 40 to 20, were led by Jason Mack's two goals and one assist. Dan Parker also contributed a goal and an asist for the Green Wave, who are now 3-1 on the year. Senior High's next game is Friday in Bemidji.
