The Canadian Press RED DEER, Alta. The Richmond Sockeyes scored five firstperiod goals and coasted to a 7-3 victory over the Red Deer Rustlers to square their western Centennial Cup semifinal at three wins each Wednesday. The Sockeyes, B.C. Junior Hockey League champions, led 5-1 after the first period and 5-3 after the second. The Sockeyes outshot the Rustlers, Alberta junior champions, 36-33. Rob Clarke scored three times for Richmond and Tony Bobbitt, Dave Tomlinson, Brian Kozak and Mike McCormick added single goals. Pete
