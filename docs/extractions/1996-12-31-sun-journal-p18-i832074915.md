LEWISTON - The Holy Cross Black Squirt Tigers won all three of their games in the Auburn McDonald's Mites/Squirts Christmas Hockey Tournament. The Tigers trimmed Florian's Market of Auburn their opener, then blanked Central Maine Bruins 8- 0. In the third game, HC nipped Auburn's Dunham Bootmakers 3-2. Jacob Lavoie tallied goals during the tourney. Other goal-scorers: Adam Wilding, 3; Corey Worthington, 3; Mike McCormick, 2; Andrew Girouard, 2; Nathan Frechette, 2; Sam Madore, 1; Mike 1. Among those receiving assists were Matt Morgan, Jamie Jones and Kyle McCormick. Sean Carpenter goaled the shutout against Central. In league action, HC Black edged St. Peter's Blue 4-3 as Wilding tallied two goals. Frechette and Mike McCormick netted the others. Black also defeated Holy Family 7-1 as Worthington registered a hat trick. Other scorers were Lavoie, Frechette, Jason Roy and Mike McCormick.
