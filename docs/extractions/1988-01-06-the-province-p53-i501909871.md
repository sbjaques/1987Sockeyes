Lethbridge in exchange for the departed Mark Holick. DAVE PATZER IS back with the junior B Burnaby Bluehawks. Patzer won the West Coast Junior League title last season with the Bluehawks but had decided to take this season off. When Burnaby coach Gerry MacGillivary was handed a four-month suspension by the B.C. Amateur Hockey Association, Patzer stepped back into his old job. ALSO BACK WITH the Bluehawks is Larry Hayes, who is helping manage the squad in addition to serving as management assistant with the Richmond Sockeyes of the B.C. Junior League. Hayes was working in public relations with Estevan Bruins of the Saskatchewan Junior League but had some run-ins with head coach Gerry James and had his contract bought out.
