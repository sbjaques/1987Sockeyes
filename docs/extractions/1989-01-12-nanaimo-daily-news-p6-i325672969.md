## Paragraphs

By The Canadian Press Kevin Hoffman scored off a faceoff 43 seconds into sudden-death overtime to lift the New Westminster Royals to a 5-4 win over the Penticton Knights in B.C. Junior Hockey League game Wednesday night. In the only other game, the Richmond Sockeyes beat the Cowichan Valley Whalers 5-2. In Penticton, Hoffman's winner was his second goal of the game. Cam Matches, Blaine Moore and Frank Biller added the others. Penticton, which outshot New Westminster 44-37, got goals from Greg Benson, Mike Tavaroli, Curt Brennan and Daryl Mitchell. In Richmond. Dan Kesa scored twice to lead the Sockeyes and Mark Kaufmann, Jason Talo and Rob Horvath added singles.
