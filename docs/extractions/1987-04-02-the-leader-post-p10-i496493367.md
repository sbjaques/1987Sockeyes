## Paragraphs

LLOYDMINSTER (CP) - The Humboldt Broncos won their second consecutive Saskatchewan Amateur Junior Hockey League title Wednesday with a 4-3 win over the Lloydminster Lancers. The victory gave Humboldt a 4-0 sweep over the Lancers in the bestof-seven series. Bill McDougall, Brett Stewart, Brad Bergen and Garnet Kazuik scored for the Broncos. Darryl Plandowski, Todd Cornish and Travis Gould replied for Lloydminster. The Lancers outshot the Broncos 35-28 and led 1-0 after the first period. The second frame ended in a 2-2 tie.
