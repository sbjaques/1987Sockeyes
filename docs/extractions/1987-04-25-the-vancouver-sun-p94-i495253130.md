## Paragraphs

Richmond eyes hockey title Richmond Sockeyes will attempt to wrap up their sixth playoff series and first Abbott Cup title this weekend on the familiar ice of Richmond Arena. The B.C. Junior League champions entertain Saskatchewan's Humboldt Broncos tonight and Sunday (7:30) in games four and five of the best-of-seven Centennial Cup western junior A final. Richmond leads it 2-1. Coach Orland Kurtenbach's road warriors have not been home since April 8 - eight games ago - when they took a 2-0 lead over Red Deer Rustlers in the Alberta-B.C. final. And their stay at home will be short-lived. On Friday, they'll return to Humboldt to compete in the four-team Centennial Cup national championships.
