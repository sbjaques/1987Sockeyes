Sockeye swim club starts season

The Salmon Arm Sockeyes swim season starts on May 4 and continues through August. The children will be training three to five times during the week between 4:45 p.m. and 6:45 p.m., with seniors swimming in the morning as well. Weekend swim meets start in midJune, with our own meet taking place June 27 and 28. Participation in swim meets is not mandatory, but is an enjoyable part of the season for many of the swimmers. Our coaches this year will be Hamish Tucker as head coach, assistant coach Bob Tucker and junior coach Jordan Johnston.

The Salmon Arm Sockeyes swim club is taking registration for swimmers on April 25 and May 2 between 10 a.m. and 3 p.m. at the community center auditorium lobby. Children must be able to swim the length of the swimming pool unassisted to qualify
