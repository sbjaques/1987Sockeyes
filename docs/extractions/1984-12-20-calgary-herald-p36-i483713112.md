## Paragraphs

Calgarians will play Calgary's Liz Czenczek and Michelle Conn have been invited to play with the Canadian women's indoor field hockey team which will compete in a couple of weeks at the fourth European Cup. The competition takes place at the Crystal, Palace in Jan. 11-13. Czenczek and Conn, who play for the University of Calgary, will practise and play in Germany for a week prior to the event.
