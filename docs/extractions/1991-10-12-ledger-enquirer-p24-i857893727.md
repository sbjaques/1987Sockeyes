The Atlanta Falcons placed linebacker John Rade and wide receiver Jason Phillips on the active roster and put defensive end Mike Gannon injured reserve. Rade, who has had five consecutive 100-tackle seasons, was on the physically unable to perform list because of knee surgery last January. He is in his ninth NFL season... Efforts continued toward resolving the confused ownership of the New England Patriots after Fran Murray rejected Victor Kiam's bid for a 30-day extension to buy out his minority partner. The deadline for Kiam, who owns 51 percent of the team, to make a $38 million payment to buy out Murray passed without resolution Thursday. Murray holds the remaining 49 percent of the franchise. Neither Murray nor Kiam was available for comment... The Cleveland Browns acquired defensive end Mike Wise, who had been waived by the Los Angeles Raiders. Wise was a fourth-round draft pick out of Cal-Davis by Los Angeles in 1986. He played in 13 games for the Raiders last season, starting two at defensive tackle.
