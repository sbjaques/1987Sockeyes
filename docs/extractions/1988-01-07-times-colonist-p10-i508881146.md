## Paragraphs

RICHMOND - The Richmond Sockeyes took advantage of their opportunities Wednesday and tripped the Juan de Fuca Whalers 5-2 in B.C. Junior Hockey League action. The loss dropped Juan de Fuca's won-lost-tied record to 19-17-1 and left them in third place in the Coastal Division, six points behind the runner-up Sockeyes. But the score wasn't an indication of the play, according to Juan de Fuca Allan Sproule. vice just didn't finish," he explained, noting that both teams appeared to have an equal number of chances. "Randy Zulinic hit a couple of goal posts and (Richmond goaltender Wilie) Popp robbed Mike VanSickle and Ron Bhala on several chances." The score was tied 1-1 after the first period and Richmond led 3-2 going into the third. Keith Fair, Rick Rozecki, Dwayne Wills, Jason Talo and Barry Kress scored for the Sockeyes while Ian Doak and Todd Sawtell connected for the Whalers. Richmond outshot Juan de Fuca 35-28 although the Whalers came back to outgun their opponents 18-14 in the final frame. The Whalers get an opportunity for revenge and a chance to close the point gap when the Sockeyes pay a return visit to Juan de Fuca Recreation Centre at 8 p.m. Saturday.
