## Paragraphs

Montreal named Ken Macha assistant coach. BASKETBALL Cleveland placed forward Phil Hubbard on injured list; signed guard Ron Brewer to 10-day contract. Philadeiphia acquired forward Kenny Green from Washington in exchange for guard Leon Wood. HOCKEY Detroit recalled centre Ted Speers from Adirondack of American Hockey League. NY Islanders returned defenseman Gerald Diduck to Springfield of AHL. New Westminster (WHL) traded the rights to centre Erin Ginnell to Calgary for forwards David Kimble and Alan May, and the rights to Richmond midget centre Jason Talo.
