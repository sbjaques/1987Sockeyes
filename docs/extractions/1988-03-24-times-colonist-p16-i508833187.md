## Paragraphs

RICHMOND - Barry Kress's fifth goal was the winner Wednesday night as the Richmond Sockeyes came from behind to edge the Vernon Lakers 10-9 and stay alive in the B.C. Junior Hockey League championships. Vernon leads the best-of-seven final series 3-1. Next game is Saturday in Vernon. Dwayne Willis tied the game at 15:07 of the third, and Kress scored the winner 50 seconds later. Wayne Stripp scored two do goals for Richmond, while Keith Fair and Brian McFarlane added singles. Jamie Steer led Vernon with two goals. Shawn Hendry, Dallas Drake, Mark Greyeyes, Mike Barkley, Ian Alger, Mike Paller and Greg Parnell had the others.
