The Canadian Press The Richmond Sockeyes have been assured of a first-place finish in the Coastal Division of the B.C. Junior Hockey League following a decision to stip the Delta Flyers of six points for using an ineligible player. League vice-president Dave Ferguson said he overturned three of Delta's victories because the team knew it was breaking the rules.
