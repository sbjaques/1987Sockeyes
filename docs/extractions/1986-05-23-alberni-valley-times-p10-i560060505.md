BARKLEY SELECTED TO ATTEND BCAMA TRAINING CAMP Times News Service Alberni's Mike Barkley is one of 46 players selected to the B.C. Amateur Hockey Association's provincial under-17 training camp in July. Some of the top amateur coaches in Canada will be there to offer instruction. The roster was picked following evaluation of 120 players on six teams at the B.C. Cup tournament last month in Summerland. The 46 players will gather July 19-26 at Nanaimo and the top players will advance to a camp conducted by the Canadian Amateur Hockey Association in August, at which time a Pacific Region team combining Alberta and B.C. will be named for an international tour in late 1986. Named to the Nanaimo camp were six goaltenders, 16 defencemen and 24 forwards: Goal - John Colvin, Kelowna; Steve Couper, North Delta; Dusty Imoo, Surrey; Kevin Radbourne, North Shore Winter Club; Barry Rysz, Vanderhoof; Dusty Pruden, Trail.
