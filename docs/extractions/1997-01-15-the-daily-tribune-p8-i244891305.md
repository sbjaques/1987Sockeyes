John Keel Photography Amy and James Czenczek Glenzer-Czenczek

Amy Glenzer and James Czenczek were married Saturday, Dec. 28, 1996, at First Congregational United Church of Christ. The Rev. Jake Close officiated the 4:30 p.m. ceremony. Music was provided by Amy Tlachac, Amy Renner and Vicki Gaden. Keri Gray-Rolfs was the candlelighter. The bride is the daughter of Dale and Marsha Glenzer, Wisconsin Rapids. The groom is the son of Stanley and Bernice Czenczek, Mosinee. Sherry Glenzer, Wisconsin Rapids, was maid of honor. Lori Glenzer, Wisconsin Rapids, was bridesmaid. Personal attendants were Jane and Chris Czenczek. Steve Czenczek, Livermore, Me., was best man. Steve Schroeder, Schofield, was the groomsman. Ushers were Deb Gessert and Sue Steinhilber. A reception was held at the Elks Lodge. The bride, a graduate of Lincoln High School, is a customer service representative for Sentry Insurance, Stevens Point. The groom, a graduate of the University of Wisconsin-Stevens Point, is a process engineer at Consolidated Papers Inc. They live in Stevens Point.
