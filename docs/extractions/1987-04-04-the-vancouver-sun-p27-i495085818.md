By SCOTT SIMPSON Richmond Sockeyes got a passing grade Friday - but the coach thinks they should have flunked. The B.C. That score was good enough to put them up one game to none in the best -ofthree Mowatt Cup series, but it wasn't good enough for Orland Kurtenbach.
