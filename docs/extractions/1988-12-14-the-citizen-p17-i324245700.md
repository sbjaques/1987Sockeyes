## Paragraphs

Cowichan Valley Whaler coach Dale Sproule knows what he wants for Christmas - - a new referee. Leading 3-2 in Saturday's B.C. Junior Hockey League contest versus the Richmond Sockeyes, the Whalers ran into a barrage of early third period penalties courtesy the whistle of ref Dave Dodgshon. Dodgshon is a fixture at most Whaler home games, due to an apparent shortage of qualified officials. "We played two periods of great hockey," said Sproule. "But we can't get away from the penalties. We got railroaded."
