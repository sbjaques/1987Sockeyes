MIDDLETON The Poynette Home Talent baseball team couldn't hold an early -run lead, as Middleton rebounded to capture an 11-5 victory in a nightleague game Thursday. Poynette took a 5-1 lead into the bottom of the fourth inning, but Middleton erased its deficit by scoring six times to take a 7-5 lead. Mike Passini had a two-run double that tied the game and Middleton took advantage of four walks in the inning. Middleton iced the win with three runs in the fifth inning and another in the sixth. Dave Tomlinson hit his fourth home run of the season in the first

inning to put Poynette up, 2-0. Tomlinson was 2-for-4 with three RBI. Jeff Gorman was 3-for-4 and Dan Tomlinson was 2-for-4, and each added run-scoring singles. Middleton remained undefeated with a 3-0 record in the night league, while Poynette slipped to 2-1. Poynette will host Baraboo Sunday.
