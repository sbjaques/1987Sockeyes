## Paragraphs

"When the Humboldt Broncos were in Selkirk (playing the Centennial Cup quarter-finals against the Manitoba Junior Hockey League-champion Steelers) it was interesting to note they all wore face shields. I sat with an official from the SJHL (Saskatchewan Amateur Junior Hockey League) and asked him what the reaction was from their making that compulsory, and he said there was no reaction at all. He said they just wore them.' The Saskatchewan official told Allard that the rule had resulted in a 'substantial reduction' in dental accidents. The MAHA last year paid $105,000 in insurance premiums for some 27,000 members. "'That's a lot of money," said Allard. "Making (a face protection rule) compulsory would certainly result in a reduction in dental accidents, which is where most of the claims are filed against.'
