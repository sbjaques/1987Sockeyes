Eagles nip Sockeyes By The Canadian Press Neil Eisenhut scored twice to lead Langley Eagles to a 5-4 win over Richmond Sockeyes in B.C. Junior Hockey League action Friday night. In the other games, Kelowna Packers crushed Abbotsford Falcons 10-1, Penticton Knights downed Summerland Buckaroos 6.4 and Vernon Lakers thrashed 1 Merritt Warriors 13-4. In Langley, single goals were scored by Rick Graham, Wes Gentles and Verne Kinley. Tony Bobbitt scored twice for Richmond with singles from Bill Hardy and Mike Clairinbull. Jamie Stewart stopped 46 shots for Langley, while Richmond netminder Glen Pencer had 27 saves. In Vernon, the Lakers scored seven unanswered goals in t the third period en route to their 13-4 win over the Merritt Warriors. One line did most of the damage for the Lakers with a total of 17 points. Center Kevin Scott scored five goals and added three assists while right winger Doug Spooner scored four times and left winger Stacey Hartnell picked up five assists. Lakers' Campbell Blair, who is chasing a league record for career assists, set up three goals to give him 153 career assists - two less than the career mark held by Ian Kidd, formerly of Penticton Knights. -In Kelowna, four players scored two goals apiece to lead the Packers. Mike•Murinko, Pat Mangold, Greg Andrusak a and Bruce Bianchini scored two goals each, while singles came from Jay Luknowsky and Kevin Harter. Brad Heschuk led the Knights with two goals, including the winner. Penticton's singles came from Sean Hartnell, Jackson Penney, Jan' Zeidel and Dowell Frantz.
