Unlike some previous years, there's no overwhelming favorite in this season's B.C. Junior Hockey League playoffs. There's definitely no squad quite like last year's Penticton Knights, winners of two consecutive league titles and the first BCJHL club to win the Centennial Cup. But the two division champions, Richmond Sockeyes and Kelowna Packers, get the nod as favorites. Play began last night in three best-of-seven division semifinals.
