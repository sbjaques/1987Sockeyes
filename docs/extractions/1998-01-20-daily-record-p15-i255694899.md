ICE HOCKEY

Doug Murry's hat trick and James Lenz's two goals capped a five goal second period and led Portledge (N.Y.) to a 5-0 win over Delbarton in non ice hockey action. It was Delbarton's first loss of the year. Portledge scored four goals in a span of 3:26 on five shots, prompting Delbarton to pull goaltender Jim Tyrell in favor of Ross Cherry. Despite outshooting their opponents 22-17, Delbarton (9-1-1) could not get past Portledge goalie Jon Olso.
