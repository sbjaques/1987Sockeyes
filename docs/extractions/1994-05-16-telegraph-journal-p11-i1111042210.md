Clark had two goals and an assist in powering the Leafs to a 4-2 victory over the San Jose Sharks in the seventh and deciding game of their Western Conference semifinal. The Leafs now play the Vancouver Canucks in a series that begins tonight in Toronto at 9 p.m.

LeBlanc beat goaltender Garth Snow on Saturday, but - you guessed it - he hit the goalpost. He did, however, earn his fourth assist of the playoffs on Craig Fisher's game winner at 16:22 of the first period. During the last few games of his slump, he has been visible through hard work and a morethan-usual physical style. LeBlanc isn't the only one having trouble scoring these playoffs. The line of Craig Fisher, Andy Brickley and Ross Wilson is responsible for much of the offence with 21 of the club's 45 goals. Three others Arto Blomsten, Dave Tomlinson and Ken Gernander - have scored three or more and no one else •has more than two.
