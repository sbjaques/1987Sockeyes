## Paragraphs

Fargo North pounced on Bismarck Century from the moment the Patriots walked off the bus and the Spartans • rolled to a 10-4 win Friday night in non-conference high school hockey at the Fargo Coliseum. North scored eight of the game's first nine goals. Doug Metcalf knocked in three goals for the Spartans and Tom Astrup added a pair. "They were just banging goals in early," said Dexter Frank, CHS assistant coach. "For the most part, we skated with them. But we didn't get results until the third period.' The Patriots scored three times in the final frame, including two goals by Kelly Bachmeier and one by John McDonald. Mike Sullivan added a first-period goal for CHS. North outshot the visitors 33 to 23. Chris Waterlund, who was hurt in practice earlier this week when a puck hit his facemask, finished with 23 saves for the Patriots. Century will conclude its weekend road trip today with a 3 p.m. game against Fargo Shanley. "Hopefully." Frank said, "the things we did in the third period will carry over into that game.'
