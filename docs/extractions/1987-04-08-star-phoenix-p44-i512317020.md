Humboldt Broncos have a goal and coach Bernie Lynch isn't about to let them forget it. When the Broncos briefly lost sight of their quest for a Centennial Cup championship, Lynch - an individual who has never been afraid to experiment with a new idea - came up with his own method to pick up his sagging squad. Now standard equipment for every Bronco player includes a hospital identification bracelet, with the word GOAL boldly printed on it. "Everytime they pick their nose, blow their nose, take a drink, look at their watch - anything they do with their hand - they see this word: GOAL," explains Lynch. "It means goals, oneness, attitude and loyalty. "*Attitude means dedication, discipline, determination. Loyalty means loyalty to the team, loyalty to the cause.
