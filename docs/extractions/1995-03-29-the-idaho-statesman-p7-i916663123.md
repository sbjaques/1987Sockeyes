HIGH SCHOOL ROUNDUP Whiting, Emry lift Kuna past Payette: Statesman staff Trent Whiting had four hits and scored three runs and Matt Emry clamped down the Payette offense in relief to help power Kuna to a 13-9 Snake River Valley victory in Payette Tuesday. Jason Phillips added three hits, including a pair of doubles, while Emry drove in two as well as earning the victory by striking out three and walking just one. Payette (4-1, 1-1) committed seven errors in the contest. Jeff Valenzuela had two RBIs, while Matt Burke drove in three and Ciriaco Franks had two RBIs.
