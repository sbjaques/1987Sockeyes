## Paragraphs

Sockeyes advance Richmond Sockeyes stopped Delta Flyers 3- 2 last night to sweep their best-of-seven BCJHL Coastal Division final in straight games. Richmond now meets Interior champion Vernon in a best-of-seven series for the league championship. On Friday, Rob Clark scored two goals to lead Richmond to a 5-3 win in the third game.
