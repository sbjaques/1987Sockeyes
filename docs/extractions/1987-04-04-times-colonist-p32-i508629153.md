RICHMOND (CP) - The Richmond Sockeyes scored a 4-3 win over the Quesnel Millionaires in the opening game of the B.C. Tier-Two junior hockey championship Friday night. The second game in the best-ofthree series between the Sockeyes, B.C. Junior League champions, and Quesnel, champion of the Peace-Cariboo League, is here tonight.
