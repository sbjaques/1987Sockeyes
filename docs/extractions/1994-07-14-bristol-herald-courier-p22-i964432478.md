Baseball bits SAFE Despite getting beat by Cal Ripken's relay throw, Tony Gwynn slid safely under the tag of catcher Ivan Rodriguez in the 10th inning to give the NL the win. STREAKS The National League has won all nine extra-inning All-Star games. SMITH, OZZIE St. Louis shortstop Ozzie Smith, playing in his 13th All-Star Game, singled in his second at-bat and made a diving backhanded stop of Chuck Knoblauch's grounder toward the hole in the seventh inning to save a run. Despite an abundance of closers in the American League, Baltimore's Lee Smith was the only AL reliever brought to the AllStar game in Pittsburgh. Not since 1972, when its eight-man pitching staff was comprised of eight starters, had the AL brought so few relievers.
