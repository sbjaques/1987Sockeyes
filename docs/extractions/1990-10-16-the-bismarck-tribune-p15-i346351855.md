NHL HOCKEY I CANADIENS 3, CAPITALS 1: The Montreal Canadiens rebounded from two weekend losses with a victory over the Washington Capitals. Guy Carbonneau and Mike McPhee gave the Canadiens, who had lost consecutive games to the New York Rangers and Hartford, a 2-0 lead after two slow periods. Dale Hunter scored early in the third period for the Capitals but Montreal's Brian Skrudland got it back for the final margin. In a defensive struggle, Washington outshot Montreal 23-17.
