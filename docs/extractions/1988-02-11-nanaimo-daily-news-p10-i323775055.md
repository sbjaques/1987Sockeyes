## Paragraphs

RICHMOND, B.C. (CP) T Dwayne Wills scored three goals as the Richmond Sockeyes defeated the Chilliwack Eagles 10-1 in the only B.C. Junior Hockey League game Wednesday night. Jason Talo had two goals for the Coastal Division leaders, who held period leads of 2-1 and 7-1. Singles came from Rick Rozecki, Barry Kress, Wayne Stripp, Steve Rutledge Garth Lamb. Ray Savard was the only scorer for the Eagles, who were outshot 61-35. Meanwhile, the Victoria Cougars scored five times in the third period to break open a tight game and beat visiting Seattle Thunderbirds 7-3. Bob Foglietta led the Victoria attack with two goals, while Grant Chorney, Len Barrie, Dwayne Keller, Guy Burr and Micah Aivazoff contributed singles. Glen Goodall scored twice for Seattle, with Kent Dochuk providing the other goal. Swift Current Broncos caught the Saskatoon Blades on an off night and broke out of a brief scoring slump to beat the Blades 5-3. In other games, the Prince Albert Raiders trimmed the Brandon Wheat Kings 6-2, and Portland Winter Hawks edged the Spokane Chiefs 6-5 in overtime.
