SIMSBURY Lyman Hall needed to rebound Thursday after a disappointing offensive effort five days earlier. The Trojans did that and then LYMAN HALL 11 FARMINGTON O some. Brett McCarthy had two goals and three assists as No. 3 Lyman Hall-Wallingford beat No. 10 Farmington 11-0 at the International Skating Center in a nonleague boys hockey game. Jay Messina had three assists and Matt Williams two goals and two assists for the Trojans (2-1), who were coming off a 3-1 loss to Xavier. "We had bunch of shots Saturday and couldn't put them in," Lyman Hall coach Tim Belcher said. "Tonight, we were able to capitalize. Hockey's a game of bounces. We came out flat and didn't get them the other, night. We came out with a common goal in this game, to execute, and we did that."
