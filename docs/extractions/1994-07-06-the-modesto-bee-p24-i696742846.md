SUWANEE, Ga. The Atlanta Falcons signed their fourth-round draft pick and two veteran free agents. The veterans are quarterback Bob Gagliano, 35, who has played eight NFL seasons and two in the USFL, and wide receiver Jason Phillips. Gagliano joined the Falcons last Oct. 7, but did not play in a game. Phillips, a five-year veteran, has played in 56 games with 49 catches for 578 yards and two touchdowns. He joined Atlanta in 1991 after two seasons in Detroit. The rookie is quarterback Perry Klein, a 6-2, 218- pounder who set NCAA Division I passing records with 38 touchdown passes and 3,757 yards at C.W. Post.
