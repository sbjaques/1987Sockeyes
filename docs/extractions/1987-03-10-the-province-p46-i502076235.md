## Paragraphs

A 10-day break might be more of a problem for the Richmond Sockeyes than overconfidence, says head coach Orland Kurtenbach. The Sockeyes swept Nanaimo Clippers in their best-of-seven B.C. Junior Hockey League Coastal Division semifinal and have done nothing but practise while Delta Flyers and Langley Eagles went the distance in their series. Delta beat host Langley Sunday to win that series, getting a day off before meeting Richmond in the Coastal final, another best-of-seven affair, tonight and tomorrow at 7:30 at Richmond Arena. The third and fourth games go at 8 p.m. Friday and Saturday at Sungod Arena
