## Paragraphs

Dave Tomlinson scored three goals to pace the visiting Richmond Sockeyes to a 9-1 win over the Red Deer Rustlers in the seventh and deciding game of the Centennial Cup Western semi-final on Thursday. The teams were tied 0-0 after the first period, but Richmond exploded for five goals in the second. Richmond now meets the Humboldt Broncos in the Western best-of-seven final. The opening game in the series is Monday in Humboldt.
