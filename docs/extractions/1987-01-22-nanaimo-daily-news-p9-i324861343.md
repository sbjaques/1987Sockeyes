### Region 206,5932 → 802,7002

B.C. Junior Hockey League All-Star Game at Nicola Valley Memorial Arena in Merritt. Forwards Darren Biggs and Mike Knapton will join defenceman Grant McDonald on the Coastal Division All-Stars, team coached by Richmond Sockeyes' coach Orland Kurtenbach. Clipper blueliner Sean Stotts did not travel to Merritt Wednesday night due to an injured ankle suffered in Nanaimo's 6-2 win over Delta Flyers Tuesday night. He will likely play for the Clippers Friday night at home against Richmond. Stotts' place on the team will be taken by either Brad Pascal of Langley Eagles or Rod Sumner of Richmond.
