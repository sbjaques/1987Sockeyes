How did the Red Deer Rustlers feel about going seven games with the Canadian junior hockey champions? Were the Richmond Sockeyes as tough a competition as they looked? Will the 1987-88 edition of the Rustlers go all the way this year? You can ask all these and other questions on last year's edition of the Rustlers or on the upcoming season, at Eleo's Soup and San at No. 178 5201 43rd St., just west of Red Deer Regional Hospital Centre. An Advocate advertising feature by JACQUELINE JANSEN Everyone's favorite players will be on hand at the restaurant from 10 a.m. to 4 p.m. Thursday to get Red Deer in the mood for this year's hockey season and to promote a season ticket sales drive for the club. Season tickets can be purchased Thursday at the low price of $129 a pair. With 30 home games scheduled for the 1987-88 season, this works out to $2.15 per person per game. Where else can you find so much excitement and entertainment for so little?
