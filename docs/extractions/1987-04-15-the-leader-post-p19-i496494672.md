Richmond Sockeyes 7-5 in overtime Tuesday to take a 3-2 game lead in the best-of-seven Centennial Cup semi-final series. Both teams picked up 17 minor penalties and a misconduct. Game 6 of the series is set for tonight. The winner of the series will face Humboldt Broncos of the Saskatchewan Amateur Junior Hockey League in the western final . . . The hometown Miramichi Gagnon Packers played a solid 60 minutes of senior A hockey Tuesday as they defeated the Smiths Falls Rideaus 5-2 and won the Eastern Canadian Hardy Cup final series four games to one. The Packers advance to the national championship against the Stoney Plain, Alta., Eagles. They will find out Thursday whether they will be hosts or visitors. Stoney Plain eliminated Weyburn Devils
