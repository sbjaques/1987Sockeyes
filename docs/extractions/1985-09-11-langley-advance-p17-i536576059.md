First exhibition loss Eagles fall prey to Richmond Sockeyes

Langley Eagles opened their exhibition schedule on a losing note, dropping an 8-2 decision to the Richmond Sockeyes in Richmond on Thursday, Sept. 5th. Next up for the local Junior 'A' hockey squad is a game against Sidney' Capitals at 8 p.m. on Friday, followed by a 7:30 match against the Sockeyes. Both games are at the Civic Centre.
