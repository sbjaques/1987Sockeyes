## Paragraphs

DAVE TOMLINSON

To get an idea of how the hockey lives of Division 1 and Division 3 players differ, the Globe accompanied a player from each division to a practice at a school from the other division and had him comment on various aspects of the program. On one side was Dave Tomlinson, a senior center from Division 1 Boston University, taking in a practice at Division 3 Curry. On the other side, Brian Horan, a senior center from Division 3 Suffolk, attended a Boston College practice session at Conte Forum.
