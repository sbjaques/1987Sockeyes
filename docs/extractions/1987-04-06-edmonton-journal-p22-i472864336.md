RICHMOND, B.C. (CP) - The Richmond Sockeyes defeated Quesnel Millionaires 5-1 Saturday night to sweep the best-of-three B.C. Tier 2 junior hockey championship 2-0. Richmond advances to play the Red Deer Rustlers of the Alberta Junior Hockey League in a bestof -seven series, beginning Tuesday at Richmond.
