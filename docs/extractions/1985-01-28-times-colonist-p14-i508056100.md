## Paragraphs

Up-Island team wins bantam crown Cecil Vaughan scored four times and Jeff Kerr three times as Campbell River trounced Kamloops 11-0 in the championship final of the Racquet Club bantam rep hockey tournament on Sunday. Fuller Lake took the bronzemedal game with a 10-6 win over Racquet Club. Pat Hodgins scored four goals for Fuller Lake and Mike Bonde three. David Stevens and Jason Oliver scored twice each for Racquet Club. Named to the all-star team were Rob Kool of Nanaimo in goal; Scott Farrell of Richmond and Rod Brind' Amour of Campbell River on defence; and Kelly Ross of Kamloops, Jason Talo of Richmond and Steve Flynn of Racquet Club at forward. Bob Brind' Amour of Campbell River was named coach of the tournament and Burnaby the most sportsmanlike team.
