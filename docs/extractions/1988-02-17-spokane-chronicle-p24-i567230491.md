The hockey tournament tightened up with a suprising 1-1 tie between top-seeded Sweden and No. 8 Poland, and a 4-2 victory by unbeaten Canada over Switzerland. "We Canadians saw the Polish team play the and knew it (would be) a very difficult game for us,' said Swedish assistant coach Curt Lindstrom, who watched the Poles in a 1-0 loss to Canada on Sunday. •Al Davis, owner of the Los Angeles Raiders, has narrowed his search for the team's next head coach to three men. Considered the front runner for the position is Dan Henning, an assistant coach with Washington Redskins and the former head coach of the Atlanta Falcons. Other top contenders, are Mike Shanahan, the offensive coordinator of the Denver Broncos, and Joe Bugel, the Redskins' offensive line coach.
