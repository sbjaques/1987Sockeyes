America's Line By Michael "Roxy" Roxborough SUNDAY'S SCOOP: Swinging back over to the baseball diamond, there are some arms that deserve a mention. Jaime Navarro has a 4-0 time record against the Orioles. Jim Abbott is 0-4 vs. the A's. John Smiley has won eight of 11 against the Mets. Ron Darling has a fine 11-5 lifetime mark against the Pirates. Rick Sutcliffe has won 12 of 15 lifetime vs. the Reds. Tom Glavine does not have a win (0-6) against the Astros. CIRCULAR REPORT: The circle (0) around the Celtics-Pacers game is for the questionable status of Boston's Larry Bird (he was sent directly to the hospital after game one on Friday night). The circle around the Pistons-Hawks game is due to Detroit's Bill Laimbeer.
