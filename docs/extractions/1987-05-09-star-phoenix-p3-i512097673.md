## Paragraphs

SPORTS REPORTER Ken Juba sends a tip of the hat out to George Hitchings of R.R. 3. After covering the Centennial Cup game between the Humboldt Broncos and Richmond Sockeyes on Wednesday, Juba was returning home to file his story. He was about seven miles from Saskatoon, when without warning, a deer came out of the ditch. He couldn't avoid it and the result of the collision damaged the frontend of the car so extensively he couldn't drive it. Three motorists passed Juba by before Hitchings, who was travelling in the opposite direction, stopped and drove him into the city to get some assistance.
