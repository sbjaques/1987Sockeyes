HOCKEY NHL Boston Bruins trade defenceman Paul Boutilier to the M Minnesota North Stars for a fourth-round pick in 1987. Edmonton Oilers trade left winger Raimo Summanen to the Vancouver Canucks for left winger Moe Lemay: Hartford Whalers acquire right winger Pat Hughes on waivers from the St. Louis Blues. Montreal Canadiens send right winger David Maley to Sherbrooke of the American Hockey League. New York Rangers trade centre Raimo Helminen to the Minnesota North Stars for future considerations; acquire right winger Stu Kulak from the Edmonton Oilers to complete the trade made Oct. 24 for defenceman Reijo Ruotsalainen; trade defenceman Tom Laidlaw and centre Bobby Carpenter to the Los Angles Kings for centre Marcel Dionne, left winger Jeff Crossman and Los Angeles's third-round draft choice in 1989.
