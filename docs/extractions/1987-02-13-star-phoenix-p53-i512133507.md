The Moose Jaw Warriors thrive on overtime. A 7-6 win over Swift Current Broncos on Sunday was the Warriors' fifth win in eight overtime appearances this season. The club's other three overtime outings have resulted i in ties. The Spokane Chiefs (2-0-0), are the only other Western Hockey League club that can boast of an undefeated record in extra time this season. Kamloops Blazers have appeared in the most overtime games, a total of 11. CalWranglers, Medicine Hat Tigers and Swift Current have each played 10 OT games. At the other end of the scale, Spokane's two appearances are the fewest overtime games played by one club. Brandon Wheat Kings (0-3- 3) and the Saskatoon Blades (0-2-2) are still hunting for that elusive first OT victory of the season. Actually, the Blades have not found overtime to be a blessing in recent years. The club is 2-9- 10 in the extra period during Marcel Comeau's tenure as head coach.
