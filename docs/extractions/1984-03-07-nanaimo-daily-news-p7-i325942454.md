## Paragraphs

Hockey fans should note that Friday night's B.C. Junior Hockey League playoff game between Nanaimo Clippers and Richmond Sockeyes will start at 8:15 p.m. The switch from the original 8 p.m. starting time was made due to Vancouver Island Midget Rep playoff game between Nanaimo and Victoria Racquet Club, slated begin at 5:10 p.m. Fans are also reminded that with every purchase of a program Friday night, a free raffle ticket will be given out, enabling fans a chance at some Lions move

In other BCJHL games Tuesday, Langley Eagles defeated Abbotsford Flyers 4-1 and Richmond Sockeyes drubbed Cowichan Capitals 14-1. The Penticton victory sealed first place in the Interior Division for the Knights, who have one game left in the regular season. They outshot the second-place Lakers, now four points behind, 40- 32 and held period leads of 5-2 and 7-4. Hull linemate Rod Schluter collected three goals. At Langley, the Eagles got two goals from Earl Graham and one goal and two assists from Neil Reimer in their 4-1 decision over Abbotsford in the last game of the season for both clubs. Reimer's three points gave him the Coast Division scoring crown with 121 points, one ahead of Randy Douville of Abbotsford who was kept off the scoresheet.
