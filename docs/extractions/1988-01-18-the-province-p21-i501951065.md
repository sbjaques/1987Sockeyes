## Paragraphs

Canadian Press Richard Reay scored two goals to lead Kelowna Packers to a 6-1 win over the visiting Penticton Knights in B.C. Junior Hockey League action Saturday. Scoring singles for the Packers were Cary Kit, Scott Hargreaves, Bruce Bianchini and Mike Kasubuchi. Dowell Franz was Penticton's lone goalgetter. Wayne Stripp scored the winner at 9:12 of overtime to lead Richmond Sockeyes to a 6-5 win over home-ice Chilliwack Eagles. Steve Rutledge, Barry Cress, Brad Dickson, Rick Rozecki and Sasha Pogor added singles for Richmond. Rod Ewacha scored four goals as Salmon Arm Tigers pummelled Summerland 12-3. Wayne Kirbyson had a pair for the Tigers, with singles from Brad Neumier, Andy Faulkner, Greg Schulte, Jason Fox, Jim Eliason and Rob Ripley. The Buckaroos got goals from Shane Varey, Curtis York and Gary Taylor.
