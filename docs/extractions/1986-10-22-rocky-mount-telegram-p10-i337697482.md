TONY BOBBITT Tony Bobbitt, 9, is a little shy at times. But once you get tof know him, you'll love him. Tony needs a male role volunteer to spend time with. He's a typical boy who likes to do all the things boys enjoy doing. He just needs somebody to do it with. Nash-Edgecombe Resources for Youth matches youngsters such as Tony with caring adult volunteer companions. To volunteer, or for more information, contact Resources for Youth at 106 S. Washington St., Suite 203, Rocky Mount; phone 977-2331.
