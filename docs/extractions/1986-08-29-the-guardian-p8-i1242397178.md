By KEN GILES National Hockey League veteran defenceman Arnie Brown, new coach of the Bramalea Blues, doesn't have aspirations of promoting himself back into the NHL. Brown, who logged 10 years in the NHL, almost seven with the New York Rangers, isn't looking at his Blues' coaching job as a stepping stone to anywhere. "I've done that route," said Brown, Tuesday, following a training camp session at Terry Miller Recreation Centre. Brown, the Ontario Sales manager for Monsanto Plastics Division, Mississauga, was assistant coach of the Vancouver Canucks in 1977-78.
