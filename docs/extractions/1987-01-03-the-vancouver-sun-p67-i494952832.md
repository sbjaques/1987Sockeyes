## Paragraphs

Canadian Press Neil Eisenhut scored twice to lead the Langley Eagles to a 5-4 win over the Richmond Sockeyes in one of five games played Friday night in the B.C. Junior Hockey League. In the other games: Nanaimo Clippers defeated Delta Flyers 9-7, Kelowna Packers crushed Abbotsford Falcons 10-1, Penticton Knights downed Summerland Buckaroos 6-4 and Vernon Lakers thrashed Merritt Warriors 13-4. In Langley, single goals were scored by Rick Graham, Wes Gentles and Verne Kinley. Tony Bobbitt scored twice for Richmond with singles from Bill Hardy and Mike Clairinbull.
