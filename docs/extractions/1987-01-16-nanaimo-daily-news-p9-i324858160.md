Clippers 4, Sockeyes 11 By MICHAEL RHODE for The Daily Free Press Three goals by Rob Clarke and two more by former Clipper Stan Czenczek were more than enough for Richmond Sockeyes to defeat Nanaimo Clippers 11-4 Junior League "action Thursday at Richmond Arena. It's generally only a matter of time before dynamite explodes, and that's what the Clippers were up against. Nanaimo were down 3-0 before they knew what hit them. Said Sockeye coach Orland Kurtenbach, "We played three pretty strong periods tonight. We were skating pretty well and moving the puck.'

After Tony Bobbitt gave the Sockeyes the lead, Czenczek's first goal on the power play made it 2-0. On a great individual effort, Czenczek skated the length of the ice, walked around a Clipper defenceman, and beat goalie Wade Flaherty with a hard shot through the legs. The closest Nanaimo got was. 3-1 as Corky Parkes, with his first of two on the night to give him 32 on the season, closed the gap to two. Parkes snared a rebound in front of the Richmond net and beat goaltender Frank Romeo with a backhand shot low, to the right side.
