HUMBOLDT - It was a classic performance, and it certainly deserved rave reviews. The play of Richmond goaltender Frank Romeo was a tragedy in four acts for the Humboldt Broncos, who lost a 4-3 overtime decision to the Sockeyes in front of a small crowd of less than 900. at the Uniplex on Wednesday. With the win, Richmond leads the best-of-seven Abbott Cup series two games to one. Tony Bobbitt's goal 1:04 into the 10-minute overtime period provided Richmond with their margin of victory, but it was Romeo who commanded centre stage. "'We were lucky to win tonight's game," said Sockeye defenceman Matt Hervey. "If it wasn't for our goaltending, we would've got blown out. We only showed up for maybe one period.
