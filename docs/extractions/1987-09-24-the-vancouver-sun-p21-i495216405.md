PENDER HARBOR - Fair for coho to seven pounds at Point Upwood, Whitestone Island, Francis Point, Lowe's Resort reports. POWELL RIVER - Fair to good for anglers willing to put time in, Westview Fishing Charters reports. Coho to 15 pounds. Try Vananda Cove, top of Harwood Island. CAMPBELL RIVER - Good run of northerns expected after seiners wrap up in Seymour Narrows, reports River Sportsman. Cape Mudge lighthouse, Willow Point reef, Deepwater Bay, Browns Bay all good bets. Good fishing for northerns reported at Kelsey Bay, Sayward. *COURTENAY-COMOX - Fair for coho to six pounds, the odd northern, at Bates Beach, reports Jasper's Fishing Resort, Courtenay. Surface bucktailing in morning, herring strip or Hot Spot in evening. Fair for northerns from Bates beach to Oyster River mouth, using Zzinger or black and charcoal Buzz-Bomb, reports Black Fin Pub Marina, Comox. PARKSVILLE - Slow to fair for coho to 10 pounds, feeder chinook to 12 reports Beachcomber Marina, Northwest Bay. Buzz-Bomb, etc., working best. Ballenas Islands, Mistaken Island best bets. NANAIMO - Five Finger Island spot closure lifted Tuesday, good for coho, northerns to 11 pounds. Island Tackle and Billiards advises anglers to follow schools of bait. Troll, jig or mooch. PORT ALBERNI - Port Alberni harbor fair for chinook, reports Harbour Sports. Franklin River mouth, Barkley Sound good for large chinook and coho.

MERRITT - Ponderosa Sports says effort has dropped off with advent of hunting season. Hot weather has slowed fishing. OKANAGAN - Okanagan Lake still good for kokanee, but largest fish dark in readiness for spawning. Water levels low in upper lakes, evenings best. CARIBOO - Fawn Lake, Sheridan Lake. excellent using green Carey, reports Osprey Angling Guide Services.
