## Paragraphs

HUMBOLDT (CP) Three players scored two goals each as the Humboldt Broncos defeated the Lloydminster Lancers 10-2 in Saskatchewan Amateur Junior Hockey League playoff action Tuesday. The Broncos lead the best-of-seven final 3-0 and can wrap it up tonight in Lloydminster. Dave Shyiak, Bill McDougall and Joey Johannson all scored twice for the winners. Neil Clark, Brett Stewart, Garnet Kazuik and Morgan Watts added singles. Darryl Plandowski and Travis Gould replied for the Lancers, who trailed 5-0 and 7-1 by periods. The Broncos outshot Lloydminster 32-20 and took seven of 13 minor pepalties.
