Times Globe staff Chris Ryan and Andrew Ough scored three goals each yesterday, sparking the Saint John A team to a pair of victories in the bantam A division of the 28th annual Saint John Irving Oil Christmas Hockey Tournament. Ryan drilled two goals and Ough one in a 7-1 decision over Quispamsis, while Ough had a pair and Ryan one in a 6-0 triumph over Kennebecasis Valley. In the game against Quispamsis, Mike Dort, Doug Pare, Mike McCormick and Dave Dickie added single markers for Saint John while Chris Savoy answered for the losers. Against KV, McCormick, Rick Carrier and Craig Miller also scored for Saint John while netminder Ryan Steele produced the shutout. The event consists of five divisions, peewee A, peewee AAA, bantam A, bantam AAA and high school. All championship games will be played Saturday at Harbour Station. The peewee A final goes at 9 a.m., the bantam A championship at 11 a.m., the peewee AAA final at 1 p.m., the bantam AAA final at 3 p.m. and the high school championship at 5 p.m. The following are scores from games played yesterday: PEEWEE A Sussex Sussex Walsh 3 F'ton 2.
