Canadian Press VERNON - Vernon Lakers blew a four-goal lead but recovered in the late stages of the final period to defeat Richmond Sockeyes 9-6 Saturday and win the B.C. Junior Hockey League championship bestof-seven series in five games. "The character came through on this team," said Vernon coach Ernie Gare. "A lot of teams would have folded after blowing a fourgoal lead but we hung in there." The Lakers led 5-1 in the second period before the Sockeyes rallied. pulling into a 5-5 tie by 7:13 of the third.
