SACO The Thornton Academy Hockey Boosters Association will meet on Tuesday, Nov. 10 at 7 p.m. in the T.A. Science building. All persons interested in the Thornton Academy varsity, junior varsity and Saco Middle School ice hockey programs are invited. For additional information, call Dave Roop at 283-2933.

By LAURIE K. BEALS Journal Tribune Staff Writer The Maine Mariners may be long gone from the Cumberland County Civic Center, but it won't be long before Don Dietrich is back on the ice in Portland again. The 31-year-old Dietrich, a professional hockey player for 10 years, is now coaching the Bonny Eagle High team. "I'm excited about the upcoming year," Dietrich said. "I do think I have my work cut out for me, but hopefully I'll put my years of professional hockey experience to good use." Dietrich replaces former Biddeford High player Jim Gunn as the Bonny Eagle coach, taking over a varsity program that was winless last winter and has only a handful of triumphs in the last five seasons.
