Meanwhile, the Richmond Sockeyes jumped back into second place in the Coastal Division of the British Columbia Junior Hockey League Wednesday night with a 3-1 win over the Juan de Fuca Whalers. Barry Kress, Jason Talo and Greg Hadden scored for Richmond while Mike Van Sickle replied for the Whalers. Juan de Fuca goalie Byron Dafoe was outstanding as Richmond outshot the Whalers 52-28. Willie Popp was in goal for Richmond which led 3-1 after the second period. The first and third periods were scoreless.
