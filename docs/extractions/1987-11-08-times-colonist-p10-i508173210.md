By Stew Lang Times-Colonist staff The Juan de Fuca Whalers mauled the Richmond Sockeyes 7-3 in a fightfilled B.C. Junior Hockey League game Saturday night at Juan de Fuca Arena. The game was marred at 1:04 of the third period by a bench-clearing brawl. After the donnybrook, Dale Walsh, Glen Whitehead and Ron Trenholm of Juan de Fuca picked up double game misconducts while Todd Demchuk had a game misconduct.
