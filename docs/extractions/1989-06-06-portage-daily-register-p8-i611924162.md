## Paragraphs

PORTAGE After watching its first two games get wiped out by inclement weather, Village Pub finally opened its fastpitch softball season Thursday and with a victory. The Pub, riding a three-run second inning and a four-run third, topped Trecek Chev-Olds, 8-1. Village Pub managed only two hits in the contest, but was aided by numerous walks. Dave Howe smacked a double, while Jim Staveness belted a single for the winners. Dan Paul and Kevin Stillson each scored two runs. Wayne Sainsbury went 2-for-4, including a triple, for Trecek, now winless in three attempts. In one other game, Party House kept its record unblemished with a 7-6 triumph over Lee Realty. Todd Beeman cracked two doubles, while teammate Brad Condon ripped a triple for 3-0 Party House. Dave Tomlinson collected three safeties in four at-bats for Lee Realty. Action resumes tonight with Fishermen's Cove battling Party House at 7 p.m. and Service Drug facing Village Pub at 8:30.
