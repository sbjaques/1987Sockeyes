News Services OTTAWA - With four finalists decided, the Canadian Amateur Hockey Association announced yesterday the schedule for the Centennial Cup junior hockey final in Humboldt, Sask. Starting tomorrow, Atlantic champion Dartmouth, N.S., Fuel Kids open against the host Humboldt Broncos. Then on Sunday, the Richmond Sockeyes play Dartmouth, and Humboldt meets the Pembroke, Ont., Lumber Kings. The round-robin concludes with Pembroke against Richmond on Monday, Dartmouth playing Pembroke on Tuesday and Richmond meeting Humboldt on Wednesday.
