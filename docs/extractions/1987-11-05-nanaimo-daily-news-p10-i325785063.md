By The Canadian Press Dwayne Wills scored at 8:25 of the 10-minute overtime Wednesday night to hand the Richmond Sockeyes a 4-3 win over Merritt Centennials in the only B.C. Junior Hockey League game played. The other Richmond scorers were Keith Fair, Kirk Phare and Rob Clarke. The Centennials got goals from Frank Biller, with his 23rd goal in $16 games, Darren Harrold and Chris Forsberg. Merritt goalie Randy Beekhuizen was bombarded, stopping 67 shots. Richmond goalie Jason Palmer stopped 25 shots. Richmond now has an 11-6-1 record while is 10-8. Graham Lees scored with less than two minutes remaining in the third period to break a tie and lift the hometown Melville Millionaires to a 6-5 win over the Nipawin Hawks
