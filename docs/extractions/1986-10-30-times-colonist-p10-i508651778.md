## Paragraphs

Times-Colonist news services RICHMOND The Juan de Fuca Whalers converted a great start into their fourth B.C. Junior Hockey League win of the season Wednesday, beating the Richmond Sockeyes 7-5. The Whalers jumped out to a 4-0 lead in the first period and held a 5-2 advantage after two periods. Two of Juan de Fuca's first-period goals came with Whaler goaltender Byron Dafoe pulled as Richmond was being assessed delayed penalties. Dafoe, a 15-year-old rookie, sparkled when he was on the ice, making 39 saves. Glen Pencer stopped 32 shots for Richmond, which suffered its first home loss of the season. Randy Zulinick paced the Whalers with two goals and one assist. Rob Coultish and Michael Barkley added a pair of goals each. Bruce Robinson had a goal and two assists, Kevin Nohr three assists and Ian Doak and Todd Sawtell teo assists each. The Sockeyes' Mario Desjardin scored twice and Tony Bobbitt, Mike McCormick and Jeff Nottingham once. The Whalers play the Abbotsford Falcons Friday ay 7:30 p.m. at Juan de Fuca Arena.
