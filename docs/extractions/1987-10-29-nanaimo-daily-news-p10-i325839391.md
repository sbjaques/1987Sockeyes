## Paragraphs

Rob Clarke fired three goals Wednesday night to lead the Richmond Sockeyes to a 9-4 home-ice win over the Abbotsford Falcons in the only B-C Junior Hockey League game played tonight. The Sockeyes also got goals from Tony Bobbitt, Keith Fair, Rick Rozecki, Mario Desjardins, Brad Dixon and Dan Walton. The Falcons scorers were Paul Thompson, Rob Ripley, Doug McDonald and Blain Moore. Richmond goalie Jason Palmer stopped 25 shots while Abbotsford goalie Darcy D'Amours blocked 32.
