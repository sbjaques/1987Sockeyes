Hockey TORONTO Referee Kerry Fraser, who was involved in a controversial call in a National Hockey League playoff game between Montreal and Quebec, won't work any more games this season, says Jim Gregory, the NHL's executive director of hockey operations. Fraser was among two referees dropped as the NHL pared down to six for the rest of the playoffs.
