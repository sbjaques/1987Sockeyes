HUMBOLDT, Sask. (CP) - Following is the schedule for the Centennial Cup Junior A tournament that begins today. Teams from Humboldt, Pembroke, Ont., Dartmouth, N.S., and Richmond, B.C., will compete in the round-robin competition. All Times MDT: Today's Game Humboldt vs. Dartmouth, 7:30 p.m. Sunday Games Richmond vs. Dartmouth, 2:30 p.m. Humboldt vs. Pembroke, 7:30 p.m. Monday Game Pembroke vs. Richmond, 7:30 p.m. Tuesday Game Dartmouth vs. Pembroke, 7:30 p.m. Wednesday Game Richmond vs. Humboldt, 7:30 p.m. Thursday Game Semifinal Third vs. second, 8:30 p.m. Friday, May 8 No games scheduled Saturday, May 9 Final, 7:30 p.m.
